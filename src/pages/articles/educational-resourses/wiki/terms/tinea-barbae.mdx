# Definition
    
    Tinea barbae is a type of dermatophyte fungal infection of the facial hair and beard area, which is caused by a group of fungi known as Trichophyton.
    
    # Significance 
    
    Tinea barbae is the most common form of dermatophyte infection of the beard area and is of considerable importance to mycologists in terms of diagnosis and treatment.
