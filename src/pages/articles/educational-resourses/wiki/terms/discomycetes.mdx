# Discomycetes
    
    Discomycetes are a group of ascomycete fungi that are characterized by the presence of a fruiting body that is cup-, disc-, or apothecium-shaped and typically spread spores via an asci. This is an important group of fungi, as they represent one of the most diverse orders of fungal fruiting bodies and are an important part of the ecosystem.
