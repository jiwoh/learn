# Definition of Eumycota
    
    Eumycota is a major division of the kingdom Fungi that consists of true fungi, sharing similar characteristics and anatomy. It contains all the multicellular organisms that have a chitinous cell wall, lack chlorophyll and reproduce by means of spores.
