## Definition of Gymnomycota
    
    Gymnomycota, commonly known as "naked spores", are a group of fungi that produce spores that lack a cell wall and are released in the form of protoplasts. They are also known as Acrasiomycetes and have thin hyphal structures that divide asexually.

    Gymnomycota are a significant group of fungi to mycologists, as they are considered to be the evolutionary link between prokaryotes and higher fungi.
