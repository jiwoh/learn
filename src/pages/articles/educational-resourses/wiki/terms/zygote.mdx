# Zygote
    
    Zygote is a fungal cell resulting from the process of sexual reproduction. It is the initial cell formed when two compatible nuclei fuse together following the release of fungal mating types.

The study of zygotes is essential to furthering the understanding of fungal species and the processes of sexual reproduction.
