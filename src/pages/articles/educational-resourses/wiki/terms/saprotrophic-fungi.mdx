## Saprotrophic fungi
    
    Saprotrophic fungi are a group of fungi that obtain their nutrients by breaking down dead organic matter. These fungi play an important role in the global carbon and nutrient cycles, as they decompose dead organic matter and release essential minerals and energy back into the environment. Saprotrophic fungi are an essential part of the natural ecosystem, as they prevent the buildup of organic matter and form the basis of food webs.
