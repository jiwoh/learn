# Ligneous
    
    Ligneous (from Latin lignum meaning "wood") is a mycologic term used to describe fungi that produce wood-like rigid structures. These fungi forms fruiting bodies which are often woody and hard, making them difficult to break down.

    Ligneous fungi are an important group of fungi, as they contribute to the decomposition of woody substrates and play a key role in the global carbon cycle.
