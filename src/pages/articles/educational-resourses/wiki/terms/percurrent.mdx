## Definition of Percurrent
    Percurrent is a term used in mycology to describe mycelium growth and development that proceeds from one end of a fungal body. The growth of fungal hyphae is continuous in one direction and neither branches nor terminates in septa or cross walls, but rather continues in a single direction.
