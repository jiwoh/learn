# Mushroom Carbonara 
    
    ## Ingredients 
    - 8 ounces fettuccine 
    - 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil 
    - 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced 
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced 
    - 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper 
    - 1 large egg 
    - 2 tablespoons butter 
    - 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese 
    - 1/4 cup thinly sliced fresh parsley 
    
    ## Directions 
    1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over medium-high heat.
    2. Add the fettuccine to the boiling water and cook according to package instructions.
    3. Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    4. Add the mushrooms, garlic and black pepper to the skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until the mushrooms are tender and golden, about 8 minutes.
    5. Remove the skillet from the heat.
    6. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg and 2 tablespoons of the Parmesan cheese. 
    7. When the pasta is done, reserve 1/2 cup of the cooking water. 
    8. Drain the pasta and add it to the skillet with the mushrooms.
    9. Add the egg and Parmesan mixture to the skillet, stirring constantly to ensure the egg does not scramble.
    10. Add the butter and the reserved cooking water to the skillet and toss the pasta until itâ€™s coated with the sauce.
    11. Remove the skillet from the heat and add the remaining Parmesan cheese and parsley.
    12. Toss to combine and serve immediately.
