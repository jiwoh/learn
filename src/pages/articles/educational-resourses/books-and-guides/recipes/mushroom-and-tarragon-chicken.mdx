# Mushroom and Tarragon Chicken
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom and Tarragon Chicken is a classic French dish that has been a staple of French cuisine for many centuries. The combination of earthy mushrooms and aromatic tarragon creates a flavor profile that is both rich and comforting. The dish is often served as part of an elegant dinner party spread, as well as a cozy family dinner.

# Recipe Overview

This Mushroom and Tarragon Chicken dish has a Prep Time of 10 minutes, a Cooking Time of 20 minutes, a Skill Level of Intermediate and a Spice Level Scoville rating of 2,500 to 8,000.
