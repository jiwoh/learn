# Mushroom and Chicken Marsala
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom and Chicken Marsala is a classic Italian-American dish that combines sautéed mushrooms, chicken cutlets, Marsala wine, and butter. The dish’s origins have been traced back to Sicily in the late 18th century, where Marsala wine was paired with mushroom-based sauces to enhance the flavor of poultry. Since then, it has become a popular dish in Italian-American cuisine, as well as a staple in many homes and restaurants around the world.

# Recipe Overview

    Mushroom and Chicken Marsala has a Prep Time of 20 minutes, Cooking Time of 30 minutes, Skill Level of Intermediate, and a Spice Level with a Scoville rating of 0-500. Utensils needed to prepare Mushroom and Chicken Marsala include a large non-stick pan, a bowl, measuring cups, measuring spoons, a wooden spoon, and a sharp knife.

# Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons olive oil 
    - 4-6 boneless, skinless chicken breasts 
    - Salt and pepper 
    - 1/2 cup all-purpose flour 
    - 1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced 
    - 1/2 cup Marsala wine 
    - 1/2 cup chicken broth 
    - 2 tablespoons butter 
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Chicken Marsala Instructions
    1. Preheat the oven to 375F degrees.
    2. In a shallow bowl, combine the flour and salt. 
    3. Heat 1 tablespoon of butter and 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    4. Coat the chicken breast with the flour mixture, shaking off any excess.
    5. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until lightly browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes per side.
    6. Remove the chicken from the skillet and place on a plate.
    7. Add the remaining butter and oil to the skillet.
    8. Add the mushrooms and sauté until golden brown, about 5 minutes.
    9. Add the garlic, stirring for about one minute.
    10. Add the Marsala wine and broth, stirring occasionally.
    11. Return the chicken to the skillet, reduce the heat to medium-low, and simmer for about 10 minutes.
    12. Place the skillet in the preheated oven and bake for 10 minutes.
    13. Serve the chicken and mushrooms over cooked noodles or rice.
