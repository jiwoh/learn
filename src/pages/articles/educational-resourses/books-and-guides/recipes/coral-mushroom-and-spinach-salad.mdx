# Instructions

## Coral Mushroom and Spinach Salad Instructions
    
    1. Warm up a large skillet on medium-high heat.
    2. Add 1 tablespoon of olive oil and 1 tablespoon of butter to the skillet.
    3. When the butter has melted, add 8 ounces of sliced mushrooms to the skillet.
    4. Cook until the mushrooms are lightly browned, stirring occasionally.
    5. When the mushrooms are done cooking, remove them from the skillet and set aside in a bowl.
    6. Add 1 tablespoon of olive oil, 1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder and 1/4 teaspoon of salt to the same skillet.
    7. Turn the heat down to low and add 1 cup of baby spinach leaves to the skillet.
    8. Cook until the spinach has wilted, stirring frequently.
    9. Remove the spinach from the skillet and add it to the same bowl as the mushrooms.
    10. To the same bowl, add 1/4 cup of chopped red onion and 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese.
    11. To make the vinaigrette, add 3 tablespoons of olive oil, 2 tablespoons of white wine vinegar, 1 teaspoon of honey, 1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder, 1/4 teaspoon of salt and a pinch of pepper to a jar with a tight-fitting lid.
    12. Shake the jar until all ingredients are combined.
    13. Pour the vinaigrette over the mushroom and spinach mixture and stir to combine.
    14. Serve the salad with freshly chopped parsley for garnish. Enjoy!
