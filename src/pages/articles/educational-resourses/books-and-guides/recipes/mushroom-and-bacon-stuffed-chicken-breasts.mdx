# Ingredients

## Mushroom and Bacon-Stuffed Chicken Breasts Ingredients
    
    - 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    - 4 ounces bacon, finely chopped
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 8 ounces mushrooms, chopped
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 tablespoons dried parsley
    - Salt and pepper to season
    - 2 tablespoons flour
    - 1 cup chicken broth
    - 1/4 cup cream
    - 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
    - 2 tablespoons butter

    5. Place chicken breasts between two pieces of plastic wrap and pound with a meat mallet until uniformly thin.
    
    6. Place a tablespoon of the filling in the middle of each chicken breast and fold the chicken around the filling. Secure with a few toothpicks.
    
    7. Heat butter in a large oven-proof skillet over medium heat. Add chicken breasts, seam-side-down, and cook for 3-4 minutes per side until lightly browned.
    
    8. Transfer skillet with chicken to preheated oven and bake for 20 minutes.
    
    9. Serve warm with your favorite sides. Enjoy!
