# Cremini Mushroom and Beef Bourguignon
    
    ## Origin and History
    Cremini Mushroom and Beef Bourguignon is an evolved version of the classic French beef stew, known as Beef Bourguignon. It combines the rich, earthy flavor of cremini mushrooms with the irresistible taste of slow-cooked beef and a classic French red-wine sauce. This classic dish has been a favorite in French and bistro-style restaurants for decades, and its flavor and versatility makes it a favorite for home cooks as well.

# Recipe Overview

    Prep Time: 30 minutes
    Cook Time: 2 hours
    Skill Level: Moderate 
    Spice Level Scoville Rating: Low
    
    To make Cremini Mushroom and Beef Bourguignon, you'll need a Dutch oven or large pot, a large skillet, measuring cups, measuring spoons, and a few other kitchen utensils.

# Ingredients

## Ingredients
    
    - 2 lb. beef chuck, diced into 1-inch cubes
    - 2 Tbsp. olive oil
    - 1 large yellow onion, diced
    - 2 celery stalks, diced 
    - 2 carrots, diced
    - 6 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 cups dry red wine
    - 2 Tbsp. tomato paste
    - 2 sprigs fresh thyme
    - 2 bay leaves
    - 8 oz. cremini mushrooms, halved
    - 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
    - 2 cups beef broth
    - 2 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
    - 2 Tbsp. butter
    - Salt and pepper to taste

# Instructions

## Cremini Mushroom and Beef Bourguignon Instructions
    1. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
    2. Add 1 lb. of diced beef chuck roast and season with salt and pepper.
    3. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until lightly browned on all sides.
    4. Remove beef from the pot and set aside.
    5. Add 1 tablespoon of olive oil to the pot and add 1 chopped onion, 3 cloves of minced garlic, and 1 teaspoon of dried thyme.
    6. Cook for 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    7. Add 1/2 cup of red wine and deglaze the pan.
    8. Return the beef to the pot and add 3 cups of beef broth.
    9. Bring the mixture to a boil, reduce the heat to low, and simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
    10. Add 1 lb. of sliced cremini mushrooms and 1 tablespoon of tomato paste and stir to combine.
    11. Simmer for 20-30 minutes, or until the beef is tender.
    12. Serve with cooked egg noodles or mashed potatoes. Enjoy!
