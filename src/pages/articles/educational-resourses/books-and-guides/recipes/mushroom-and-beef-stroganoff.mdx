# Ingredients

## Mushroom and Beef Stroganoff Ingredients
    
    - 3 tablespoons butter
    - 1 onion, minced
    - 1 pound mushrooms, sliced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 pound lean ground beef
    - 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    - 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
    - 2 tablespoons ketchup
    - 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    - 1 1/2 cup beef broth
    - 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    - 8 ounces sour cream
    - 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Beef Stroganoff
    
    1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    2. Add 1 tablespoon of oil to the pan and heat.
    3. Once the oil is hot, add 1 pound of beef and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Cook the beef until it is browned, about 5 minutes.
    Remove the beef from the pan and set aside.
    
    4. Add 1 tablespoon of oil to the pan and heat.
    5. Once the oil is hot, add 1 chopped onion and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Cook until the onion is softened and lightly browned, about 8 minutes.
    
    6. Add 1 pound of sliced mushrooms to the pan and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
    
    7. Add 1 minced garlic clove, 1 teaspoon of dried thyme and 1 teaspoon of dry mustard. Cook for 1 minute.
    
    8. Add the cooked beef back to the pan along with 2 tablespoons of tomato paste, 1/2 cup of beef broth and 1/4 cup of sour cream. Stir to combine.
    
    9. Reduce heat to low and simmer until the sauce is slightly thickened, about 5 minutes.
    
    10. Serve with egg noodles or mashed potatoes.
