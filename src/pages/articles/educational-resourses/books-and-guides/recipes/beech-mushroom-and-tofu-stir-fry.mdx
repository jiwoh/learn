# Beech Mushroom and Tofu Stir-fry
    
    ## Origin and History
    Beech Mushroom and Tofu Stir-fry is a popular Chinese dish that has been a staple of Chinese cuisine for centuries. It has its roots in Jiangsu cuisine which features the combination of beech mushrooms, tofu, and various aromatic ingredients. Traditionally it is pan-fried and served with steamed rice. Over time, this delicious stir-fry has evolved in some parts of China to include a variety of vegetables, such as bell peppers, celery, and mushrooms to give it a variety of flavors and textures.

# Recipe Overview

    ### Prep Time, Cooking Time and Skill Level for Beech Mushroom and Tofu Stir-fry
    - Prep Time: 10 minutes
    - Cooking Time: 20 minutes 
    - Skill Level: Easy 
    - Spice Level Scoville rating: Low (no chilli added)
    
    To prepare Beech Mushroom and Tofu Stir-fry you would need a wok, chopping board, knife and large spoon.

# Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 teaspoons freshly grated ginger
    - 1 teaspoon dried red pepper flakes
    - 8 ounces beech mushrooms, sliced
    - 1 (8-ounce) package firm or extra-firm tofu, cubed
    - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    - 2 teaspoons sesame oil
    - 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
    - Salt and freshly ground black pepper
    - 1/4 cup chicken or vegetable broth
    - 1 tablespoon cornstarch
    - 2 tablespoons cold water

# Instructions

## Beech Mushroom and Tofu Stir-fry Instructions
    
    1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    
    2. Add the tofu cubes and cook for 7 minutes, or until golden brown. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside.
    
    3. Add the garlic and ginger to the skillet and sauté until lightly browned.
    
    4. Add the mushrooms to the skillet and sauté for 3 minutes.
    
    5. Add the soy sauce and sesame oil to the skillet and stir to combine.
    
    6. Add the cooked tofu back to the skillet along with the bell pepper.
    
    7. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    
    8. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve hot. Enjoy!
