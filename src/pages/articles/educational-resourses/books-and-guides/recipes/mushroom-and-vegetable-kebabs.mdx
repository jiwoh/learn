# Instructions

### Ingredients:
    - 1/2 lb. portobello mushrooms
    - 2 red bell peppers
    - 2 zucchini
    - 2 small onions
    - 1/4 cup oil
    - 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
    - 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    - 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    - 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 8 wooden skewers
    
    ### Instructions:
    1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
    2. Cut the mushrooms, bell peppers, zucchini, and onions into cubes about 1 to 1 1/2 inches in size and place them in a large bowl.
    3. In a small bowl, whisk together the oil, red wine vinegar, garlic powder, oregano, pepper, and salt. Pour the marinade over the vegetables and stir until all the vegetables are evenly coated.
    4. Soak the wooden skewers in water for 15 minutes. This will help to prevent them from burning on the grill.
    5. Thread the vegetables onto the skewers, alternating with different vegetables.
    6. Place the kebabs on the preheated grill and cook, turning them frequently, for 12 to 15 minutes until the vegetables are tender and lightly browned on all sides.
    7. Remove the kebabs from the grill and serve. Enjoy!
