# Lions Mane Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto 
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Originating from Italy, risotto is a versatile dish that is cooked using rice and broth. The combination of Lions Mane mushroom and Parmesan cheese for risotto is a popular combination among chefs and foodies alike. Lions Mane mushrooms have a unique texture and flavor, which makes them the perfect addition to this classic Italian dish. The combination of these two ingredients creates a delicious, creamy and decadent risotto that is sure to be a hit with all your dinner guests.

# Recipe Overview

The Prep Time for Lions Mane Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto is approximately 10 minutes, the Cooking Time is 25-30 minutes, the Skill Level is intermediate, and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0. To prepare this dish, you will need a cutting board, knife, pot, and skillet.

# Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Dried Lions Mane Mushrooms
    - 1/4 cup Butter
    - 1/2 Onion, diced
    - 2 cloves Garlic, crushed
    - 2 cups Arborio Rice
    - 5 cups Vegetable Broth
    - 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese, grated
    - 2 tablespoons Fresh Parsley, chopped
    - Salt and pepper to taste

# Instructions

## Lions Mane Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto Instructions
    
    1. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
    
    2. Add 1 finely chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
    
    3. Add 2 cloves of minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
    
    4. Add 2 cups of Arborio or Carnaroli rice to the pan and stir to coat in the oil and onion mixture.
    
    5. Add 1/2 cup of white wine and simmer until most of the liquid has been absorbed.
    
    6. Add 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth, 1 cup at a time, stirring frequently. When the broth is almost gone, add 1 more cup to ensure the risotto is cooked through.
    
    7. While the risotto is cooking, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    
    8. Add 8 ounces of sliced lion's mane mushrooms and cook until softened, about 10 minutes.
    
    9. Add 1 teaspoon of minced thyme and a pinch of salt and pepper.
    
    10. When the rice is cooked through, stir in the mushroom mixture, 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese, and 1 tablespoon of butter.
    
    11. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.
    
    12. Serve the risotto topped with more Parmesan cheese and fresh thyme leaves. Enjoy!
