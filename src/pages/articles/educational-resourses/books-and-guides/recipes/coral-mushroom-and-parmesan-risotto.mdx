# Coral Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Coral mushroom and Parmesan risotto is a classic Italian dish hailing from the Northern region of Italy. The dish is believed to be a creation of the late 1800s and has been a staple on Italian tables ever since. Traditionally, the dish is composed of Arborio rice, sautéed garlic and onions, white wine, Coral mushrooms, Parmesan cheese and a variety of herbs, spices, and seasonings. The creamy texture of the risotto is achieved by slowly stirring in the broth, allowing the ingredients to slowly simmer together until all the liquid is absorbed.

# Recipe Overview

    - **Prep Time for Coral Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto**: 10 minutes  
    - **Cooking Time for Coral Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto**: 30 minutes  
    - **Skill Level for Coral Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto**: Beginner  
    - **Spice Level Scoville rating for Coral Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto**: Not spicy
    
    For preparing Coral Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto, you will need a large saucepan, a wooden spoon, and a strainer.

# Ingredients

- Olive oil
    - 1/2 cup finely chopped shallots
    - 2 large cloves garlic, minced
    - 1/2 pound cremini mushrooms, chopped
    - 1/2 pound coral mushrooms, chopped
    - 2 cups arborio rice
    - 6 to 8 cups chicken broth, heated
    - 1/2 cup white wine
    - 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    - 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan
    - 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
    - Chopped parsley, for garnish (optional)

# Instructions

## Coral Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto Instructions
    
    1. Heat 3 tablespoons of olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
    
    2. Sauté 1 large diced onion until it is lightly browned, about 7 minutes.
    
    3. Add 1 cup of arborio rice and stir for 2-3 minutes to coat the rice with the oil.
    
    4. Add 1/2 cup of white wine and stir to combine.
    
    5. Let the wine reduce for about 1 minute.
    
    6. Add the 2 cups of vegetable broth, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper.
    
    7. Stir and reduce the heat to a simmer.
    
    8. Simmer the risotto, stirring occasionally, until the liquid is absorbed and the rice is cooked, about 18 minutes.
    
    9. While the risotto is cooking, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
    
    10. Sauté 1/2 pound of fresh coral mushrooms for about 5 minutes, until they are lightly browned.
    
    11. Remove the mushrooms and set aside.
    
    12. When the risotto is done, remove it from the heat and add 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese and the sautéed mushrooms.
    
    13. Stir to combine and serve.
