# Instructions

### Maitake Mushroom and Beef Stir-fry
    
    #### Ingredients: 
    * 1 lb. lean ground beef
    * 8 ounces maitake mushrooms, torn
    * 2 tablespoons vegetable or canola oil
    * 3 tablespoons garlic, minced
    * 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
    * 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
    * 2 tablespoons honey
    * 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
    * 1 teaspoon freshly grated ginger
    * 2 scallions, sliced
    * salt and pepper to taste
    
    #### Instructions: 
    1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    2. Add beef to the skillet and cook until it is lightly browned and cooked through, about 5 minutes. 
    3. Add the mushrooms, garlic, soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, sesame oil, and ginger.
    4. Reduce heat to low and simmer for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
    5. Remove from the heat and stir in the scallions. 
    6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. 
    7. Serve over steamed rice or quinoa. Enjoy!
