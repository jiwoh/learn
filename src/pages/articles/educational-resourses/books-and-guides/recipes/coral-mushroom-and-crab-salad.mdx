# Instructions

## Coral Mushroom and Crab Salad 
    
    ### Ingredients
    - 4 large crab legs
    - 4 ounces of coral mushrooms
    - 2 cups of spring emeralds
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1 lemon
    - 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    
    ### Instructions
    
    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
    
    2. Place the crab legs on a baking sheet and bake for 15 minutes, or until cooked through. 
    
    3. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. 
    
    4. Add the coral mushrooms and cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring frequently. 
    
    5. Remove the mushrooms from the heat and transfer to a bowl. 
    
    6. Peel and de-shell the crab legs, then add the meat to the mushrooms.
    
    7. Squeeze the juice of one lemon into the bowl, then stir in the fresh basil.
    
    8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    
    9. In a bowl, combine the spring emeralds and the crab and mushroom mixture.
    
    10. Serve and enjoy!
