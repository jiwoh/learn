# Shiitake Mushroom and Asparagus Risotto
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Shiitake Mushroom and Asparagus Risotto is a classic Italian dish that was developed in the mid-20th century. This dish fuses the savory earthiness of shiitake mushrooms with the bright, grassy flavors of asparagus. These flavors are then complemented by the creamy texture of the risotto, making it a truly delicious, comforting dish.

# Recipe Overview

    *The Prep Time for Shiitake Mushroom and Asparagus Risotto is 30 minutes, the Cooking Time is 45 minutes, the Skill Level is Intermediate and the Spice Level is Mild. To make this dish, you will need a large pot, a wooden spoon, a knife and a cutting board.*

# Ingredients

* 1 tablespoon olive oil
    * 1 onion, finely chopped 
    * 2 cloves garlic, minced 
    * 2 cups Arborio rice 
    * 4 cups vegetable stock 
    * 1 cup dry white wine 
    * 1/2 teaspoon salt 
    * freshly ground black pepper, to taste 
    * 1/2 lb. asparagus, cut into 1 inch pieces
    * 1/2 lb. shiitake mushrooms, stemmed and chopped 
    * 1/2 cup grated Parmesan 
    * 2 tablespoons butter 
    * 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

# Instructions

## Shiitake Mushroom and Asparagus Risotto Instructions
    
    1. Prepare the ingredients: 
        - 8 ounces shiitake mushrooms, stems removed and sliced
        - 2 tablespoons olive oil
        - 2 cloves garlic, minced
        - 1 onion, diced
        - 2 cups arborio rice
        - 6 cups vegetable or chicken stock
        - ½ cup dry white wine
        - 1 cup asparagus, chopped
        - ¼ cup Parmesan cheese 
        - 2 tablespoons butter
        - Salt and pepper, to taste
    
    2. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add garlic and onion and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring frequentl.
    
    3. Add the rice to the saucepan. Stirring constantly, cook for about 3 minutes until the rice is lightly toasted.
    
    4. Add the white wine to the saucepan and cook until the liquid has been absorbed.
    
    5. Begin adding the stock to the saucepan, one cup at a time. Allow the liquid to be absorbed before adding each new cup of stock. This process should take about 20 minutes.
    
    6. When the risotto is nearly done, add the mushrooms and asparagus. Stir until the vegetables are tender.
    
    7. Turn off the heat and stir in the Parmesan cheese, butter, salt, and pepper. Serve immediately.
