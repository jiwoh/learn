# Instructions

### Ingredients 
    * 6 ounces uncooked angel hair pasta
    * 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil 
    * 2 small shallots, diced 
    * 2 cloves garlic, minced 
    * 2 cups sliced mushrooms 
    * 2/3 cup frozen peas 
    * 2 large eggs, lightly beaten 
    * 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese 
    * 1 tablespoon chopped flat-leaf parsley 
    * Salt and black pepper to taste 
    
    ### Instructions
    1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 6 minutes or until al dente.
    2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add shallots and garlic and cook until softened, about 2 minutes.
    3. Add mushrooms and cook until tender and liquid released has evaporated, about 4 minutes.
    4. Add peas and cook until just heated through, about 1 minute.
    5. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
    6. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and add it to the skillet with the mushrooms and peas.
    7. Over low heat, add the egg mixture to the skillet and stir to combine.
    8. Cook for 1 minute more or until the egg sauce is warmed through.
    9. Remove from heat. Stir in parsley and season with salt and black pepper to taste.
    10. Serve warm. Enjoy!
