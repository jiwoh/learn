    ## Origin and History
    
    Black trumpet mushrooms, also known as 'horn of plenty', have been used in Italian cooking for centuries. They have a unique flavor and aroma, making them a popular choice for risotto. Parmesan cheese has also been a staple for Italian cooking for centuries. Both the mushroom and cheese lend their unique flavors to this classic risotto recipe.

# Recipe Overview

    **Black Trumpet Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto:**
    
    - Prep Time: 20 minutes
    - Cooking Time: 60 minutes
    - Skill Level: Intermediate
    - Spice Level Scoville rating: 0
    
    You will need a large pot, risotto pan, wooden spoon, and Parmesan cheese for preparing Black Trumpet Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto.

# Ingredients

## Ingredients 
    - 1 tablespoon of olive oil 
    - 1 large shallot, minced 
    - 2 cloves of garlic, minced 
    - 1 cup of Arborio rice 
    - 2 cups of chicken or vegetable stock 
    - 1/2 cup of white wine 
    - 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese 
    - 1/2 pound of black trumpet mushrooms, thinly sliced 
    - Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste 
    - 2 tablespoons of finely chopped fresh parsley

# Instructions

## Black Trumpet Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto Instructions
    
    1. In a large saucepan, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat. 
    2. Saut√© 1/2 sliced onion and 1 minced garlic clove until softened and lightly browned, about 5 minutes. 
    3. Stir in 1 cup of Arborio rice and cook for 1 minute.
    4. Reduce the heat to medium and add 1/2 cup of white wine. 
    5. Stir until the wine is completely absorbed by the rice. 
    6. Add 1 cup of vegetable broth, 1/4 cup at a time, stirring continuously until each addition has been absorbed. 
    7. Continue adding 1/4 cup of broth at a time, stirring continuously, until the rice is creamy and al dente, about 20 minutes. 
    8. Meanwhile, prepare the black trumpet mushrooms by removing the stem and wiping them clean with a damp cloth. Cut the mushrooms into pieces. 
    9. Once the risotto is almost finished, add the mushrooms and cook for 2 minutes. 
    10. Stir in 1/2 cup finely grated Parmesan cheese and season with salt and freshly ground pepper. 
    11. Serve the risotto hot with additional Parmesan cheese.
