# Instructions

## Chanterelle Mushroom and Goat Cheese Pizza Recipe
    
    ### Ingredients:
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1 large red onion, thinly sliced
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
    - 1/4 cup white wine
    - 8 ounces chanterelle mushrooms, cleaned and sliced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
    - 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
    - 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
    - 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
    - 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
    - 1/2 cup crumbled goat cheese
    - 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
    - 1 (13.8-ounce) package pre-made pizza dough
    - 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
    
    ### Instructions:
    1. Preheat oven to 425Â°F.
    2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    3. Add in the onion, salt and pepper and cook, stirring occasionally, until softened and golden brown, about 10 minutes.
    4. Add in the wine and cook until it is completely evaporated and the onion is tender, about 2 minutes.
    5. Add in the mushrooms, garlic and all of the spices. Cook until the mushrooms are tender, about 7 minutes.
    6. Remove from heat and stir in the parsley.
    7. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to desired shape and place onto a greased baking sheet.
    8. Spread the mushroom and onion mixture onto the rolled out pizza dough.
    9. Sprinkle the goat cheese and Parmesan cheese over the mixture.
    10. Bake until the crust is golden brown, about 15 minutes.
    11. Remove from oven and brush the melted butter over the top of the pizza.
    12. Slice and serve. Enjoy!
