# Porcini Mushroom and Goat Cheese Crostini
    
    ## Ingredients
    - 2 cloves garlic
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1/4 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
    - 1/4 cup fresh oregano, finely chopped
    - 1/4 cup porcini mushrooms, coarsely chopped
    - 2 tablespoons white wine
    - Salt and pepper, to taste
    - 1 baguette, sliced into 1/4-inch-thick rounds
    - 4 ounces goat cheese
    
    ## Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. 
    2. In a medium skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
    Add the garlic and cook until fragrant and golden, about 1 minute. 
    3. Add the parsley, oregano, mushrooms and white wine. Cook until the mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes.
    Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
    4. Arrange the baguette slices on a baking sheet.
    Top each slice with the mushroom mixture and goat cheese.
    5. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes, or until cheese is melted and crostini are lightly toasted. 
    6. Serve warm. Enjoy!
