# Mushroom and Swiss Stuffed Zucchini
    
    ## Ingredients 
    * 2 medium zucchini 
    * 2 tablespoons olive oil 
    * 2 cloves garlic, minced 
    * 2 cups mushrooms, diced 
    * 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped 
    * 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped 
    * Salt and pepper, to taste 
    * 2/3 cup shredded Swiss cheese
    
    ## Instructions 
    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. 
    2. Cut the zucchini in half lengthwise and scoop out the middle, leaving an edge. 
    3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic, mushrooms, parsley, and basil. Season with salt and pepper. Sauté until the mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes. 
    4. Arrange the zucchini halves in a baking dish. Spoon the mushroom mixture into the cavities of the zucchini and top with shredded cheese. 
    5. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and golden. Serve hot.
