# Instructions

### Ingredients 
    - 2 tablespoons sesame oil
    - 12 ounces extra-firm tofu, drained, pressed, and cut into cubes
    - 3 cloves garlic, minced 
    - 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
    - 2 cups sliced lions mane mushrooms
    - 1/2 cup vegetable broth
    - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    - 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
    - 1/2 teaspoon sugar
    - 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes 
    
    ### Instructions 
    1. Heat the sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. 
    2. Add the cubed tofu and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring often, until lightly browned.
    3. Reduce the heat to medium-low, then add the garlic and ginger, stirring quickly to combine. 
    4. Add the lions mane mushrooms and cook for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
    5. Pour in the vegetable broth, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sugar, and red pepper flakes. 
    6. Cover the skillet and simmer for 10 minutes. 
    7. Uncover the skillet and cook for 5 more minutes. 
    8. Serve the stir-fry over steamed rice and enjoy!
