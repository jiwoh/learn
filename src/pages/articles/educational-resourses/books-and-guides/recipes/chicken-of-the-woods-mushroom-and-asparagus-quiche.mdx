# Instructions

###Ingredients:
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 cups Chicken-of-the-woods mushroom, chopped
    - 1 cup asparagus, chopped
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
    - 1/4 cup white or yellow onion, finely diced
    - 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    - 2 cups milk
    - 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
    - 4 large eggs
    - 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
    - 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
    - 1/2 cup shredded Swiss or Gruyere cheese
    - 1 unbaked 9-inch deep-dish pie crust
    
    ###Instructions:
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
    2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the mushrooms, garlic, asparagus, salt and pepper. Cook, stirring often, until the mushrooms are tender and the asparagus is cooked through, about 8 to 10 minutes.
    3. Add the onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until it is softened, about 2 minutes.
    4. Sprinkle the flour over the mixture and stir to combine. Cook for 1 minute.
    5. Gradually stir in the milk and bring to a simmer. Cook until the mixture thickens, about 5 minutes.
    6. Remove from the heat and stir in parsley.
    7. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
    8. Place the pie crust in a 9-inch pie dish.
    9. Sprinkle the Monterey Jack, cheddar, and Swiss cheese evenly over the bottom of the crust.
    10. Pour the vegetable mixture over the cheeses.
    11. Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables and cheese.
    12. Bake until the top is golden-brown and the center is set, about 30 minutes.
    13. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
