# Instructions

## Mushroom and Herb Stuffed Baked Potatoes 
    
    ### Ingredients:
    - 4-6 large russet potatoes
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - 1 onion, diced
    - 1 cup mushrooms, diced
    - 1 teaspoon fresh thyme, minced
    - 1 teaspoon fresh rosemary, minced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - Kosher salt
    - Black pepper
    - 3/4 cup grated cheese
    
    ### Instructions:
    
    1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
    
    2. Scrub potatoes clean and pat dry. Place potatoes on baking sheet and rub with olive oil. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
    
    3. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, heat butter over medium-high heat. Add onion, mushrooms, thyme, rosemary, garlic, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Cook until vegetables are softened, about 8 minutes.
    
    4. Cut potatoes in half lengthwise and scoop out the insides. Place potato halves back on baking sheet and spread the vegetable mixture into each potato half. Top with grated cheese.
    
    5. Bake for an additional 15 minutes, until cheese is melted and potatoes are heated through. Serve hot. Enjoy!
