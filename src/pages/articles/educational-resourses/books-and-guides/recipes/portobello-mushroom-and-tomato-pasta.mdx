# Ingredients

## Portobello Mushroom and Tomato Pasta Ingredients
    
    - 4 Portobello mushrooms
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 teaspoon dried basil
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
    - 2 plum tomatoes, diced
    - 1/4 cup dry white wine
    - 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese 
    - 1/2 pound penne pasta, cooked al dente

# Instructions

## Portobello Mushroom and Tomato Pasta Instructions
    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Grease a baking dish with oil or nonstick cooking spray.
    3. Slice the portobello mushrooms into ¼-inch thick slices and arrange them in the baking dish.
    4. Add the cherry tomatoes to the baking dish.
    5. Drizzle the mushrooms and tomatoes with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
    6. Roast in the preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until the mushrooms are tender.
    7. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
    8. Add the pasta and cook according to package directions.
    9. Drain the pasta and set aside.
    10. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
    11. Add the roasted mushrooms and tomatoes to the skillet.
    12. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are lightly browned and fragrant, about 5 minutes.
    13. Add the cooked pasta, garlic, and basil to the skillet and toss to combine.
    14. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    15. Serve warm. Enjoy!
