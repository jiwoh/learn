# Instructions

## Cremini Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Chicken
    
    ### Ingredients:
    - 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts 
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil 
    - 1 cup diced cremini mushrooms
    - 1 cup baby spinach 
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - ½ teaspoon dried sage 
    - ½ teaspoon dried thyme 
    - ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    - ¾ cup shredded mozzarella cheese 
    
    ### Directions: 
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F. 
    2. Place chicken breasts in a baking dish. 
    3. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. 
    4. Add mushrooms and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. 
    5. Add spinach and garlic and cook until spinach is wilted, about 3 minutes.
    6. Stir in sage, thyme, and pepper.
    7. Spoon mushroom and spinach filling into chicken breasts. 
    8. Top with cheese. 
    9. Bake until chicken is cooked through and cheese is melted, about 40 minutes. 
    10. Enjoy!
