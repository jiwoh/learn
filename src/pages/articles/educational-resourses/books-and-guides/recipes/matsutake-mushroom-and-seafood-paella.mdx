# Instructions

## Matsutake Mushroom and Seafood Paella 
    
    ### Ingredients : 
    * 2 tablespoons olive oil
    * 1/2 cup diced onion
    * 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
    * 1/2 cup diced celery
    * 2 cloves garlic, minced
    * 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
    * 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    * 1 teaspoon saffron threads
    * 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
    * 1 teaspoon salt
    * 2 cups Arborio rice
    * 3 1/2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
    * 1 cup white wine
    * 2 ounces chorizo, diced
    * 4 ounces shiitake mushrooms, sliced
    * 4 ounces matsutake mushrooms, sliced
    * 8 ounces shrimp, peeled and deveined
    * 8 ounces mussels
    * 8 ounces squid, sliced
    * 1/2 cup peas
    * 2 lemons, cut in wedges
    
    ### Instructions:
    1. Heat a large paella pan over medium-high heat and add the olive oil.
    2. Add the onion, bell pepper, celery, garlic, smoked paprika, oregano, saffron, and crushed red pepper. Cook for about 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are softened.
    3. Add the salt and rice and stir to combine.
    4. Add the broth and wine and bring to a simmer. Cook for 18-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice is cooked through and all the liquid has been absorbed.
    5. Add the chorizo, mushrooms, shrimp, mussels, squid, and peas. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, or until the seafood is cooked through.
    6. Serve with lemon wedges. Enjoy!
