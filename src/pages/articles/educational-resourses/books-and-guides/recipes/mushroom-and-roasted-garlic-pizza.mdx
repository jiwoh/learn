# Instructions

### Ingredients
    - 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
    - 2 cloves garlic, peeled and lightly crushed
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
    - 1 tablespoon butter
    - 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
    - 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    - 1 pre-made pizza crust 
    - 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese 
    
    ### Directions 
    1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
    2. In a medium bowl, toss mushrooms and garlic with olive oil and parsley. 
    3. Spread butter over the pizza crust. 
    4. Spread the mushroom and garlic mixture evenly over the pizza. 
    5. Sprinkle oregano, salt, Parmesan cheese and pepper over the pizza. 
    6. Top with the mozzarella cheese. 
    7. Bake in preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes, until cheese is melted and crust is golden brown.
