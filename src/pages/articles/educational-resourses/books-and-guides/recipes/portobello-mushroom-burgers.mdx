# Portobello Mushroom Burgers
    
    ## Origin and History
    Portobello Mushroom Burgers have been around for more than a century and their popularity has grown in recent years due to their versatility, flavour and excellent nutritional content. Originally from Italy, the Portobello Mushroom Burger combines the earthy flavour of a portobello mushroom with a variety of ingredients such as cheeses, sauces, and veggies to create a unique and delicious burger.

# Recipe Overview

    **Prep Time:** 15 minutes 
    
    **Cooking Time:** 10 minutes 
    
    **Skill Level:** Easy
    
    **Spice Level Scoville rating:** No spice
    
    To prepare Portobello Mushroom Burgers, you'll need a grill, spatula, and a bowl.

# Ingredients

**Ingredients:**
    
    - 4 large portobello mushrooms
    - 4 hamburger buns
    - 1/4 cup olive oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
    - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    - 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
    - 1 teaspoon dried basil
    - 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    - 1 teaspoon minced fresh parsley
    - 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
    - 2 tablespoons butter, melted
    - 4 slices mozzarella cheese
    - 4 slices tomato
    - 4 lettuce leaves

# Instructions

## Portobello Mushroom Burgers Instructions
    
    1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
    2. Clean and lightly brush the portobello mushrooms with olive oil on both sides.
    3. Grill the mushrooms for 4-5 minutes per side, or until the mushrooms have softened, and lightly browned.
    4. Place the buns on the grill and toast lightly.
    5. Assemble the burgers by layering a portobello mushroom slices on each bun and topping it with your favorite condiments. Serve immediately.
