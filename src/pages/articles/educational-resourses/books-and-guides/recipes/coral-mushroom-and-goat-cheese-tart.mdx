# Recipe Overview

    **Prep Time:** 25 minutes 
    **Cooking Time:** 35 minutes 
    **Skill Level:** Medium
    **Spice Level Scoville Rating:** 0
    
    You will need a tart pan, rolling pin, pastry brush, and bowl to prepare the Coral Mushroom and Goat Cheese Tart.

# Ingredients

## Ingredients 
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 10 ounces (about 3 cups) fresh coral mushrooms, trimmed and quartered 
    - 2 cloves garlic, thinly sliced 
    - 6 ounces soft goat cheese 
    - 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper 
    - 1/4 teaspoon salt 
    - 1/2 cup heavy cream 
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh thyme 
    - 1 (9-inch) ready-made pastry shell 
    - 1 large egg, lightly beaten

# Instructions

###### Preheat oven to 375°F
    
    1. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
    
    2. Add 8 ounces of chopped coral mushrooms and sauté for about 8 minutes, or until the mushrooms are browned and cooked through.
    
    3. Meanwhile, roll out a 9-inch round of store-bought or homemade pastry dough on a lightly floured surface. Place the pastry in a 9-inch tart pan and trim off any excess dough.
    
    4. Spread a thin layer of softened goat cheese over the pastry.
    
    5. Top the goat cheese with the sautéed mushrooms.
    
    6. Bake the tart in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until the pastry is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
    
    7. Remove the tart from the oven and let cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
