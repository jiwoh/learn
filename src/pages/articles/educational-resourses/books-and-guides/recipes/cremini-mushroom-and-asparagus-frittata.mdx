# Ingredients

* 8 eggs
    * 1/2 cup cremini mushrooms, diced
    * 1/2 cup asparagus, trimmed and diced
    * 1/4 cup onion, chopped
    * 2 cloves garlic, minced
    * 2 tablespoons olive oil
    * Salt and pepper, to taste

# Instructions

## Cremini Mushroom and Asparagus Frittata Instructions 
    1. Preheat oven to 375° F (190° C).
    2. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil over medium-high heat in a 10-inch oven-safe skillet.
    3. Add 1 cup of diced cremini mushrooms and 1 cup of chopped asparagus. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring often.
    4. In a medium bowl, whisk together 8 eggs, 2/3 cup of milk, 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder, 1/4 teaspoon of salt, and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
    5. Add egg mixture to skillet and simmer over medium-low heat until egg starts to set, about 5 minutes.
    6. Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes or until egg is cooked through.
    7. Turn oven on to broil, and broil for 2-3 minutes or until top of frittata is golden.
    8. Cut into wedges and serve. Enjoy!
