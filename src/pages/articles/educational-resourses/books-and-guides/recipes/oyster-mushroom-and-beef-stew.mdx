# Oyster Mushroom and Beef Stew
    
    Oyster mushroom and beef stew is an aromatic dish that combines two nutritious ingredients, oyster mushrooms and beef, in a rich, flavorful sauce. This comforting and hearty stew has its roots in Chinese culture, and is a popular choice for both everyday meals and special occasions. The combination of the two ingredients makes it both nutritious and filling, making it a perfect meal for all occasions.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Oyster Mushroom and Beef Stew is 20 minutes, the Cooking Time is 60 minutes, the Skill Level is Easy, and the Spice Level Scoville rating is Low. To prepare Oyster Mushroom and Beef Stew, you will need a large pot, a wooden spoon, a cutting board, a knife, and a colander.

# Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    - 2 pounds chuck beef, cubed
    - 1 onion, diced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 carrots, diced
    - 1 celery stalk, diced
    - 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    - 1 cup red wine
    - 2 cups beef broth
    - 3 bay leaves
    - 2 tablespoons fresh thyme leaves
    - 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
    - 8 ounces oyster mushrooms, shredded
    - 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

# Instructions

## Oyster Mushroom and Beef Stew Instructions
    
    1. Heat a large pot over medium-high heat and add 1 tablespoon of canola oil.
    
    2. Add 1 diced yellow onion and 1 teaspoon of minced garlic and saut√© for 5 minutes.
    
    3. Add 1 pound of lean ground beef and cook until lightly browned, about 5-7 minutes.
    
    4. Add 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika, 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano, 1/2 teaspoon of ground coriander, 1/2 teaspoon of ground cumin, and 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes. Stir to combine and cook for an additional minute.
    
    5. Add 2 cups of beef broth and bring to a simmer. Simmer for 10 minutes.
    
    6. Add 1 (8 ounce) package of sliced oyster mushrooms and simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
    
    7. Add 1 (14-ounce) can of diced tomatoes and season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for 5 minutes.
    
    8. Serve warm with cooked rice or vegetables. Enjoy!
