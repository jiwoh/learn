#  Coral Mushroom and Shrimp Stir-fry 
    
    ## Ingredients
    
    - 1 tablespoon vegetable oil 
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1/2 onion, chopped
    - 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
    - 1/2 pound oyster mushrooms 
    - 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
    - 2 tablespoons fish sauce
    - 4 tablespoons emerald onion, chopped
    - Salt and pepper, to taste
    
    ## Instructions
    
    1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. 
    2. Add garlic and onion and sauté until softened, about 3 minutes.
    3. Add shrimp and sauté until cooked, about 5 minutes. 
    4. Add mushrooms and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes. 
    5. Add oyster sauce and fish sauce and mix until everything is coated.
    6. Add emerald onion, salt, and pepper and stir until everything is mixed together.
    7. Serve and enjoy!
