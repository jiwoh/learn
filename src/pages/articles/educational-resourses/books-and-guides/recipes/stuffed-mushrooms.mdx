# Stuffed Mushrooms
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Stuffed Mushrooms are a classic Italian dish dating back to the 16th century. Traditionally made with breadcrumbs, cheese, and herbs, the dish has evolved over the years to include various combinations of ingredients, such as sundried tomatoes, sausage, spinach, and shallots. Today, it is a popular appetizer served in restaurants around the world.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Stuffed Mushrooms is 10 minutes, Cooking Time is 30 minutes, Skill Level is Medium, and Spice Level is Low on the Scoville rating scale. Utensils required for preparing Stuffed Mushrooms include a baking tray, small bowl, spoon, knife, and cutting board.

# Ingredients

- 24 large mushrooms 
    - 4 tablespoons butter, melted 
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced 
    - 1/4 cup minced onion 
    - 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley 
    - 8 ounces cream cheese, softened 
    - 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese 
    - 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano 
    - 1/4 teaspoon dried basil 
    - Salt and pepper to taste

# Instructions

## Stuffed Mushrooms Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F (176°C).
    2. Clean mushrooms by wiping with a damp cloth and removing stems.
    3. Place mushrooms on baking sheet and evenly spread 1 tablespoon of olive oil over them. 
    4. Prepare the stuffing:
        1. In a mixing bowl, combine 8 ounces (225 g) of soft cream cheese, ½ cup (50 g) of Parmesan cheese, 1 minced garlic clove, and chopped parsley to taste. Stir until all ingredients are combined.
    5. Stuff the mushrooms with the cheese mixture, mounding the mixture at the top of each mushroom.
    6. Bake mushrooms in preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until cheese is melted and golden.
    7. Serve mushrooms hot. Enjoy!
