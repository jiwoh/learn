# Instructions

## Mushroom and Eggplant Lasagna Roll-Ups
    
    ### Ingredients:
    - 8 uncooked lasagna noodles
    - 1 cup diced eggplant
    - 1 cup diced mushrooms
    - 1/4 cup diced onion
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
    - 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
    - 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    - Salt and pepper, to taste
    - 1/4 cup marinara sauce
    
    ### Instructions:
    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
    3. In a medium skillet over medium heat, saute eggplant, mushrooms, onion, garlic, oregano, and basil in olive oil until tender and lightly browned, about 8 minutes.
    4. In a medium bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
    5. Spread the ricotta cheese mixture onto each lasagna noodle. Place an equal amount of eggplant, mushroom, and onion mixture onto each.
    6. Roll up each noodle and place in a 9x13 inch baking dish. 
    7. Spoon marinara sauce evenly over each roll-up.
    8. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until heated through.
