# Ingredients

- 2 cups baby spinach
    - 1/2 cup beech mushrooms
    - 1/4 cup sliced red onion
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
    - Salt and pepper to taste

# Instructions

&lt;h2&gt;Steps to Make Beech Mushroom and Spinach Salad:&lt;/h2&gt;
    1. Heat a medium sized skillet over medium heat.
    2. Add 1 tablespoon of oil, 2 cloves of minced garlic, and 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes.
    3. Cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
    4. Add 1 pound of whole beech mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
    5. Remove from heat and allow to cool slightly.
    6. In a medium bowl, combine the beech mushrooms, 4 cups of baby spinach leaves, 1/4 cup of toasted pine nuts, 1/4 cup of diced red onion, and 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese.
    7. In a small bowl, whisk together 1/4 cup of olive oil, 1 tablespoon of balsamic vinegar, 1 tablespoon of lemon juice, 1 tablespoon of honey, 1/2 teaspoon of dijon mustard, and salt and pepper to taste. 
    8. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine. 
    9. Serve the salad and enjoy!
