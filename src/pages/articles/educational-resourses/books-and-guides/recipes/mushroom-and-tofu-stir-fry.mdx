# Instructions

### Ingredients:
    - 2 tablespoons canola oil
    - 8 ounces mushrooms, thinly sliced
    - 1 package (14 ounces) firm tofu, drained and cut into cubes 
    - 2 garlic cloves, minced
    - 3 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
    - 1 teaspoon freshly grated ginger
    - 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
    - 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
    
    ### Instructions:
    1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    2. Add the mushrooms and tofu and cook, stirring occasionally, until the mushrooms are tender and the tofu is lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
    3. Add the garlic and cook, stirring constantly, until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
    4. Stir in the soy sauce, ginger, and pepper.
    5. Reduce the heat to medium-low, cover, and cook until the tofu is heated through, about 5 minutes.
    6. Remove from the heat and stir in the sesame seeds. Serve warm.
