# Mushroom and Bacon Frittata
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom and Bacon Frittata originated in Italy and is a classic type of egg-based dish quite similar to an omelette. It is a great way to use up leftovers and is a one-pan meal that can be served at any time of day. Frittatas can be served hot or cold, and are enjoyed by all.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Mushroom and Bacon Frittata is 10 minutes, the Cooking Time for Mushroom and Bacon Frittata is 30 minutes, the Skill Level for Mushroom and Bacon Frittata is Easy, and the Spice Level Scoville rating for Mushroom and Bacon Frittata is 0 SHU. To prepare the Mushroom and Bacon Frittata, you will need a non-stick skillet, a wooden spoon, a cutting board, and a bowl.

# Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil 
    - 4 ounces bacon, diced 
    - 1/2 onion, diced 
    - 8 ounces mushrooms, thinly sliced 
    - 6 eggs 
    - 1/4 cup milk 
    - 2 tablespoons chopped parsley 
    - 2 teaspoons chopped thyme 
    - 1/4 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 
    - 1/2 cup shredded Gruyere cheese

# Instructions

# Mushroom and Bacon Frittata Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
    
    2. Heat a large non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add 3 slices of bacon and cook until crisp, about 5 minutes. Remove bacon from the skillet and place on a paper towel-lined plate to drain.
    
    3. To the same skillet, add 1 tablespoon olive oil and diced onion. Cook until onion is softened, about 5 minutes.
    
    4. Add diced mushrooms and continue cooking until mushrooms are softened, about 5 minutes.
    
    5. Add the bacon back to the skillet and season with salt and pepper.
    
    6. In a medium bowl, whisk together 8 eggs, ½ cup milk, ½ teaspoon dried thyme, and ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes.
    
    7. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes, until eggs start to set.
    
    8. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, until eggs are cooked through.
    
    9. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
