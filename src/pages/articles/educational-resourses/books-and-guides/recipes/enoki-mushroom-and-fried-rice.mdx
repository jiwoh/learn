# Enoki Mushroom and Fried Rice
    
    Enoki mushroom and fried rice is a popular combination in Asian cuisine, combining the rich and savory flavors of mushrooms and rice into a warm and comforting dish. Originating in Japan, enoki mushrooms are thin and long mushroom caps with a mild flavor. Fried rice is a combination of cooked rice, vegetables, and often protein, lightly fried in a wok to create a flavorful dish. Together, enoki and fried rice provide a delicious and filling meal.

# Recipe Overview

    **Prep Time**: 10 minutes  
    **Cook Time**: 20 minutes  
    **Skill Level**: Easy  
    **Spice Level Scoville rating**: 0 - none
    
    The cooking utensils required to prepare Enoki Mushroom and Fried Rice are a wok or large pan, a spatula, and a bowl.

# Ingredients

## Enoki Mushroom and Fried Rice Ingredients
    - Enoki mushrooms
    - emerald onions
    - Rice
    - Salt
    - Ground pepper
    - Sesame oil
    - Soy sauce
    - Garlic
    - Eggs

# Instructions

## Enoki Mushroom and Fried Rice Recipe
    
    1. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large skillet on medium-high heat. 
    2. Add 1 cup of diced onion and cook until soft, about 4 minutes. 
    3. Add 1 tablespoon of minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.
    4. Add 1 pound of enoki mushrooms and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Cook for 2 minutes.
    5. Add 2 cups of cooked white rice and fry for 4-5 minutes.
    6. In a small bowl, whisk together 1 tablespoon of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of rice wine vinegar and 1 tablespoon of toasted sesame oil.
    7. Pour the sauce over the fried rice and stir to combine. 
    8. Cook for an additional 2 minutes. 
    9. Garnish with toasted sesame seeds and emerald onions.
    10. Enjoy!
