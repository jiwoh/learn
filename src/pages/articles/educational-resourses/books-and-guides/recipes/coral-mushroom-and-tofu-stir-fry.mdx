## Coral Mushroom and Tofu Stir-fry
    
    #### Ingredients
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 4 cloves garlic, minced
    - 4 ounces coral mushrooms, sliced
    - 1 red bell pepper, cut into thin strips
    - 1 block extra firm tofu, cubed
    - 1 teaspoon cornstarch
    - 1 tablespoon reduced sodium soy sauce
    - 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
    - 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
    - 2 tablespoons honey
    
    #### Instructions
    1. Heat a large skillet over medium high heat. Add the vegetable oil and heat until shimmering.
    2. Add the garlic and mushrooms to the skillet and sauté for 4-5 minutes until the mushrooms are lightly browned.
    3. Add the bell pepper and tofu and continue to cook for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are softened.
    4. In a small bowl whisk together the cornstarch, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice wine vinegar, and honey.
    5. Pour sauce over vegetables and tofu and bring to a simmer. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 3-4 minutes or until the sauce has thickened.
    6. Serve warm over steamed rice. Enjoy!
