# Instructions

## Mushroom and Brie Bruschetta
    
    ### Ingredients
    - 1/2 pound mushrooms, sliced
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil 
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
    - 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
    - 2 ounces Brie cheese, cut into small cubes
    - 4 slices Italian or French bread
    - 2 tablespoons butter, melted
    
    ### Instructions
    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    2. In a medium bowl, combine the mushrooms, olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper, parsley, and balsamic vinegar. Mix until the mushrooms are evenly coated.
    3. Spread the mushroom mixture evenly onto a baking sheet. Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes.
    4. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, combine the Brie cubes with the butter.
    5. Toast the bread slices in a toaster or under a broiler.
    6. Once the mushrooms are done baking, spoon them on top of the toasted bread slices.
    7. Top with the Brie cubes.
    8. Place the bruschetta back under the broiler for a few minutes until the cheese is melted.
    9. Serve warm. Enjoy!
