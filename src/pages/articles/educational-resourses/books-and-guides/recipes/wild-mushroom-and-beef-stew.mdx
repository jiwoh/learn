# Recipe Overview

The Prep Time for Wild Mushroom and Beef Stew is 20 minutes, the Cooking Time is 45 minutes, the Skill Level is Easy and there is no Spice Level Scoville rating. Pots, pans, a cutting board, a sharp knife and a wooden spoon are among the utensils needed to prepare Wild Mushroom and Beef Stew.

# Ingredients

## Wild Mushroom and Beef Stew Ingredients
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 1 pound lean stew beef, cubed
    - 1 onion, finely diced
    - 2 cloves garlic, finely minced
    - 1 teaspoon minced fresh thyme leaves
    - 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    - 1 quart beef broth
    - 1/2 cup tomato paste
    - 12 ounces shiitake mushrooms, stemmed and sliced
    - 8 ounces cremini mushrooms, stemmed and quartered
    - Salt and freshly ground black pepper
    - 1/4 cup minced fresh parsley leaves

# Instructions

## Wild Mushroom and Beef Stew Instructions
    
    1. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
    2. Add 1 lb. of cubed stew beef and season with salt and pepper. Cook for 3-4 minutes.
    3. Add 2 tablespoons of butter and once melted, add 1 large diced onion and 4 cloves of minced garlic. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
    4. Add 2 tablespoons of tomato paste, 1 tablespoon of fresh thyme, and 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce. Stir to combine.
    5. Add 3 cups of beef broth, 1 bay leaf, and 1/2 cup of dry white wine. Bring to a boil.
    6. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes.
    7. Meanwhile, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a separate skillet over medium heat.
    8. Add 8 ounces of sliced mushrooms and season with salt and pepper. Saute for 4-5 minutes.
    9. Once the stew has cooked for 30 minutes, add the mushrooms.
    10. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
    11. Serve hot with freshly chopped parsley. Enjoy!
