# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Mushroom and herb stuffed beef roast is approximately 10 minutes, the Cooking Time is approximately 50 minutes, the Skill Level is medium and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 2,300 to 2,600. You will need a large plate, knife, cutting board, spoon, and larger roasting tin with lid or foil to prepare Mushroom and herb stuffed beef roast.

# Ingredients

# Mushroom and Herb Stuffed Beef Roast Ingredients
    
    - 1 beef roast
    - 1/2 cup fresh mushrooms, chopped
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1/2 cup onion, minced
    - 2 tablespoons fresh thyme, chopped
    - 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon pepper
    - 1/4 cup dry white wine

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Herb Stuffed Beef Roast Recipe
    
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
    
    2. Prepare the stuffing by combining 1 cup of mushrooms, ¼ cup of chopped parsley, 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 1 teaspoon of minced garlic, and 2 tablespoons of chopped sage in a bowl.
    
    3. Cut a pocket in a 2 lb beef roast.
    
    4. Fill the pocket with the stuffing.
    
    5. Place the roast in a roasting pan and cover with aluminum foil.
    
    6. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
    
    7. After 1 hour and 15 minutes, remove the foil and continue to bake until the roast is cooked through, about 30 minutes more.
    
    8. Let stand at least 10 minutes before slicing.
    
    9. Slice and serve. Enjoy!
