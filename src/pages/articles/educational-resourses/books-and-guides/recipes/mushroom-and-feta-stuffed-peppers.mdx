# Instructions

## Mushroom and Feta Stuffed Peppers
    
    ### Ingredients:
    - 4 bell peppers, any color
    - 1 tablespoon olive oil
    - 1/2 cup diced onion
    - 8 ounces sliced mushrooms
    - 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
    - 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
    - Salt and pepper, to taste
    - 3/4 cup cooked quinoa
    - 2/3 cup crumbled feta cheese
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
    
    ### Instructions:
    1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
    2. Cut the bell peppers in half lengthwise and remove the seeds. Place the pepper halves onto a greased baking sheet.
    3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and sauté until fragrant and translucent, about 2 minutes.
    4. Add the mushrooms, garlic powder, oregano, salt and pepper. Continue to cook until mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes.
    5. Add the cooked quinoa and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
    6. Remove the skillet from heat and stir in the feta cheese and chopped parsley.
    7. Divide the filling evenly among the pepper halves.
    8. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes.
    9. Serve with your favorite side dish. Enjoy!
