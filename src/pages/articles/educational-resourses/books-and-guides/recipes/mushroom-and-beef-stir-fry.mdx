# Instructions

# Mushroom and Beef Stir-Fry
    
    ## Ingredients
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 1/2 lb. lean ground beef
    - 1/2 lb. mushrooms, sliced
    - 1/2 onion, sliced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 1/2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
    - 1 1/2 teaspoons freshly grated ginger
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    - 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
    
    ## Instructions
    1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    
    2. Add the beef and cook, stirring occasionally, until browned, about 5 minutes.
    
    3. Add the mushrooms, onion, garlic, soy sauce, and ginger. Season with salt and pepper.
    
    4. Stir-fry until the vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes.
    
    5. Remove from the heat and stir in the cilantro. Serve immediately. Enjoy!
