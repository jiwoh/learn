    ## Origin and History

This classic combination of risotto rice and Parmesan cheese is often combined with the flavorful and unique mushroom known as the "Chicken of the Woods" mushroom. # Ingredients

## Chicken of the Woods Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto Ingredients
    * 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
    * 1 onion, minced
    * Sea salt
    * 2 cups Arborio rice
    * 1/2 cup dry white wine
    * 4 cups chicken broth
    * 2 tablespoons minced fresh thyme
    * 2 teaspoons minced fresh sage
    * 6-8 ounces fresh Chicken of the Woods mushroom, sliced
    * 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
    * 2 tablespoons minced flat-leaf parsley
    * Freshly ground black pepper

# Instructions

## Chicken of the Woods Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto Instructions
    
    1. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a medium-sized frying pan.
    
    2. Add 2 cloves of minced garlic, 1 diced onion and 1 cup of diced leeks and cook until softened.
    
    3. Add 1 cup of arborio rice and stir until it is coated with oil.
    
    4. Add 2 cups of chicken or vegetable broth and 1/2 cup of white wine and bring to a boil.
    
    5. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer, stirring occasionally, until the liquid is absorbed and the rice is cooked, about 25 minutes.
    
    6. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a separate skillet and add 1 cup of sliced chicken of the woods mushrooms. Cook until lightly browned.
    
    7. Remove the mushrooms from the skillet and set aside.
    
    8. Add the cooked mushrooms to the rice along with 6 tablespoons of grated Parmesan cheese. Stir to combine.
    
    9. Serve the risotto hot with extra Parmesan cheese, if desired. Enjoy!
