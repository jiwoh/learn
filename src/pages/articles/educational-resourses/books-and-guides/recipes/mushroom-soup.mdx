# Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
    - 1 medium onion, chopped
    - 1 clove garlic, minced
    - 2 pounds mushrooms, sliced
    - 4 cups chicken broth
    - 1 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
    - 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
    - 1/2 cup heavy cream
    - 2 tablespoons chopped chives (optional)

# Instructions

## Mushroom Soup Recipe
    
    1. Heat a large pot over medium heat. Add 2 tablespoons of butter and let it melt.
    
    2. Once the butter has melted, add 1 cup of chopped onion and 1/2 teaspoon of salt and cook until the onion is softened and translucent, about 3 minutes.
    
    3. Next, add 1 pound of sliced mushrooms, 1 teaspoon of dried thyme, and 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper. Cook, stirring frequently, until the mushrooms are softened, about 5 minutes.
    
    4. Add 4 cups of vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil.
    
    5. Reduce the heat to a simmer and let the soup cook for 15 minutes.
    
    6. Add 1/4 cup of heavy cream, stir, and let the mixture simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
    
    7. Serve with a sprinkle of chopped parsley and some crusty bread. Enjoy!
