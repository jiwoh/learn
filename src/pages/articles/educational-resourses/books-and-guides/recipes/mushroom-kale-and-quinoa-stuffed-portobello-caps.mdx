# Mushroom, Kale, and Quinoa Stuffed Portobello Caps
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom, Kale, and Quinoa Stuffed Portobello Caps is a vegetarian-friendly dish made with roasted portobello mushrooms, kale, quinoa, and a mix of flavorful seasonings. # Instructions

## Mushroom, Kale, and Quinoa Stuffed Portobello Caps
    
    ### Ingredients 
    - 4 large portobello mushroom caps
    - 1 tablespoon olive oil
    - 1/2 cup quinoa
    - 1 cup vegetable broth
    - 1 small onion, diced
    - 1 cup chopped kale
    - 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
    - 1/2 cup crumbled feta garlic
    - 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    
    ### Instructions
    1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
    2. Place the portobello mushrooms caps on a baking sheet and brush each cap with the olive oil.
    3. Bake the mushrooms for 10 minutes, until they are tender but not soggy.
    4. In a medium saucepan, bring the vegetable broth to a boil. Add the quinoa and reduce the heat to low and simmer for 15 minutes, or until all the liquid is absorbed. 
    5. Heat a small frying pan over medium heat. Add the onion and bell pepper and sauté for 5 minutes, or until tender.
    6. Add the kale and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
    7. In a medium bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, sautéed vegetables, feta cheese, oregano, and salt and pepper.
    8. Divide the mixture between the 4 mushroom caps.
    9. Bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, until the filling is hot and the mushroom caps are cooked through.
    10. Enjoy!
