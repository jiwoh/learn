# Enoki Mushroom and Scallop Risotto
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Enoki Mushroom and Scallop Risotto is a classic Italian dish. The flavorful combination of enoki mushroom, scallops, and arborio rice is popular among both home and restaurant chefs. The marriage of these ingredients creates an elegant and savory meal that is sure to please. There is some evidence of this dish being served in Italy for centuries, but it has recently gained popularity in the United States.

The necessary cooking utensils required to prepare Enoki Mushroom and Scallop Risotto are a cutting board, a knife, a pan, a bowl, and a stirring spoon.

# Ingredients

## Ingredients 
    - 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 shallots, minced
    - 2 cups arborio rice
    - 1/2 pound scallops, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
    - 1/2 pound Enoki mushrooms, trimmed and cut into 1/2 inch pieces
    - 2 teaspoons dried thyme
    - 6 cups chicken broth
    - 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
    - Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley leaves

# Instructions

## Enoki Mushroom and Scallop Risotto Instructions
    1. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a medium pot over medium heat.
    2. Add 1 finely chopped small onion and saut√© for 2-3 minutes until softened.
    3. Add 2 cups of Arborio rice and stir to combine.
    4. Pour in 3 cups of hot chicken broth and 1 cup of white wine.
    5. Bring to a simmer, reduce heat to low, and cover. Cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    6. While cooking, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a separate pan over medium heat.
    7. Add 1 package of Enoki mushrooms and 1 tablespoon of butter. Cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
    8. Add 1 pound of cleaned scallops to the Enoki mushrooms, cooking until they are golden brown, about 4-5 minutes.
    9. Take off heat and set aside.
    10. After 15 minutes, uncover the pot with the risotto and stir in the Enoki mushrooms and scallops.
    11. Cook for an additional 5 minutes until the rice is al dente.
    12. Serve and enjoy!
