# Instructions

## Cremini Mushroom and Feta Salad Instructions
    
    1. To begin, preheat oven to 400Â°F and line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper.
    
    2. Spread 2 cups of cleaned and sliced cremini mushrooms on the prepared baking sheet.
    
    3. Drizzle the mushrooms with 1 tablespoon of olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
    
    4. Roast the mushrooms in the oven for 15-20 minutes, tossing once or twice throughout cooking time.
    
    5. To assemble the salad, combine the roasted mushrooms with 2 cups of baby spinach, 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese and 1/4 cup of chopped walnuts in a large bowl.
    
    6. In a separate small bowl, whisk together 1 tablespoon of olive oil, 2 tablespoons of balsamic vinegar, 1 tablespoon of honey and 1 teaspoon of Dijon mustard.
    
    7. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
    
    8. Serve the Cremini Mushroom and Feta Salad immediately. Enjoy!
