# Instructions

### Portobello Mushroom and Roasted Garlic Risotto 
    
    #####Ingredients 
    - 2 tablespoons of olive oil
    - 2 shallots, finely chopped
    - 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
    - 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
    - 2 cups of arborio rice
    - 5-6 cups of vegetable broth 
    - Salt and pepper
    - 1/2 cup of white wine
    - 2-3 portobello mushrooms, cubed
    - 2 tablespoons of butter
    - 2 tablespoons of grated parmesan cheese
    - 2 tablespoons of freshly chopped parsley
    
    #####Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. 
    2. Place the garlic in aluminum foil and drizzle with olive oil. Wrap the garlic up and bake in the oven for 30 minutes, or until golden and soft. 
    3. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. 
    4. Add the shallots and sauté until they are softened and translucent. 
    5. Add the tomato paste and cook for a few minutes. 
    6. Add the arborio rice and stir to coat with the oil and tomato paste.
    7. Pour in the white wine and stir for 1-2 minutes. 
    8. Start adding the vegetable stock, one ladle at a time, stirring continuously until the liquid is absorbed. 
    9. Continue adding the stock, one ladle at a time, stirring continuously until all the liquid is absorbed and the rice is creamy and al dente.
    10. Add the cubed portobello mushrooms and stir to combine. 
    11. Remove from heat and add the butter, roasted garlic, and parmesan cheese. Stir until melted. 
    12. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and serve.
