# Ingredients

### Mushroom and Gruyere Tart Ingredients
    - 453 g puff pastry
    - 2 tablespoons of olive oil
    - 1 clove garlic, minced
    - 225 g cremini mushrooms, sliced
    - 175 g Swiss brown mushrooms, sliced
    - 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh thyme
    - 1 teaspoon of sea salt
    - 1/2 teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper
    - 4 tablespoons of Gruyere cheese, grated
    - 2 eggs, lightly beaten
    - 2 teaspoons of sesame seed

# Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    2. In a medium bowl, mix together 2 cups shredded Gruyere cheese, 1/4 cup minced fresh parsley, and 1/4 cup minced fresh thyme. Set aside.
    3. Place 1 sheet of frozen puff pastry on a lightly floured work surface. Brush with 1 egg beaten with 1 tablespoon of cold water. Cut into 4 equal squares.
    4. Place 4 (approximately 9-inch) tart pans on a baking sheet. Place a cut piece of puff pastry in each tart pan.
    5. Spread 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard over each piece of pastry.
    6. Divide the Gruyere cheese mixture among the 4 tarts, mounding the mixture in the center slightly.
    7. Arrange 1 1/2 cups of sliced mushrooms over the cheese mixture in each tart.
    8. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, or until golden brown.
    9. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
