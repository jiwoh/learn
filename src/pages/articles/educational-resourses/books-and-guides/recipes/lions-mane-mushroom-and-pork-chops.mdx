# Instructions

## Lions Mane Mushroom and Pork Chops
    
    ### Ingredients
    - 2 bone-in pork chops
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - 2 cloves minced garlic
    - 2 tablespoons finely chopped shallots
    - 2 cups sliced Lions Mane mushrooms
    - 2 tablespoons fresh thyme leaves
    - 2 tablespoons white wine
    - Salt and pepper, to taste
    
    ### Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    3. Season both sides of the pork chops with salt and pepper.
    4. Place the pork chops in the skillet and cook for 4 minutes on each side.
    5. Transfer the pork chops to an oven-safe baking dish.
    6. In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium-high heat and add the garlic and shallots. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    7. Add the Lions Mane mushrooms to the skillet and stir in the thyme leaves. Cook for 5 minutes, until they are tender.
    8. Add the white wine and cook for a minute or two, stirring to scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan.
    9. Pour the mushroom mixture over the pork chops in the baking dish.
    10. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until the pork chops are cooked through. 
    11. Serve and enjoy!
