# Portobello Mushroom and Red Pepper Panini 
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    The Portobello Mushroom and Red Pepper Panini is a modern Italian classic that is believed to have originated in the late 1990s, with the concept of a “pressed sandwich” becoming popular at the time. Since then, it has become an international favorite, with variations around the globe. The Portobello Mushroom and Red Pepper Panini is a combination of two flavorful ingredients: earthy mushrooms and sweet bell peppers, lightly grilled between two slices of crusty bread and served hot.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Portobello Mushroom and Red Pepper Panini is 15 minutes, the Cooking Time is 10 minutes, the Skill Level is Easy and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0. You'll need a panini press or grill, a cutting board and a knife to prepare Portobello Mushroom and Red Pepper Panini.

# Ingredients

## Portobello Mushroom and Red Pepper Panini Ingredients
    * 2 Portobello mushrooms, stemmed and sliced
    * ¼ cup roasted red peppers, sliced
    * 2 slices of sourdough bread
    * 2 tablespoons olive oil
    * 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
    * Salt and pepper to taste
    * 2 tablespoons cream cheese spread
    * 2 slices of mozzarella cheese
    * 2 tablespoons butter, softened

# Instructions

## Portobello Mushroom and Red Pepper Panini Instructions
    1. Preheat a panini press or a grill to medium-high heat.
    2. Slice a large portobello mushroom cap into ¼ inch slices.
    3. Slice 1 red pepper into thin strips.
    4. Spread a thin layer of olive oil onto the inside of two slices of crusty Italian bread.
    5. Place the portobello mushroom slices and the red pepper strips onto one side of the bread slices.
    6. Sprinkle a pinch of salt and pepper over the ingredients.
    7. Place the other piece of bread onto the ingredients.
    8. Place the sandwich onto the panini press or grill.
    9. Grill the sandwich for 4 minutes.
    10. Carefully turn the sandwich over and grill for an additional 3 minutes until golden brown.
    11. Serve and enjoy!
