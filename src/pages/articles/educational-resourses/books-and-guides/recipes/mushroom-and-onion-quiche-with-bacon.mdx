# Mushroom and Onion Quiche with Bacon
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom and onion quiche with bacon is a classic dish found in French cuisine. It has been around since the 16th century, when it was first created as an open-faced pastry filled with a savory custard. Today, it is often topped with bacon, mushrooms, and onions for a satisfying and flavorful meal. This dish has become extremely popular in recent years, and can be found in many traditional and modern recipes.

# Recipe Overview

*Prep Time: 15 minutes
    *Cooking Time: 40 minutes
    *Skill Level: Moderate
    *Spice Level Scoville rating: 0
    
    To prepare the Mushroom and Onion Quiche with Bacon, you will need a whisk, 9-inch pie plate, skillet, blade knife, cutting board, and baking dish.

# Ingredients

### Ingredients
    
    - 1 9-inch unbaked pie crust
    - 3 pieces of bacon, diced
    - 2 cloves of garlic, minced 
    - 2 tablespoons of butter
    - 4 ounces of mushrooms, sliced
    - 1/2 cup of chopped onion 
    - 2 eggs
    - 1/4 cup of heavy cream
    - 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
    - 1/8 teaspoon of garlic powder
    - 1/2 teaspoon of salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
    - 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
    - 1/3 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese

# Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    
    2. Grease a 9-inch (23 cm) pie dish and set aside.
    
    3. In a medium bowl, mix together 6 slices of cooked bacon, diced, 1/2 cup (125 mL) each of chopped mushrooms and onions, 1/4 cup (60 mL) of grated cheese, 1/4 cup (60 mL) of chopped parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon (2 mL) of salt.
    
    4. Beat together 4 eggs and 1 cup (250 mL) of cream in a medium bowl.
    
    5. Pour egg mixture into the prepared pie dish.
    
    6. Evenly spread the bacon and vegetable mixture over the egg mixture.
    
    7. Bake in preheated oven for 40 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through.
    
    8. Serve and enjoy!
