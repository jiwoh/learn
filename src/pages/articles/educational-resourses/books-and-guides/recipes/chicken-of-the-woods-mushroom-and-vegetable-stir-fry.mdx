# Chicken of the Woods Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Chicken of the Woods Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry is a classic Chinese dish originating from Fujian Province. First documented in the Song Dynasty (960-1279), this dish is traditionally prepared with chicken, mushrooms, and vegetables stir-fried in a light ginger-soy sauce. The modern version features the flavorful and nutrient-rich Chicken of the Woods mushroom, a unique fungus found in the wild throughout rural China. Today, it is enjoyed as a hearty and healthy dish throughout the world, as well as a popular vegan and vegetarian alternative.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Chicken of the Woods Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry is 15 minutes, the Cooking Time is 20 minutes, the Skill Level is Easy and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0. To make this dish, you'll need a stove, a large skillet, a wooden spoon, a cutting board, a knife, and a bowl.

# Ingredients

### Ingredients 
    
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 small onion, finely chopped
    - 2 cups Chicken of the Woods mushrooms, diced
    - 1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced
    - 2 cups broccoli florets
    - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    - 1 tablespoon sesame oil
    - 1 teaspoon sugar
    - Salt and pepper, to taste

# Instructions

## Chicken of the Woods Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry Instructions
    
    1. Soak chicken of the wood mushrooms in water for around 15 minutes.
    
    2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add the oil.
    
    3. Add the diced onions and stir to coat with the oil. Cook the onions until they are almost translucent, about 3 minutes.
    
    4. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes and cook an additional minute.
    
    5. Drain the soaked mushrooms and add them to the skillet.
    
    6. Add the diced bell peppers and stir well to combine.
    
    7. Cook for an additional 3 minutes.
    
    8. Add the soy sauce and stir well.
    
    9. Add the cooked rice and stir until everything is combined.
    
    10. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    
    11. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
    
    12. Serve hot. Enjoy!
