# Ingredients

## Ingredients
    - 1 pound jumbo shells
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 1 medium onion, finely minced
    - 8 ounces crimini mushrooms, finely diced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    - 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
    - 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
    - 2 cups ricotta cheese
    - 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    - 1 (26 ounce) jar of marinara sauce
    - 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

# Mushroom, Spinach, and Ricotta Stuffed Shells
    
    ## Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
    2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    3. Cook the jumbo shells for 9-10 minutes, until al dente.
    4. Meanwhile, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    5. Add the mushrooms and sauté for 3-4 minutes, until lightly browned.

    12. Pour the marinara sauce over the shells.
