# Instructions

## Mushroom and Chicken Pot Pie
    
    ### Ingredients: 
    * 2 tablespoons butter 
    * 2 tablespoons olive oil 
    * 1 medium onion, diced
    * 4 cloves garlic, minced
    * 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
    * 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    * 2 cups chicken broth
    * 1 cup milk 
    * 2 cups diced cooked chicken
    * 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
    * 1/4 teaspoon thyme
    * 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
    * Salt, to taste
    * 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley 
    * 2/3 cup biscuit mix
    * 2 tablespoons butter, melted
    
    ### Instructions: 
    1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
    
    2. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt 2 tablespoons of butter with olive oil. Stir in the onion, garlic, and mushrooms. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the mushrooms are lightly browned, about 10 minutes.
    
    3. Reduce heat to low and sprinkle the flour over the mushroom mixture. Stir until mixture is blended, and cook for an additional minute.
    
    4. Add the chicken broth and milk. Cook, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens and boils.
    
    5. Stir in the chicken, frozen vegetables, thyme, pepper, and salt. Continue to cook over medium-low heat until the vegetables are tender, about 10 minutes.
    
    6. Stir in the parsley.
    
    7. Pour the mixture into a 9-inch pie plate.
    
    8. In a small bowl, stir together the biscuit mix and melted butter. Sprinkle the biscuit mixture over the chicken and mushroom mixture in the pie plate.
    
    9. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes, or until the biscuit topping is golden brown.
