King Trumpet mushrooms have been used for centuries in French cooking, and when paired with asparagus, the combination creates a wonderfully light and flavorful tart. # Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for King Trumpet Mushroom and Asparagus Tart is 20 minutes, the Cooking Time is 30 minutes, the Skill Level is beginner, and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0. To prepare this dish you will need a 9-inch tart pan, rolling pin, and a baking sheet.

# Ingredients

**Ingredients**
    
    - 4 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1 red onion, thinly sliced 
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
    - 2 pounds king trumpet mushrooms, thinly sliced
    - 5 ounces baby spinach
    - 2 bunches asparagus, ends trimmed and cut into 1/2-inch pieces
    - 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
    - Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
    - 1 (9-inch) pre-made pie crust
    - 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
    - 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    - 1/4 cup toasted pine nuts, optional

# Instructions

## Ingredients
    - 1 sheet frozen puff pastry
    - 2 cups King Trumpet mushrooms, chopped into cubes
    - 1/4 cup olive oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
    - 2 tablespoons fresh thyme
    - 1 teaspoon sea salt
    - 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
    - 2 cups baby asparagus, sliced into thin matchsticks
    - 2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
    
    ## Instructions
    
    1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
    
    2. Place the puff pastry sheet on the lined baking sheet and spread it out.
    
    3. In a large bowl, combine the mushrooms, olive oil, garlic, thyme, salt and pepper. Mix until everything is well combined.
    
    4. Add the asparagus and parmesan cheese and mix again.
    
    5. Spread the mixture onto the puff pastry sheet, leaving a 1-inch border.
    
    6. Fold over the edges of the puff pastry so the filling is completely covered.
    
    7. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes, or until the puff pastry is golden and the filling is cooked through.
    
    8. Serve warm. Enjoy!
