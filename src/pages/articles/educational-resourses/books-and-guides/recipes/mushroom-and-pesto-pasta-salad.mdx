# Instructions

## Mushroom and Pesto Pasta Salad
    
    ### Ingredients:
    - 8 ounces of dry tricolored pasta
    - 1 (14-ounce) can quartered artichoke hearts, drained
    - 8 ounces sliced button mushrooms
    - 1/4 cup prepared pesto
    - 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
    - 3 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
    - Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
    
    ### Instructions:
    1. Cook the pasta according to package directions.
    2. Drain the cooked pasta and rinse with cold water.
    3. Transfer the cooled pasta to a large serving bowl and add the artichoke hearts, mushrooms, pesto, olive oil, and lemon juice.
    4. Sprinkle the salt and pepper to taste and toss the ingredients together.
    5. Serve the pasta salad cold or at room temperature. Enjoy!
