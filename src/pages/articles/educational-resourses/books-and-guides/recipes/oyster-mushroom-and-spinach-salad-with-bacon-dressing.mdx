## Oyster Mushroom and Spinach Salad with Bacon Dressing
    
    ### Ingredients
    
    - 4 slices bacon, diced
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    - 1/4 cup white wine vinegar
    - 2 tablespoons honey
    - 1/4 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    - 8 ounces oyster mushrooms, quartered
    - 2 bunches fresh spinach, washed, stemmed and torn
    - 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
    
    ### Instructions
    
    1. In a medium skillet, cook the bacon over medium-high heat until crisp. Drain on a paper towel and set aside.
    
    2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, garlic powder, vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper.
    
    3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add the mushrooms. Saute for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the mushrooms are lightly browned.
    
    4. Add the spinach and bacon to the skillet and cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the spinach is wilted.
    
    5. Divide the salad among bowls and drizzle the vinaigrette over the top. Sprinkle with feta cheese and serve.
