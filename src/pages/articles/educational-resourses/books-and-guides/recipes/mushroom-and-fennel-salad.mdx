# Mushroom and Fennel Salad
    
    ## Ingredients
    
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1/2 a fennel bulb, sliced
    - 1 garlic clove, chopped
    - 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
    - 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
    - Salt and pepper, to taste
    
    ## Instructions
    
    1. Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
    
    2. Add the fennel and garlic and cook, stirring occasionally, until the fennel is softened and lightly golden brown, about 8 minutes.
    
    3. Add the mushrooms and continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until the mushrooms are softened and lightly browned, about 6 minutes.
    
    4. Remove from the heat and add the lemon juice, parsley, salt and pepper.
    
    5. Transfer the salad to a serving dish and serve. Enjoy!
