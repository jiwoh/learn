    The Prep Time for Mushroom, Spinach, and Goat Cheese Quesadilla is 10 minutes, the Cooking Time is 10 minutes, the Skill Level is Beginner, and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0. In order to prepare this recipe you will need a cutting board, knife, skillet, measuring cups, and spatula.

# Ingredients

### Mushroom, Spinach, and Goat Cheese Quesadilla Ingredients
    - 4 flour or whole wheat tortillas
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 teaspoons dried oregano
    - 2 cups baby spinach leaves
    - 6 ounces soft goat cheese
    - Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

# Instructions

## Mushroom, Spinach, and Goat Cheese Quesadilla Instructions
    
    1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
    2. Add 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil to the pan.
    3. Add 8 ounces of sliced mushrooms, 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder, and 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder to the pan.
    4. Saute the mushrooms until they are soft and lightly browned, about 8 minutes.
    5. Add 1 cup of fresh spinach to the pan and stir to combine.
    6. Saute until the spinach is wilted, about 2 minutes.
    7. Remove the skillet from the heat and set aside.
    8. Heat a large non-stick skillet over medium heat.
    9. Place two 8 inch flour tortillas in the pan.
    10. Spread a layer of goat cheese on one side of each tortilla.
    11. Top the goat cheese with the mushroom and spinach mixture.
    12. Top the mushroom and spinach mixture with another layer of goat cheese.
    13. Place the remaining tortilla on top of the cheese to form a quesadilla.
    14. Cook the quesadilla on one side for 3-4 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is golden and crispy.
    15. Carefully flip the quesadilla over and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes on the other side.
    16. Cut the quesadilla into fourths and serve warm.
