# Instructions

## Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Chicken Breasts
    
    ### Ingredients:
    - 4 boneless and skinless chicken breasts
    - 4 ounces of cream cheese
    - 2 cups of cooked spinach
    - 4 ounces of mushrooms, chopped
    - 1/4 cup of shredded Parmesan cheese
    - 2 cloves of garlic, minced
    - 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    
    ### Instructions:
    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
    2. In a medium bowl, combine cream cheese, spinach, mushrooms, Parmesan cheese, garlic, Italian seasoning, salt and pepper; stir until ingredients are well blended.
    3. Place chicken breasts onto a greased baking dish.
    4. Open chicken breasts and stuff with the spinach mixture; use kitchen twine to tie the chicken breasts.
    5. Bake for 30 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through. 
    6. Let chicken cool; serve with your favorite side dish.
