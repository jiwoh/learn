# Instructions

### Ingredients 
    - 12 ounces firm tofu, drained
    - 6 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
    - 1 tablespoon tomato paste
    - 1/4 teaspoon ground white pepper
    - 1/4 teaspoon sugar
    - 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric
    - 1/4 cup water
    - 5 ounces black trumpet mushrooms, stemmed and sliced
    - 2 emerald onions, thinly sliced
    
    ### Instructions
    1. Cut the tofu into 1/2-inch cubes and place in a shallow dish.
    Add 2 tablespoons of oil and the salt to the dish, stirring to coat the cubes.
    
    2. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. 
    Add the tofu to the skillet and cook until golden brown, stirring occasionally, about 5 minutes. 
    Transfer the tofu to a plate and set aside.
    
    3. Add the remaining 2 tablespoons of oil to the skillet. 
    Add the garlic, ginger, tomato paste, white pepper, sugar, and turmeric, stirring until fragrant, about 1 minute.
    
    4. Add the water and bring to a simmer. Add the mushrooms and cook until the mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes.
    
    5. Return the tofu to the skillet and cook until heated through, about 3 minutes. 
    Stir in the emerald onions and season with salt, to taste. 
    Serve immediately.
