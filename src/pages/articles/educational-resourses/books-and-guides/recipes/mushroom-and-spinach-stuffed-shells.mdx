# Recipe Overview

    - Prep Time: 30 minutes
    - Cooking Time: 25 minutes
    - Skill Level: Easy
    - Spice Level Scoville Rating: 0 - 1,000
    
    You will need a baking dish, a pot, a mixing bowl, a spoon, and a stove to prepare Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Shells.

# Ingredients

- 12 ounces jumbo shell pasta 
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil 
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced 
    - 1/2 cup diced onion 
    - 1 package (8 ounces) sliced mushrooms 
    - 1 teaspoon dried basil 
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt 
    - 1/4 teaspoon pepper 
    - 1 package (10 ounces) frozen chopped spinach, thawed, drained and squeezed dry 
    - 1 container (15 ounces) ricotta cheese 
    - 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese 
    - 1 egg 
    - 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese 
    - 1 jar (24 ounces) marinara sauce

# Instructions

# Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Shells
    
    ## Instructions
    
    1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
    2. Cook the jumbo shells according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
    3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the mushrooms and onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
    4. Add the garlic, spinach and salt and pepper. Cook for an additional 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    5. Remove from heat and stir in the ricotta cheese, mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
    6. Grease a 9x13 baking dish with cooking spray.
    7. Stuff each jumbo shell with the cheese and mushroom mixture and place in the baking dish.
    8. Pour the marinara sauce over the stuffed shells and top with the additional mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
    9. Cover and bake for 20-25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
    10. Let cool for 5-10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
