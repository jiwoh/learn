# Porcini Mushroom and Beef Stew
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Porcini Mushroom and Beef Stew is a classic Italian recipe, dating back centuries. The ingredients used in the stew are typical to Italian cooking, and have remained the same for generations. It consists of beef, mushrooms, onions, white wine, and herbs in a tomato-based sauce. It's a hearty and comforting dish, perfect for a cold winter night.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Porcini Mushroom and Beef Stew is 30 minutes, the Cooking Time for the dish is 2.30 hours, the Skill Level for the dish is Moderate, and the Spice Level Scoville rating is Low. For preparing Porcini Mushroom and Beef Stew, you need a Dutch oven, cutting board, knife, stock pot, and wooden spoon.

# Ingredients

* 1 pound stew meat
    * 1 onion, chopped
    * 4 cloves garlic, minced
    * 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    * 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    * 4 cups beef stock
    * 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
    * 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
    * 2 tablespoons light brown sugar
    * 2 tablespoons brandy
    * 2 tablespoons fresh thyme leaves
    * 1/2 cup dried porcini mushrooms, soaked in 1 cup warm water for 15 minutes
    * 1 bay leaf
    * 2 tablespoons olive oil
    * Salt and pepper, to taste

# Instructions

## Porcini Mushroom and Beef Stew Instructions
    
    1. Place 1 ounce of dried porcini mushrooms in a bowl and cover with 1 cup of boiling water. Let stand for 30 minutes.
    
    2. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large soup pot over medium heat. Add 1 chopped onion and 1 chopped celery stalk and cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 5 minutes.
    
    3. Add 1 pound of cubed stewing beef and cook until browned, about 10 minutes.Season with salt and pepper.
    
    4. Add 1 teaspoon of dried thyme, and 1 bay leaf. Cook for 1 minute.
    
    5. Add the porcini mushrooms and the soaking liquid, 4 cups of beef broth, 1 cup of white wine, 1/2 cup of diced carrots, and 2 teaspoons of tomato paste. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer, covered, for 1 hour.
    
    6. Add 1 diced potato and 1/4 pound of chopped fresh mushrooms. Simmer, covered, for 30 minutes.
    
    7. Remove bay leaf and season with additional salt and pepper, to taste. Serve warm.
