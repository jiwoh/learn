# Ingredients

### Ingredients
    - 10 medium shiitake mushrooms
    - 2 tablespoons grated ginger
    - 4 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    - 1/2 cup vegetable broth
    - 2 tablespoons sesame oil
    - 8 ounces extra-firm tofu, drained and cubed
    - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    - 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
    - 1 red bell pepper, julienned
    - 2 cups cooked brown rice

# Instructions

## Shiitake Mushroom and Tofu Stir-fry Instructions
    
    1. In a large bowl, mix together the shiitake mushrooms, tofu, garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch.
    
    2. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat and add the vegetable oil.
    
    3. Add the mushroom and tofu mixture to the skillet and cook until lightly browned, about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    
    4. Add the bell pepper and scallions and cook for an additional few minutes, stirring occasionally until the vegetables are lightly cooked.
    
    5. Add the rice wine vinegar, sugar, and a pinch of salt. Stir to combine and cook for an additional few minutes.
    
    6. Serve the stir-fry over cooked brown rice and garnish with sesame seeds and emerald onions, if desired. Enjoy!
