# Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Turkey Breast 
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom and spinach stuffed turkey breast is a classic dish that dates back to the 18th century. Originally created in England, the dish has become popular in other parts of the world and is now a favorite in many households. The dish typically consists of turkey breast that is stuffed with a mixture of mushrooms, spinach and herbs before being cooked. This dish is a delicious and nutritious way to enjoy turkey that is sure to impress your guests!

# Recipe Overview

**Prep Time:** 20 minutes  
    **Cooking Time:** 1 hour and 15 minutes  
    **Skill Level:** Medium  
    **Spice Level Scoville Rating:** Mild  
    To prepare Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Turkey Breast you will need a baking dish, a large skillet, a spoon, a knife, and aluminum foil.

# Ingredients

## Mushroom and spinach stuffed turkey breast Ingredients
    
    - 6-7 lb turkey breast, deboned
    - 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
    - 1 onion, chopped
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
    - 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    - ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
    - 1 teaspoon salt
    - ½ teaspoon black pepper
    - 2 cups baby spinach
    - 1 cup ricotta cheese
    - ½ cup Parmesan cheese, grated
    - 1 egg, beaten
    - 3 tablespoons butter, melted

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Turkey Breast Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
    
    2. In a medium bowl, combine 1/2 cup of diced onion, 1/2 cup of chopped mushrooms, 1/2 cup of thawed and drained spinach, 2 tablespoons of olive oil and 1 teaspoon of salt.
    
    3. Place the turkey breast on a baking sheet and season the outside with 1 teaspoon of salt and 1/4 teaspoon of pepper.
    
    4. Spread the stuffing mixture in the center of the turkey breast.
    
    5. Roll the turkey breast over the stuffing and tie it closed with kitchen twine.
    
    6. Brush the turkey breast with 2 tablespoons of melted butter.
    
    7. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 350°F and bake for an additional 30 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F.
    
    8. Allow the turkey breast to rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
