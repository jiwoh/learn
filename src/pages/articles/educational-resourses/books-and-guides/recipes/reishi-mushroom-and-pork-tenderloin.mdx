    ## Origin and History

This pairing is believed to have originated in China, although it is popular in many other countries. # Instructions

## Reishi Mushroom and Pork Tenderloin Recipe
    
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
    
    2. Heat a lightly oiled skillet over medium heat.
    
    3. Add 1/2 cup of reishi mushrooms and cook until lightly browned.
    
    4. Place pork tenderloin in a roasting pan and season with salt and pepper to taste.
    
    5. Take the cooked reishi mushrooms out of the skillet and spread them over the pork tenderloin.
    
    6. Roast in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until a thermometer inserted into the center of the pork registers 145°F.
    
    7. Let the pork rest on a cutting board for 5 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
