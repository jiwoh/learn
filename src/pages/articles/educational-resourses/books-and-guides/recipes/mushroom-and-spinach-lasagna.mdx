# Mushroom and Spinach Lasagna
    
    ## Ingredients
    * 2 tablespoons olive oil
    * 1 onion, chopped
    * 2 cloves garlic, minced
    * 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen spinach, thawed and drained
    * 1 (16 ounce) package white mushrooms, sliced
    * 2 (26 ounce) jars spaghetti sauce
    * 12 lasagna noodles
    * 3 eggs
    * 2 (15 ounce) containers ricotta cheese
    * 1/2 teaspoon salt
    * 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    * 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    
    ## Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    3. Add the onion and garlic and cook until lightly browned and softened, about 5 minutes.
    4. Add the mushrooms and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
    5. Stir in the spinach and cook until heated through, about 5 minutes.
    6. Stir in one of the jars of spaghetti sauce and mix until combined.
    7. Spread a thin layer of the remaining spaghetti sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    8. Arrange 6 lasagna noodles in the bottom of the dish.
    9. In a large bowl, beat the eggs with the ricotta cheese, salt, and pepper. Mix until combined.
    10. Spread the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
    11. Layer the remaining 6 lasagna noodles over the ricotta cheese layer.
    12. Spread the mushroom and spinach mixture over the noodles.
    13. Top with remaining jar of spaghetti sauce.
    14. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
    15. Bake for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
    16. Let cool for 15 minutes before cutting and serving. Enjoy!
