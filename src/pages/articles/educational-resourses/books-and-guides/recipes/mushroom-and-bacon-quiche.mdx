# Mushroom and Bacon Quiche 
    
    ## Ingredients 
    * 2 cups chopped mushrooms
    * 1/2 cup cooked, crumbled bacon
    * 3 eggs
    * 1/2 cup heavy cream
    * 1/2 cup shredded Swiss cheese
    * 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    * 1/3 cup diced yellow onion
    * 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    * Salt and pepper, to taste
    * 9-inch unbaked pie crust
    
    ## Instructions 
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
    2. In a medium bowl, mix together mushrooms, bacon, and onion.
    3. Distribute the mushroom and bacon mixture evenly over the bottom of the pie crust.
    4. In a small bowl whisk together eggs, cream, cheeses, garlic powder, and salt and pepper.
    5. Pour egg mixture evenly over the mushroom and bacon mixture.
    6. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until top is golden brown and a knife inserted into the center comes out clean.
    7. Let cool for 5 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!
