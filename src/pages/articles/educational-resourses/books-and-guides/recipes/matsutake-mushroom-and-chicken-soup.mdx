# Instructions

## Matsutake Mushroom and Chicken Soup
    
    ### Ingredients
    
    - 4 chicken breasts, cubed
    - 3 tablespoons oil
    - 2 medium onions, diced
    - 4 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 tablespoons grated ginger
    - 1 tablespoon soy sauce
    - 1 teaspoon Chinese five-spice powder
    - 6 cups vegetable broth
    - 4 cups water
    - 2 cups matsutake mushrooms, sliced
    - 2 tablespoons cornstarch
    - 2 tablespoons cold water
    - Salt and pepper, to taste
    
    ### Instructions
    
    1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
    
    2. Add the chicken cubes and cook until lightly browned, about 4 minutes.
    
    3. Add the onions and garlic and cook until the onions are softened, about 3 minutes.
    
    4. Stir in the ginger, soy sauce, and five-spice powder and cook for 1 minute.
    
    5. Pour in the vegetable broth and water and bring the mixture to a boil.
    
    6. Add the mushrooms, reduce the heat to low, and simmer for 15 minutes.
    
    7. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and cold water until combined.
    
    8. Stir the cornstarch mixture into the soup and simmer for an additional 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    
    9. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
    
    10. Serve hot. Enjoy!
