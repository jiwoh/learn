# Instructions

# Mushroom and Sweet Potato Hash
    
    ## Ingredients 
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil 
    - 1/2 large onion, chopped 
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 8-10 ounces mushrooms of your choice, sliced 
    - 2 sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
    - 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
    - 2 tablespoons fresh chopped parsley
    - Salt and fresh ground pepper to taste 
    
    ## Instructions 
    
    1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. 
    2. Add the onion and sauté for 6-7 minutes, until softened and lightly browned.
    3. Add the garlic and sauté for another 1-2 minutes. 
    4. Increase heat to medium-high and add the mushrooms. 
    5. Sauté the mushrooms for 4-5 minutes until they are lightly browned. 
    6. Add the diced sweet potatoes and smoked paprika to the skillet and stir to combine. 
    7. Reduce the heat to medium-low, cover the skillet and cook for 25-30 minutes, stirring every 5-7 minutes until the sweet potatoes are softened and lightly browned. 
    8. Remove from heat, season with salt and pepper, and stir in the fresh chopped parsley. 
    9. Serve warm. Enjoy!
