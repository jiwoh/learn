# Ingredients

## Maitake Mushroom and Goat Cheese Crostini Ingredients
    
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1 clove garlic, minced
    - 4 ounces maitake mushrooms, sliced
    - 2 tablespoons dry white wine
    - Salt and freshly ground pepper
    - 4 ounces crumbled goat cheese
    - 1 baguette, sliced into ½-inch rounds
    - 2 tablespoons freshly chopped thyme leaves

# Instructions

## Maitake Mushroom and Goat Cheese Crostini Instructions
    1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Slice a baguette into 12 thin slices.
    3. Place the slices on a baking sheet.
    4. In a bowl, mix 2 tablespoons of olive oil with 1 teaspoon of fresh thyme, 1 teaspoon of garlic powder, 1 teaspoon of salt, and 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper.
    5. Brush the olive oil mixture over both sides of the baguette slices.
    6. Bake in preheated oven for 8-10 minutes until lightly golden brown.
    7. Meanwhile, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    8. Add 1/2 cup of thinly-sliced maitake mushrooms, 1/4 cup of finely-chopped onion, and 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder.
    9. Saute the mixture for 4-5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    10. Remove the skillet from heat.
    11. In a small bowl, mix 2 tablespoons of cream cheese, 2 tablespoons of goat cheese, and 1/4 teaspoon of salt.
    12. Spread the cheese mixture over the crostini slices.
    13. Top each slice with a dollop of the maitake mushroom mixture.
    14. Sprinkle with freshly-chopped parsley and black pepper, if desired.
    15. Serve the crostini warm or at room temperature. Enjoy!
