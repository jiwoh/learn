# Instructions

## Mushroom and Cauliflower Curry 
    
    ### Ingredients
    - 1 tablespoon olive oil
    - 1 large onion, chopped
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 teaspoon freshly grated ginger
    - 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    - 1 teaspoon ground coriander
    - 1 teaspoon garam masala
    - 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    - 1 (14-ounce) can diced tomatoes
    - 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets
    - 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    - 1/2 cup water
    - 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
    
    ### Instructions
    1. Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until the onion is soft, about 5 minutes.
    
    2. Add the ginger, cumin, coriander, and garam masala and cook for 1 minute. Add the tomato paste, diced tomatoes, cauliflower, mushrooms, salt, pepper, and water and bring to a boil.
    
    3. Reduce heat to low and simmer, covered, for 15 minutes, or until the cauliflower is tender. Stir in the cilantro.
    
    4. Serve hot over rice or with naan bread. Enjoy!
