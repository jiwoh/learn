# Black Trumpet Mushroom and Shrimp Fried Rice
    
    This delicious dish is a modern take on traditional fried rice, melding the flavors of earthy black trumpet mushrooms and juicy shrimp together with delicious seasonings.     ## Origin and History
    
    Black trumpet mushrooms, also known as horn of plenty, are a wild mushroom found in many parts of the world. These mushrooms are often used in upscale restaurants as a flavorful and sophisticated ingredient, and are also frequently incorporated into recipes for home-cooking. In this fried rice recipe, the earthy flavors of the black trumpet mushrooms combine wonderfully with the juiciness of the shrimp, creating a delicious and unique meal.     The Prep Time for Black Trumpet Mushroom and Shrimp Fried Rice is 10 minutes, the Cooking Time is 30 minutes, the Skill Level is Advanced, and the Spice Level is Mild. You will need a cutting board, knife, wok, spatula, and bowl for preparing Black Trumpet Mushroom and Shrimp Fried Rice.

# Ingredients

## Ingredients
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 1/2 cup diced onion
    - 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
    - 1 (8-ounce) package black trumpet mushrooms
    - 1/2 pound shrimp, peeled, deveined, and diced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 cups cooked jasmine rice
    - 1 tablespoon soy sauce
    - 2 teaspoons sesame oil
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    - Chopped emerald onion, for garnish (optional)
