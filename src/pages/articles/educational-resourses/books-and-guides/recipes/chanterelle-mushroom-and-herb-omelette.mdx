# Instructions

# Chanterelle Mushroom and Herb Omelette 
    ## Ingredients
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - ¼ cup shallots, diced
    - 3 cups chanterelle mushrooms, cleaned, sliced
    - 2 eggs
    - 2 tablespoons cream
    - 2 tablespoons fresh herbs (such as dill, chives, and/or tarragon)
    - Pinch of salt and pepper
    - Freshly grated Parmesan cheese, for serving
    
    ## Instructions
    1. Heat butter in a medium-sized skillet over medium heat. 
    2. Add shallots and mushrooms and cook until golden brown, about 5 minutes.
    3. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs, cream, herbs, salt, and pepper. 
    4. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet with the mushrooms and shallots and reduce the heat to low.
    5. Cook for about 5 minutes, or until the eggs are nearly set. 
    6. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
    7. Fold the omelette in half. 
    8. Serve warm. Enjoy!
