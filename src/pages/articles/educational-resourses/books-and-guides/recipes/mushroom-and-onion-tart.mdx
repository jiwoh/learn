# Instructions

# Mushroom and Onion Tart Recipe
    
    ## Ingredients
    - puff pastry sheet
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
    - 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
    - 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
    - 2 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan
    - Salt and pepper
    
    ## Directions
    1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
    
    2. Place the puff pastry sheet on a baking sheet.
    
    3. In a medium skillet, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the onion and mushrooms, stirring often, until softened, about 8 minutes.
    
    4. Add the thyme, Parmesan, salt and pepper.
    
    5. Spread the mushroom-onion mixture over the puff pastry sheet and bake for 15 minutes.
    
    6. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!
