# Mushroom and Herb Stuffing
    
    Mushroom and herb stuffing is a delicious and popular side dish that is enjoyed around the world. It originated in European cooking but is common in many cultures. The dish is made by combining mushrooms and herbs with a range of ingredients, such as breadcrumbs, onions, celery and spices, to create a savory, flavorful filling. It's generally used to stuff poultry or other dishes, although it can also be served as a side dish on its own.

# Recipe Overview

    - Prep Time for Mushroom and herb stuffing: 30 mins
    - Cooking Time for Mushroom and herb stuffing: 30-35 mins
    - Skill Level for Mushroom and herb stuffing: Intermediate
    - Spice Level Scoville rating for Mushroom and herb stuffing: Low
    
    A large bowl, a *sharp* knife, a skillet, a cutting board, and a spoon would be needed to prepare Mushroom and herb stuffing.

# Ingredients

## Mushroom and Herb Stuffing Ingredients
    
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - 1 onion, finely chopped
    - 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
    - 6 ounces cremini mushrooms, sliced
    - 6 ounces button mushrooms, sliced
    - 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
    - 2 teaspoons fresh thyme leaves
    - 2 teaspoons fresh sage leaves, chopped
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    - 4 cups dry white bread cubes
    - 1/2 cup chicken broth

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Herb Stuffing Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
    
    2. Heat 2 tablespoons of butter in a skillet over medium heat.
    
    3. Add 1 cup of onion and sauté for about 4 minutes.
    
    4. Add 1/2 cup diced celery and 1/4 cup of diced carrot and sauté for about 4 minutes.
    
    5. Add 2 cloves of garlic, 1/2 teaspoon of dried thyme, 1/2 teaspoon of dried rosemary and 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano. Stir and cook for another minute.
    
    6. Remove from heat and set aside.
    
    7. Place 2 cups of fresh mushrooms in a food processor and pulse until finely chopped.
    
    8. Heat 2 tablespoons of butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add the mushrooms and sauté until they are lightly browned.
    
    9. Add the sautéed vegetables, 1 cup of cooked white rice, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 1/4 teaspoon of pepper. Stir and cook for about 5 minutes.
    
    10. Place the stuffing in a greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
    
    11. Bake for 30 minutes, or until golden brown on top.
    
    12. Serve and enjoy!
