# Instructions

# Reishi Mushroom and Spinach Salad 
    
    ## Ingredients 
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil 
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced 
    - 2 ounces dried reishi mushrooms, sliced 
    - 1/4 teaspoon sea salt 
    - 7 ounces baby spinach 
    - 3 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice 
    
    ## Instructions
    1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. 
    2. Add the garlic and reishi mushrooms and cook for 2 minutes, stirring often. 
    3. Add the sea salt and continue cooking for another 2 minutes. 
    4. Add the spinach to the skillet and cook for an additional 2 minutes until the spinach is wilted. 
    5. Drizzle the lemon juice over the ingredients in the skillet. 
    6. Transfer the mushroom and spinach mixture to a large bowl and serve.
