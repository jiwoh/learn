This dish combines succulent salmon fillets with a savory mushroom and spinach stuffing, making it perfect for any seafood lover. # Recipe Overview

The Prep Time for Mushroom and spinach stuffed salmon is 20 minutes, the Cooking Time is 25 minutes, the Skill Level required is medium, and the Spice Level is 0 Scoville rating. To prepare Mushroom and spinach stuffed salmon, you will need a baking dish, sharp knife, cutting board, and wooden spoon.

# Ingredients

- 4 salmon fillets
    - 2tbsp olive oil
    - Salt & pepper to taste
    - 2 cloves garlic (minced)
    - 8oz button mushrooms (sliced)
    - 10oz baby spinach leaves
    - 2oz feta cheese (crumbled)
    - 2tsp dried oregano
    - 2tsp dried basil
    - 2tbsp butter

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Salmon Instructions
    
    1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
    2. Coat a baking sheet with cooking spray.
    3. Rinse the salmon fillets and pat dry. Place them on the baking sheet.
    4. In a large skillet, heat the butter over medium heat.
    5. Add the mushrooms and garlic to the skillet and cook until the mushrooms have softened, about 5 minutes.
    6. Add the spinach and cook until it is wilted, about 2 minutes.
    7. Transfer the cooked vegetables to a bowl and season with salt and pepper to taste.
    8. Add the breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese to the vegetables and stir to combine.
    9. Divide the vegetable-breadcrumb mixture evenly between the salmon fillets and spread it over the top, pressing lightly.
    10. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, until the salmon is cooked through and the topping is golden.
    11. Serve hot. Enjoy!
