# Recipe Overview

The Prep Time for Chanterelle Mushroom and Bacon Frittata is 10 minutes, the Cooking Time is 25 minutes, the Skill Level is Moderate, and the Spice Level Scoville rating is Mild. To prepare the Chanterelle Mushroom and Bacon Frittata, you'll need a bowl, skillet, spatula, and a baking dish.

# Ingredients

- 6 slices bacon 
    - 2 Tablespoons olive oil
    - 1/2 cup diced red onion
    - 2 cloves minced garlic
    - 1 teaspoon dried thyme
    - 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
    - 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
    - 1 pound chanterelle mushrooms, cleaned and sliced
    - 8 large eggs
    - 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    - 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley 
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives

# Instructions

## Chanterelle Mushroom and Bacon Frittata Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
    
    2. Grease an 8-inch skillet and set aside.
    
    3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    
    4. Add 4 strips of thick-cut bacon and cook until lightly browned and crisp, about 10 minutes. Remove bacon onto a plate lined with paper towels to drain.
    
    5. Crumble or chop the bacon into small pieces.
    
    6. In the same skillet, reduce heat to medium and add 1 tablespoon of butter.
    
    7. When the butter has melted, add 1 cup of chopped chanterelle mushrooms and 1 teaspoon of minced garlic.
    
    8. Saute the mushrooms and garlic until the mushrooms are soft and lightly browned, about 8 minutes.
    
    9. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together 8 eggs and 1/2 teaspoon of sea salt until the eggs are well blended.
    
    10. Add the cooked mushrooms, chopped bacon, 1/4 cup of freshly grated Parmesan cheese, and 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley to the eggs and mix until just combined.
    
    11. Pour the egg mixture into the prepared skillet.
    
    12. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes.
    
    13. Remove the frittata from oven and let cool for 5 minutes.
    
    14. Cut into wedges and serve. Enjoy!
