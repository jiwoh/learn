# Mushroom and Herb Stuffed Turkey Breast
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom and herb stuffed turkey breast originated in the United States, and quickly gained popularity as a festive dish. Traditionally served as a main course for Thanksgiving meals, this luxurious dish is a flavorful combination of tender turkey meat filled with fragrant herbs and savory mushrooms.

# Recipe Overview

    **Prep Time:** Approximately 20 minutes
    
    **Cooking Time:** Approximately 40-50 minutes
    
    **Skill Level:** Beginner to Intermediate
    
    **Spice Level Scoville Rating:** Nil
    
    For preparing Mushroom and herb stuffed turkey breast you will need a chef's knife, wooden spoon, roasting pan and parchment paper.

# Ingredients

- 5-6 pound boneless turkey breast
    - 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
    - 1/2 pound white mushrooms, diced
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh rosemary
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh sage
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh thyme
    - 2 tablespoons chopped garlic
    - Salt and freshly ground pepper
    - 2 cups low-sodium chicken broth

## Mushroom and Herb Stuffed Turkey Breast Instructions

    2. Place turkey breast in a roasting pan.
    3. In a bowl, combine mushrooms, herbs, and butter.
    4. Spread the mushroom mixture evenly over the turkey breast.
    5. Sprinkle salt and pepper over the turkey.
    6. Pour chicken stock over the turkey.
    7. Cover the roasting pan with aluminum foil.
    8. Roast turkey for 1 to 1 ½ hours, or until a thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the breast registers 165°F.
    9. Carefully remove the aluminum foil, and continue roasting for 15-20 minutes to achieve the desired golden color.
    10. Allow the turkey to rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing.
    11. Enjoy!
