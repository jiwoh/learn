# Portobello Mushroom and Feta Salad 
    
    ## Origin and History 
    
    Portobello Mushroom and Feta Salad is a traditional Mediterranean dish, which combines the slight bitterness of Portobello mushrooms with the tangy flavor of Feta cheese. This salad is traditionally served as a side dish to main courses, but can also be eaten as a light lunch or even as a snack. It is believed to have originated in Greece and is a popular favorite amongst its residents.

# Recipe Overview

The Prep Time for Portobello Mushroom and Feta Salad is 15 minutes, the Cooking Time is 15 minutes, the Skill Level for making the salad is Easy, and the Spice Level of the salad is None (0 Scoville). You will need a cutting board, a sharp knife, a large bowl, and a salad spinner to prepare the Portobello Mushroom and Feta Salad.

# Ingredients

### Portobello Mushroom and Feta Salad Ingredients
    
    - 2 large Portobello mushrooms, stems and gills removed
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 teaspoons fresh oregano, chopped
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    - 4 ounces feta cheese, crumbled
    - 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
    - 1/2 cup mixed emeralds
    - 1/4 cup red onion, diced
    - 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
    - 1 tablespoon toasted pine nuts

## Portobello Mushroom and Feta Salad
    
    ### Ingredients: 
    * 4 Portobello mushrooms, stems removed, sliced
    * 2 tablespoons olive oil
    * 2 cloves garlic, minced
    * 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
    * 1 teaspoon fresh oregano, chopped
    * Salt and pepper, to taste
    * 4 ounces feta cheese, cubed
    * 6 cups mixed emeralds
    
    ### Instructions:
    1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
    2. Place mushroom slices on a rimmed baking sheet.
    3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, garlic, vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper. 
    4. Drizzle mushrooms with oil mixture and toss to coat.
    5. Roast mushrooms for 20 minutes in preheated oven, stirring once.
    6. Place mixed emeralds in a large bowl.
    7. Add feta cheese and roasted mushrooms.
    8. Toss to combine.
    9. Serve immediately.
