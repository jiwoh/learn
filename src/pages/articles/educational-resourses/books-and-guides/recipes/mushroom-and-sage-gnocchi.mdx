# Mushroom and Sage Gnocchi
    
    ## Origin and History 
    Mushroom and sage gnocchi is an Italian dish that has been around since early 19th century. This creamy and comforting dish is made with a potato and flour dumpling dough that is combined with sautéed mushrooms, fresh sage and Parmesan cheese. It’s a simple yet delicious dish that has become a classic comfort food in Italian cuisine.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Mushroom and sage gnocchi is approximately 15 minutes, the Cooking Time is approximately 25 minutes, the Skill Level is Moderate and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0. You will need a cutting board, knife, pot, colander, wooden spoon, and slotted spoon for preparing Mushroom and sage gnocchi.

# Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1 small onion, finely chopped
    - 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
    - 200g chestnut mushrooms, sliced
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh sage
    - 250g ready-made gnocchi 
    - 150ml single cream
    - 2 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese, plus extra to serve
    - Salt and freshly-ground black pepper

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Sage Gnocchi Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
    
    2. In a large bowl, mix 2 tablespoons of butter with 1 tablespoon of sage and 1/4 teaspoon of ground pepper.
    
    3. Spread the mixture in an even layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
    
    4. Arrange 2 pounds of quartered mushrooms of your choice, cut side up, in the butter mixture.
    
    5. Roast the mushrooms in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until tender.
    
    6. Meanwhile, bring a large saucepan of salted water to a boil.
    
    7. Add 1 package of gnocchi to the boiling water and cook in accordance to the package instructions.
    
    8. Drain the cooked gnocchi and set aside.
    
    9. In a large skillet, melt 3 tablespoons of butter over medium heat.
    
    10. Add the cooked mushrooms to the butter and stir to combine.
    
    11. Add the cooked gnocchi to the skillet and gently fold it in.
    
    12. Serve the mushroom and sage gnocchi hot. Enjoy!
