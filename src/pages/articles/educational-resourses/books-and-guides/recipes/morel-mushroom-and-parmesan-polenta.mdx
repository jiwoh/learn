# Instructions

## Morel Mushroom and Parmesan Polenta 
    
    ### Ingredients 
    
    - 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
    - 2 cups water
    - 1 cup polenta
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 1 tablespoon butter
    - 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
    - 1/2 cup finely chopped shallots
    - 2 cups fresh morel mushrooms, stems trimmed and caps sliced
    - 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
    - 2 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese
    
    ### Instructions 
    
    1. In a medium saucepan, bring the chicken broth and water to a boil over high heat.
    
    2. Slowly whisk in the polenta and salt. Reduce the heat to low and cook, stirring occasionally, until the polenta is tender and thick, about 15 minutes. Stir in the butter and set aside.
    
    3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the shallots and morel mushrooms and cook, stirring occasionally, until the mushrooms are tender and golden, about 8 minutes.
    
    4. Stir in the parsley and Parmesan cheese.
    
    5. Serve the polenta topped with the mushroom mixture. Enjoy!
