# Chicken of the Woods Mushroom and Spinach Salad
    
    ## Origin and History
    Chicken of the Woods Mushroom and Spinach Salad is a light and refreshing dish that has a long history in many cultures. Originating in Southeast Asia, it has been enjoyed in Thailand and other regions for centuries. Highly nutritious and low in calories, this salad is popular amongst health-conscious eaters and is a great way to enjoy the many benefits of mushrooms.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Chicken of the Woods Mushroom and Spinach Salad is 20 minutes, the Cooking Time is 40 minutes, the Skill Level required is Intermediate, and the Spice Level is Mild. A large bowl, knife, cutting board, oven-safe pan and measuring spoons are all necessary for preparing Chicken of the Woods Mushroom and Spinach Salad.

# Ingredients

##### Chicken of the Woods Mushroom and Spinach Salad Ingredients
    - 4 cups fresh baby spinach
    - 1 cup cooked and diced chicken of the woods mushrooms  
    - 1/2 cup diced sweet onion
    - 1/3 cup crumbled feta cheese
    - 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
    - 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
    - 1 clove garlic, minced
    - 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

# Instructions

### Chicken of the Woods Mushroom and Spinach Salad Prep
    
    1. Clean the mushrooms: Gently brush off any debris from the mushrooms.
    
    2. Dice the onion: Cut the onion into small pieces.
    
    3. Saute the onion and mushrooms: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the diced onion and cook for 3 minutes, until softened. Add the mushrooms and cook for an additional 7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    
    4. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, sea salt, and pepper.
    
    5. Toss the spinach and mushrooms: In a large bowl, combine the spinach, mushrooms and onions. Drizzle the dressing over the top and toss to coat.
    
    6. Serve: Serve the salad immediately. Enjoy!
