# Mushroom and Blue Cheese Risotto
    
    Originating in Italy, risotto is a popular Northern Italian dish made with arborio rice and cooked in broth, resulting in a creamy, rich, textured dish. This mushroom and blue cheese risotto is no exception, combining earthy mushrooms, nutty blue cheese, and creamy risotto for a hearty and flavorful meal.

# Recipe Overview

    Prep Time for Mushroom and blue cheese risotto: 10 minutes
    Cooking Time for Mushroom and blue cheese risotto: 45 minutes
    Skill Level for Mushroom and blue cheese risotto: Intermediate
    Spice Level Scoville rating for Mushroom and blue cheese risotto: 0
    
    To prepare Mushroom and blue cheese risotto, you will need a large saucepan, wooden spoon, colander, and grater.

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Blue Cheese Risotto Instructions
    
    1. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat.
    
    2. Add 1 chopped onion and cook for 3-4 minutes until it starts to soften.
    
    3. Reduce the heat to medium-low, add 1 cup of Arborio rice and stir for 1-2 minutes until it is coated with the olive oil.
    
    4. Add 1/2 cup of white wine and stir until all the liquid has been absorbed.
    
    5. Add 1/2 cup of hot chicken broth to the pan and stir until it is absorbed.
    
    6. Continue to add the chicken broth in 1/2 cup increments, stirring until it is absorbed, until the rice is cooked through, about 20 minutes.
    
    7. In a separate frying pan, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat.
    
    8. Add 8 ounces of sliced cremini mushrooms and 1 teaspoon of minced garlic, and cook for 5 minutes until the mushrooms have softened.
    
    9. Add the cooked mushrooms to the risotto and stir to combine.
    
    10. Add 1/2 cup of crumbled blue cheese and stir until it is melted.
    
    11. Serve the risotto warm and top with freshly chopped parsley.
