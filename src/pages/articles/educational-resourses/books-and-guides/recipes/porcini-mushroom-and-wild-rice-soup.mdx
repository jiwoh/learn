    Skill Level for Porcini Mushroom and Wild Rice Soup: Intermediate

    To prepare the Porcini Mushroom and Wild Rice Soup, you will need a saucepan, stockpot, colander, chopping board and sharp knife.

# Ingredients

- 6 cups vegetable broth
    - 1/4 cup olive oil
    - 1 onion, diced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1/2 cup wild rice
    - 1/2 ounce dried porcini mushrooms
    - 1 carrot, diced
    - 1 celery rib, diced
    - 2 tablespoons white wine
    - 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    - 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
    - 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
    - Salt, to taste

# Instructions

## Porcini Mushroom and Wild Rice Soup Instructions
    
    1. Heat a large pot over medium heat.
    2. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil and diced onion and sauté for 5 minutes.
    3. Add the garlic, celery, and carrots and sauté for an additional 3 minutes.
    4. Add the porcini mushrooms, and cook for 2 minutes.
    5. Add 4 cups of vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
    6. Reduce heat to low and add the wild rice. Simmer for about 25 minutes until the wild rice is cooked through.
    7. Add the parsley, salt, and pepper, and stir to combine.
    8. Serve warm. Enjoy!
