# Ingredients

- 2 6-inch flour tortillas
    - 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
    - 1/2 cup shredded cheese (Monterey Jack, cheddar, or a blend)
    - 1 tablespoon oil
    - Optional: jalapenos, onion, bell pepper, garlic, cilantro

# Instructions

### Mushroom and cheese quesadilla Instructions
    
    1. Heat a non-stick skillet on medium-high heat.
    2. Place a small flour tortilla in the skillet and cook for 1 minute.
    3. Flip the tortilla over and sprinkle the cheese evenly over the tortilla.
    4. Add the mushrooms to the top of the cheese and cook for 1-2 minutes.
    5. Fold the tortilla in half, pressing down lightly.
    6. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes or until the cheese has melted.
    7. Carefully flip the quesadilla and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
    8. Remove the quesadilla from the pan and cut into wedges.
    9. Serve with salsa, sour cream, or guacamole.
