# Ingredients

## Reishi Mushroom and Broccoli Quiche Ingredients
    
    - 1 pre-made 9-inch pie crust
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1 onion, diced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
    - 1/2 cup grated Reishi mushroom powder
    - 1/2 head of broccoli, cooked and chopped
    - 4 eggs
    - 2 tablespoons milk
    - 1 teaspoon salt
    - 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    - 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese, grated
