# Instructions

## Enoki Mushroom and Mushroom Salad Instructions
    
    1. Wash and dry 1/2 lb of Enoki mushrooms and 1/2 lb of white button mushrooms.
    
    2. Cut the Enoki mushrooms into smaller pieces and set aside.
    
    3. Slice the button mushrooms lengthwise into thin strips.
    
    4. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat.
    
    5. Add the mushrooms to the pan and sauté until they start to turn golden brown, about 5 minutes.
    
    6. Add 1 teaspoon of minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
    
    7. Add 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 1 teaspoon of sesame oil, and 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper.
    
    8. Reduce the heat to low and cook for 1 minute.
    
    9. Remove the mushrooms from the heat and let cool.
    
    10. In a large bowl, combine the mushrooms with 2 cups of mixed emeralds, 1/2 cup of shredded carrot, 1/4 cup of thinly sliced red onion, 2 tablespoons of chopped cilantro, and 1/4 cup of toasted sesame seeds.
    
    11. In a small bowl, whisk together 3 tablespoons of rice vinegar, 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 1 teaspoon of sugar, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and a pinch of black pepper.
    
    12. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
    
    13. Serve the salad and enjoy!
