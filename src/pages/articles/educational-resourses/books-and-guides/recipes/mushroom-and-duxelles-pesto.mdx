# Mushroom and Duxelles Pesto
    
    ## Origin and History
    Mushroom and Duxelles Pesto is a savory Italian dish originating in the early 19th century. It combines the delicious flavors of mushrooms and duxelles (a mixture of mushrooms, shallots, and herbs) to create an exquisite blend of textures and flavors. The pesto is usually served over pasta or warm, crusty bread and is a popular dish around the world.

# Recipe Overview

    Mushroom and Duxelles Pesto has a Prep Time of 10 minutes, a Cooking Time of 15 minutes, a Skill Level of Intermediate, and a Spice Level Scoville rating of 5. You will need a food processor, a kitchen knife, a pan, and a ladle to make Mushroom and Duxelles Pesto.

# Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1/2 cup dried mushrooms
    - 1/2 cup chopped shallots
    - 1/2 cup finely chopped celery
    - 1/2 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
    - 1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley
    - 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    - 2 tablespoons white balsamic vinegar
    - 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 2 tablespoons pine nuts
    - 1/4 cup Duxelles (finely chopped mushrooms and shallots cooked in butter until golden brown)

# Instructions

# Mushroom and Duxelles Pesto Instructions
    
    1. Preheat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    2. Add 1 tablespoon of olive oil to the skillet and heat for about 1 minute.
    3. Add one diced shallot and cook until tender and slightly golden, about 5 - 7 minutes.
    4. Add 1 pound of mushrooms that have been diced into small pieces and cook until tender and all the liquid has been absorbed, about 10 minutes.
    5. Add 1 teaspoon of fresh thyme leaves and season with salt and pepper to taste.
    6. Cook until the mushrooms and shallots are golden and fragrant, about 5 minutes.
    7. Transfer the mixture to a food processor and add 1/4 cup of toasted walnuts, 1/2 cup of fresh basil leaves, 2 cloves of garlic, 1/2 cup of freshly grated Parmesan cheese, and 1/4 cup of olive oil.
    8. Blend until smooth.
    9. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.
    10. Serve with pasta, vegetables, or toast. Enjoy!
