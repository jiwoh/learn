# Instructions

## Matsutake Mushroom and Shrimp Fried Rice
    ### Ingredients: 
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 2 tablespoons sesame oil
    - 4 cups cooked long grain white rice
    - 1 cup diced matsutake mushrooms
    - 1/2 cup diced cooked shrimp
    - 4 whole emerald onion, finely chopped
    - 3 tablespoons soy sauce, or to taste
    
    ### Instructions:
    1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the vegetable oil and sesame oil, and swirl to coat the bottom of the skillet.
    2. Add the cooked rice and spread it evenly in the skillet. Fry until the rice is lightly browned and crispy, about 8 minutes.
    3. Add the mushrooms and shrimp, and stir-fry until they are heated through, about 4 minutes.
    4. Stir in the chopped emerald onion and soy sauce. Fry for an additional 1-2 minutes.
    5. Serve hot. Enjoy!
