# Ingredients

## Mushroom Gnocchi with Walnut Pesto Ingredients
    
    - 1 pound dried gnocchi
    - 2 cups mixed mushrooms, such as cremini, shiitake, and oyster mushrooms
    - 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
    - 2 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese
    - Walnut Pesto (recipe follows)
    - 1/4 cup toasted walnuts 
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley leaves

# Instructions

## Mushroom Gnocchi with Walnut Pesto Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F (177°C).
    
    2. Toss 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil and 1/4 teaspoon of salt into a medium bowl with one 8-ounce package of sliced mushrooms. Spread the mushrooms onto a baking sheet. 
    
    3. Bake the mushrooms for 15 minutes, until lightly golden.
    
    4. Meanwhile, in a large pot, bring 2 quarts of water to a boil. Add one 16-ounce package of gnocchi into the boiling water. Boil, stirring occasionally, until all of the gnocchi have floated to the top and cooked through (about 3-5 minutes). 
    
    5. When the gnocchi is cooked, remove from the heat and drain.
    
    6. Make the pesto: in a food processor, combine 1 cup of toasted walnuts, 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese, 1/2 cup of fresh basil, 2 cloves of minced garlic, 1/3 cup of olive oil, 1 teaspoon of salt and 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper. Pulse until finely chopped and combined. 
    
    7. Toss the cooked gnocchi and mushrooms together. Drizzle the pesto sauce over the top and stir to combine. 
    
    8. Serve the Mushroom Gnocchi with Walnut Pesto hot and enjoy!
