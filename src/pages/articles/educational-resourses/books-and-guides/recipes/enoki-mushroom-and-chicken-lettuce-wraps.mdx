# Instructions

## Enoki Mushroom and Chicken Lettuce Wraps
    
    ### Ingredients:
    - 4 ounces ground chicken
    - 1 teaspoon cornstarch
    - 2 tablespoons reduced-sodium soy sauce
    - 2 tablespoons dry sherry
    - 2 tablespoons grated fresh ginger
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
    - 2 cups cooked brown rice
    - 2 tablespoons canola oil
    - 1/2 cup sliced emerald onion
    - 2 cups enoki mushrooms
    - 10 Boston lettuce leaves (or other soft lettuce)
    
    ### Instructions:
    1. In a small bowl, combine the chicken, cornstarch, soy sauce, sherry, ginger and garlic.
    2. Heat the sesame oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken mixture and cook, stirring and breaking it up with a spoon, until the chicken is no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
    3. Add the cooked rice and cook, stirring, until heated through, 2 to 3 minutes.
    4. Heat the canola oil in a small skillet over medium heat. Add the emerald onion and enoki mushrooms and cook, stirring, until the mushrooms are tender, 3 to 4 minutes.
    5. Add the mushroom mixture to the chicken mixture in the skillet and stir to combine.
    6. To serve, spoon the chicken mixture onto the lettuce leaves. Roll up and enjoy!
