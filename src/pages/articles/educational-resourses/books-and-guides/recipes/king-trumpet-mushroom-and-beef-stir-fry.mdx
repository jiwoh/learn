# Instructions

## King Trumpet Mushroom and Beef Stir-fry
    
    ### Ingredients
    - 4 ounces of king trumpet mushrooms, sliced
    - 1/2 pound of sirloin steak, thinly sliced
    - 2 tablespoons of canola oil
    - 2 cloves of garlic, minced
    - 2 tablespoons of grated ginger
    - 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
    - 1 teaspoon of toasted sesame oil
    - 2 teaspoons of cornstarch
    - 2 tablespoons of cold water
    - 2 tablespoons of chopped scallions
    
    ### Directions 
    1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. 
    2. Add the mushrooms and stir-fry for 2 minutes until the mushrooms are lightly browned.
    3. Remove the mushrooms from the pan and set aside.
    4. Heat the remaining oil in the wok and add the steak.
    5. Stir-fry the steak for 2 minutes until it is almost cooked. 
    6. Add the garlic, ginger, and mushrooms to the beef and stir-fry for 1 minute.
    7. Combine the soy sauce, sesame oil, cornstarch, and cold water in a small bowl. 
    8. Pour the sauce into the pan and stir-fry for 2 minutes until everything is well combined.
    9. Add the scallions and stir-fry for 1 minute.
    10. Serve the stir-fry with steamed rice. Enjoy!
