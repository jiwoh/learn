### King Trumpet Mushroom and Goat Cheese Salad Instructions
    
    1. Start by prepping the ingredients. Clean the king trumpet mushrooms by gently wiping any dirt off with a damp cloth; finely chop the parsley; and cut the goat cheese into small cubes.
    
    2. In a bowl, mix together the olive oil, garlic, red pepper flakes, and parsley.
    
    3. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat. Add the mushrooms and the marinade and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    
    4. To assemble the salad, place the cooked mushrooms in a bowl. Add the baby kale and the cubed goat cheese.
    
    5. Drizzle the balsamic vinaigrette over the salad and toss to combine.
