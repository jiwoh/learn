# Mushroom Ramen Instructions
    1. Fill a large pot with 8 cups of water and bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
    2. Add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil or sesame oil to the boiling water.
    3. Add 2 packages of ramen noodles and cook according to package instructions.
    4. Meanwhile, in a separate large skillet or wok, heat 2 teaspoons of oil over medium-high heat.
    5. Add 1 cup of sliced mushrooms, 2 cloves of minced garlic, and 1 tablespoon of grated ginger. Saute for 3-4 minutes.
    6. Add 1/2 cup of chopped scallions, 1/2 cup of frozen peas, 1/2 cup of sliced carrots, and 1/4 cup of soy sauce. Stir and cook for another 2 minutes.
    7. When the noodles are finished cooking, drain the noodles and set aside.
    8. Add the cooked noodles to the skillet and toss with the vegetables.
    9. Serve the mushroom ramen in individual bowls and top with a sprinkle of sesame seeds and freshly chopped scallions. Enjoy!
