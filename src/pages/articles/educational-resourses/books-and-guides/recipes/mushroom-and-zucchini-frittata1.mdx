# Mushroom and Zucchini Frittata
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom and Zucchini Frittata originated in Italy in the late 19th century as a savory egg-based dish. It can be served hot or at room temperature, as a starter or main course. It is especially suited to using up leftovers and works well with a variety of vegetables, herbs and cheese.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Mushroom and Zucchini Frittata1 is 10 minutes, the Cooking Time is 30 minutes, and the Skill Level is Easy. The Spice Level Scoville rating is Low. To prepare Mushroom and Zucchini Frittata1, you will need a large skillet, a bowl, a colander, and a cutting board.

# Ingredients

## Ingredients
    
    * 4 large eggs
    * 2 tablespoons olive oil
    * 1/2 small onion, diced
    * 2 cloves garlic, minced
    * 2 cups sliced mushrooms
    * 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
    * 1/2 cup diced yellow bell pepper
    * 1/2 cup diced zucchini
    * 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
    * 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
    * 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
    * Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
    * 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Zucchini Frittata1 Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Coat a 9 x 13-inch baking dish with a thin layer of extra virgin olive oil.
    3. In a bowl, whisk together 8 eggs and 1/2 cup of milk until combined. 
    4. Set aside.
    5. Heat 1 tbsp of extra virgin olive oil in large skillet over medium-high heat.
    6. Add 1 small diced onion and 2 minced garlic cloves and cook until softened, about 3 minutes.
    7. Add 1 lb of sliced mushrooms, stir and cook for about 5 minutes until mushrooms are softened and lightly browned.
    8. Add 1 diced zucchini, 1/4 tsp each of salt and black pepper, and cook for another 3 minutes until zucchini is softened.
    9. Pour egg mixture into skillet and stir to combine.
    10. Transfer to preheated baking dish.
    11. Top with 1/2 cup of grated cheese.
    12. Bake for 25 minutes, or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
    13. Allow to cool for 5 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
