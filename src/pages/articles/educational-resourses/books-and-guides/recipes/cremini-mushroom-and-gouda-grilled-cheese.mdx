# Ingredients

- 4 slices of bread
    - 2 Tablespoons of butter
    - 8 ounces of Cremini mushrooms, sliced
    - 2 cloves of garlic, minced
    - 2 Tablespoons of olive oil
    - 2 dashes of Worcestershire sauce
    - Salt and pepper
    - 8 ounces of Gouda cheese, sliced

# Instructions

## Cremini Mushroom and Gouda Grilled Cheese Instructions
    
    1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    2. Add 2 tablespoons of butter to the pan.
    3. Once melted, add 1/2 cup of diced cremini mushrooms to the pan.
    4. Cook mushrooms until they are softened and lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
    5. Remove mushrooms from pan and set aside.
    6. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
    7. Place 2 slices of bread in the pan butter-side-down.
    8. Layer the bottom slice of bread with a single layer of cheese.
    9. Top cheese with the cooked mushrooms, spreading them evenly over the cheese.
    10. Top the mushrooms with another layer of cheese.
    11. Place the remaining slice of bread on top, butter-side-up.
    12. Cover the pan with a lid or a large plate.
    13. Cook the sandwich until the bottom slice of bread is golden brown, approximately 3 minutes.
    14. Carefully flip the sandwich and cook until the second side is golden brown, approximately another 3 minutes.
    15. Cut the sandwich in half and enjoy!
