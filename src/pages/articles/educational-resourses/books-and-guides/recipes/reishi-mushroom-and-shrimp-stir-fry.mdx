# Reishi Mushroom and Shrimp Stir-fry
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Reishi Mushroom and Shrimp Stir-fry is a classic Chinese dish that originated in the Jiangsu region of China and has been popular for centuries. This dish combines two of the most popular ingredients—shrimp and reishi mushrooms—creating a flavorful stir-fry packed with umami. It's perfect for weeknight dinners and is sure to become a favorite in your household!

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Reishi Mushroom and Shrimp Stir-fry is 30 minutes, the Cooking Time is15 minutes, the Skill Level is Beginner, and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0. In order to prepare Reishi Mushroom and Shrimp Stir-fry, a wok, cutting board, knife, and measuring cup are required.

# Ingredients

### Ingredients
    - 4 dried reishi mushrooms
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 1 teaspoon sesame oil
    - 1 pound shrimp, shelled and deveined
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 tablespoons freshly grated ginger
    - 2 scallions, chopped
    - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    - 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
    - 1/4 cup chicken broth
    - 1 teaspoon sugar
    - 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
    - 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

# Instructions

## Reishi Mushroom and Shrimp Stir-fry Instructions
    
    1. In a large bowl, combine 1 pound of peeled and deveined shrimp with 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of sesame oil and 1 tablespoon of cornstarch. Mix until the ingredients are evenly distributed throughout the shrimp.
    
    2. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
    
    3. Add 1 cup of reishi mushrooms to the skillet and cook for 3 minutes.
    
    4. Add the shrimp mixture to the skillet and cook for 4 minutes, stirring constantly.
    
    5. Add 1/4 cup of chopped scallions and cook for 1 minute.
    
    6. Add 1/4 cup of chicken broth to the skillet and cook for 2 minutes.
    
    7. Reduce the heat to medium-low and stir in 2 tablespoons of hoisin sauce.
    
    8. Garnish the stir-fry with additional scallions and serve.
