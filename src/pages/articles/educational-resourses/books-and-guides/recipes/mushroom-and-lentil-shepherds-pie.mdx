# Mushroom and Lentil Shepherds Pie
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom and Lentil Shepherds Pie is a classic vegetarian dish adapted from the traditional British Shepherds Pie. This variation was created in the 1960s as part of a trend of vegetarian cooking that has become increasingly popular ever since. It features a creamy mushroom and lentil filling topped with a layer of mashed potatoes and baked to a golden brown. The dish is hearty and comforting, a great-tasting way to enjoy a meat-free meal.

# Recipe Overview

    *The Prep Time for Mushroom and Lentil Shepherds Pie is 10 minutes, the Cooking Time is 40 minutes, the Skill Level is Easy, and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0. The necessary cooking utensils, pots, pans and etc needed for preparing Mushroom and Lentil Shepherds Pie are a large saucepan, large ovenproof dish, bowl, and a large spoon.

# Ingredients

## Mushroom and Lentil Shepherds Pie Ingredients
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil 
    - 1 red onion, chopped 
    - 1 carrot, peeled and chopped 
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced 
    - 2 cups cremini mushrooms, sliced 
    - 2 tablespoons tomato paste 
    - 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 
    - 2 tablespoons vegan Worcestershire sauce 
    - 1 teaspoon dried thyme 
    - ½ teaspoon smoked paprika 
    - ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg 
    - 1 ½ cup cooked emerald lentils 
    - 2 cups vegetable broth 
    - 1 bay leaf 
    - 3 tablespoons parsley, chopped 
    - 1 teaspoon lemon zest 
    - Salt and pepper, to taste 
    - 2 large potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes 
    - 2 tablespoons unsalted vegan butter 
    - ¼ cup almond milk 
    - ¼ cup vegan parmesan cheese

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Lentil Shepherds Pie Instructions
    
    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F and grease a 9-inch pie dish.
    
    2. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    
    3. Add 1 chopped onion and 1 clove of minced garlic and cook until the onions are soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
    
    4. Add 8 ounces of sliced mushrooms and cook until they are soft and all the liquid has evaporated, about 8 minutes.
    
    5. Add 1/2 cup of dry emerald lentils and 2 cups of vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
    
    6. Reduce heat and simmer until the lentils are tender, about 20 minutes.
    
    7. Add 1/4 cup of frozen peas and cook for another 5 minutes.
    
    8. Stir in 2 tablespoons of tomato paste, 1 teaspoon of dried oregano, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper.
    
    9. Transfer the mixture to the prepared pie dish.
    
    10. In a medium bowl, combine 1 cup of mashed potatoes with 2 tablespoons of butter and 1/4 cup of milk and stir until smooth.
    
    11. Spread the mashed potatoes over the lentil mixture.
    
    12. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until the potatoes are golden brown.
    
    13. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
