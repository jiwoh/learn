# Mushroom and Ricotta Stuffed Mushrooms
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom and ricotta stuffed mushrooms are a classic Italian recipe that has been enjoyed for centuries. The dish originated in the regions of Campania and Lazio, where mushrooms are plentiful. Ricotta cheese is combined with the mushrooms, creating a rich and creamy stuffing, which is then stuffed inside mushrooms and cooked. The result is an irresistible starter that is full of flavor and aroma.

# Recipe Overview

The Prep Time for Mushroom and ricotta stuffed mushrooms is 15 minutes, the Cooking Time is 40 minutes, the Skill Level is Moderate and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0.
    For preparing Mushroom and ricotta stuffed mushrooms you will need a baking spoon, a baking dish, a paring knife, and a bowl.

# Ingredients

## Ingredients
    
    - 12 large mushrooms
    - 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
    - Freshly ground black pepper
    - 1/2 cup part-skim ricotta cheese
    - 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
    - 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh basil
    - 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Ricotta Stuffed Mushrooms Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    
    2. Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish with olive oil.
    
    3. Clean the mushrooms and remove the stems.
    
    4. In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, garlic, onion, red pepper flakes, oregano, salt and pepper. Mix together until well combined.
    
    5. Stuff each of the mushrooms with the ricotta cheese mixture.
    
    6. Place stuffed mushrooms in the greased baking dish.
    
    7. Drizzle the mushrooms with olive oil.
    
    8. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until mushrooms are tender and golden brown.
    
    9. Serve and enjoy!
