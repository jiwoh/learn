# Instructions

## Chanterelle Mushroom and Beef Stroganoff
    
    ### Ingredients:
    - 1 lb. beef sirloin, thinly sliced
    - 1/2 cup butter
    - 1/2 cup onion, diced
    - 1 cup mushrooms, diced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 teaspoon dried thyme
    - 1 teaspoon sea salt
    - 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    - 2 cups beef broth
    - 1 cup sour cream
    - 2 cups chanterelle mushrooms, sliced
    - 4 cups egg noodles, cooked
    
    ### Instructions:
    1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, melt butter.
    2. Add the onion, mushrooms, garlic, thyme, and sea salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.
    3. Add the beef and cook for an additional 5 minutes, until the beef is browned.
    4. Reduce the heat to low and add the flour. Stir to combine.
    5. Slowly add the beef broth, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
    6. Bring the mixture to a simmer and allow to cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    7. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the sour cream.
    8. Stir in the chanterelle mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes.
    9. Serve the stroganoff over cooked egg noodles. Enjoy!
