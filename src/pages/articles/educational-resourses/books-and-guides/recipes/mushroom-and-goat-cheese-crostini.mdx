# Mushroom and Goat Cheese Crostini
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom and goat cheese crostini is a delicious appetizer or snack that originated in Italy. The classic version of this dish is simple, yet flavorful, and typically consists of mushrooms, garlic, herbs, and goat cheese topped with a slice of toasted baguette. This type of crostini has become very popular in other countries, and the ingredients and variations have changed over the years to create a variety of tasty interpretations.

# Recipe Overview

    **Prep Time**: 15 minutes  
    **Cooking Time**: 15 minutes  
    **Skill Level**: Easy  
    **Spice Level**: None  
    
    You will need a baking sheet, slice of baguette, slicing knife, a sauté pan, and measuring spoons to prepare the Mushroom and Goat Cheese Crostini.

# Ingredients

**Ingredients** 
    - 1/4 cup olive oil 
    - 2 cups finely chopped mushrooms 
    - 2 cloves of garlic, minced 
    - 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes 
    - 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano 
    - Salt and freshly ground pepper 
    - 1/2 loaf of baguette, sliced 
    - 4 ounces goat cheese, crumbled 
    - 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh parsley

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Goat Cheese Crostini Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. Arrange a single layer of baguette slices on a parchment lined baking sheet.
    3. Drizzle each slice of bread with olive oil.
    4. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10 minutes or until lightly toasted.
    5. In a medium skillet over medium high heat, add 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
    6. Add the mushrooms and garlic and sauté until softened and lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
    7. Add the parsley and season with salt and pepper.
    8. Remove from heat and set aside.
    9. In a small bowl, mix together the goat cheese, dill, garlic powder and salt and pepper.
    10. Spread the goat cheese mixture over the toasted bread slices.
    11. Top with the mushrooms and sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
    12. Bake at 350°F ( 175°C) for 8-10 minutes or until the cheese is melted and lightly browned.
    13. Serve warm. Enjoy!
