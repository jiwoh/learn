# Mushroom and Thyme Pizza
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom and Thyme Pizza is a classic Italian dish that dates back to the 1700s. The combination of savory mushrooms, sweet herbs, and tangy cheese creates a delicious dish that is beloved by pizza lovers all over the world. Its popularity has stood the test of time and continues to be a favorite on many restaurant menus.

# Recipe Overview

    Mushroom and Thyme Pizza has a Prep Time of approximately 15 minutes, a Cooking Time of approximately 35 minutes, a Skill Level of Easy, and a Spice Level Scoville rating of 0. To prepare the Mushroom and Thyme Pizza, you will need a pizza pan or stone, a bowl, a cutting board, a knife, a spoon, and measuring cups and spoons.

# Ingredients

## Mushroom and Thyme Pizza Ingredients
    * Pizza dough
    * Flour
    * Olive oil
    * Tomato paste
    * Mozzarella cheese
    * Mushrooms
    * Fresh herbs (e.g. thyme)
    * Salt and pepper

# Instructions

# Mushroom and Thyme Pizza Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
    
    2. Roll out pizza dough to desired size on a lightly floured surface. Transfer to a baking sheet.
    
    3. Spread pizza sauce over pizza dough.
    
    4. Top with mushrooms, thyme, and a sprinkle of mozzarella cheese.
    
    5. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes or until cheese is melted.
    
    6. Enjoy!
