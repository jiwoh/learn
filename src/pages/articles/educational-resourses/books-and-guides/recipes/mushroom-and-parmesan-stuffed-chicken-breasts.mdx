# Ingredients

* 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    * 1/2 cup finely chopped mushrooms
    * 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    * 1/4 cup diced onion

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Parmesan Stuffed Chicken Breasts Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    
    2. Place chicken breasts on a cutting board and season both sides with salt and pepper.
    
    3. Make a pocket in each chicken breast by using a sharp knife to cut a slit along the length of the chicken breast.
    
    4. In a medium bowl, mix together the mushrooms, Parmesan cheese, bread crumbs, garlic, parsley and oregano.
    
    5. Stuff each chicken breast with the mushroom mixture and place seam side down on a baking sheet.
    
    6. Bake for 30 minutes or until the internal temperature of the chicken breasts reaches 165°F (75°C).
    
    7. Let the chicken breasts rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
