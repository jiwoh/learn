# Ingredients

## Reishi Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto Ingredients
    - 1 tablespoon olive oil
    - 1/2 onion, diced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 cups arborio rice
    - 1/4 cup dry white wine
    - 4 cups vegetable broth
    - 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    - 1/4 cup chopped reishi mushrooms
    - salt and pepper, to taste
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

# Instructions

## Reishi Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto Instructions
    1. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
    2. Once the oil is hot, add the finely chopped shallots and cook for 2 minutes.
    3. Add the chopped mushrooms and cook for an additional 3 minutes, stirring regularly.
    4. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
    5. Add the Arborio rice and stir for 1 minute.
    6. Pour in the white wine and stir continuously until the liquid is absorbed.
    7. Begin adding the hot vegetable broth/stock one ladle at a time, stirring continuously until each ladle is absorbed.
    8. Once the rice is cooked (this should take around 15 minutes), stir in the parmesan cheese, crushed reishi mushroom powder, and butter.
    9. Taste the risotto and season with salt and pepper, if desired.
    10. Serve and enjoy.
