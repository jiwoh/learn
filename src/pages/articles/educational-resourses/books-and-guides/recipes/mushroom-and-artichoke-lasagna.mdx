# Ingredients

- 9 lasagna noodles
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 8 ounces sliced mushrooms
    - 1 (14-ounce) can quartered artichoke hearts, drained
    - 1 (14-ounce) can diced tomatoes
    - 2 teaspoons dried oregano
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
    - 2 cups ricotta cheese
    - 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    - 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
    - 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
    - 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Artichoke Lasagna 
    
    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Grease a 9-inch (23 cm) deep-dish baking dish with olive oil.
    3. In a large pot over medium-high heat, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
    4. Add 1 chopped onion and 1 minced garlic clove and sauté until the onion is softened and translucent, about 5 minutes.
    5. Add 2 (8-ounce/240-ml) packages of sliced mushrooms and sauté until softened, about 10 minutes.
    6. Add 2 (14-ounce/400-ml) cans of artichoke hearts, drained and chopped.
    7. Stir in 1 (14-ounce/400-ml) can of diced tomatoes and 1/4 teaspoon of dried oregano.
    8. Bring the mixture to a boil and then reduce the heat and let the mixture simmer for 10 minutes.
    9. In a separate medium bowl, mix together 1 (15-ounce/430-ml) container of ricotta cheese, 1 egg, 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano, and 1/2 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese.
    10. Spread 1/2 cup of the tomato sauce in the bottom of the greased baking dish.
    11. Layer with three cooked lasagna noodles and top with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture, 1/2 of the mushroom-artichoke mixture, and 1/4 cup of mozzarella cheese.
    12. Repeat with three additional noodles, the remaining ricotta cheese mixture, the remaining mushroom-artichoke mixture, and 1/4 cup of mozzarella cheese.
    13. Top with the remaining three lasagna noodles and 1/2 cup of tomato sauce.
    14. Sprinkle with the remaining 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese.
    15. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbling. 
    16. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
