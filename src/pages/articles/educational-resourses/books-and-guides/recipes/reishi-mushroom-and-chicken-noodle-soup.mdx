# Reishi Mushroom and Chicken Noodle Soup
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Reishi Mushroom and Chicken Noodle Soup is a modern take on a traditional Asian comfort food. It combines the earthy flavors of Reishi mushrooms, with the hearty and salty flavors of chicken, combined with a rich and savory broth made from chicken stock, shiitake mushrooms, and herbs and spices. This hearty soup has become particularly popular for its immune-boosting health benefits thanks to the medicinal properties of the Reishi mushrooms which are known for their anti-inflammatory and antiviral properties.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Reishi Mushroom and Chicken Noodle Soup is 15 minutes, the Cooking Time is 40 minutes, the Skill Level is Easy and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0-10. To make Reishi Mushroom and Chicken Noodle Soup, you will need a cutting board, knife, large pot, wooden spoon, and measuring cup.

# Ingredients

* 2 tablespoons olive oil
    * 2 cloves garlic, minced
    * 1 small onion, diced
    * 2 stalks celery, diced
    * 2 carrots, diced
    * 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
    * 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
    * 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
    * 4 cups chicken broth
    * 2 tablespoons reishi mushroom powder
    * 2 cups cooked shredded chicken
    * 1 cup egg noodles
    * 1/2 cup frozen peas
    * Salt and pepper, to taste
    * Chopped parsley, for garnish (optional)

# Instructions

## Reishi Mushroom and Chicken Noodle Soup Instructions
    
    1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
    
    2. Add 1 diced onion and sauté for 3-4 minutes, or until the onion is softened and slightly browned.
    
    3. Add 2 cloves of minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute. 
    
    4. Add 1 pound of diced chicken breast and cook until the chicken is no longer pink.
    
    5. Add 1 teaspoon of ground ginger, 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon and 1 tablespoon of dried thyme to the pot. Stir to combine.
    
    6. Add 8 cups of chicken broth and bring to a boil.
    
    7. Add 1 cup of uncooked egg noodles and reduce the heat to low. Simmer for 10-12 minutes, or until the noodles are cooked.
    
    8. Add 1 cup of sliced reishi mushrooms, 1/2 cup of chopped celery, 1/2 cup of sliced carrots and 1/4 cup of diced onion to the pot. 
    
    9. Simmer for 5-8 minutes.
    
    10. Add salt and pepper to taste.
    
    11. Serve the soup hot. Enjoy!
