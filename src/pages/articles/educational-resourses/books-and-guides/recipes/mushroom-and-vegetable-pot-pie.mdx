# Instructions

## Mushroom and Vegetable Pot Pie
    
    ### Ingredients
    - 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
    - 1 onion, finely chopped
    - 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
    - 2 cups shredded mushrooms
    - 1/2 cup sliced carrots
    - 1/2 cup frozen peas
    - 1/2 cup frozen corn
    - 1/2 cup chicken broth
    - 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    - Salt and pepper, to taste
    - 1 package (14.1 ounces) refrigerated piecrusts
    - 1 egg, whisked
    
    ### Directions
    1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
    2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    3. Add onions and thyme and cook, stirring, for 4 minutes or until onion is softened.
    4. Add mushrooms and cook for 4 minutes or until mushrooms are softened and most of the liquid has evaporated.
    5. Add carrots, peas, and corn and cook for 2 minutes.
    6. Mix chicken broth and flour in a small bowl and whisk until smooth.
    7. Add to skillet and cook, stirring, for 2 minutes.
    8. Remove from heat and season with salt and pepper.
    9. Spoon filling into a 9-inch deep-dish pie plate.
    10. Unroll 1 piecrust and place over the filling.
    11. Trim any overlapping crust and press on the edges of the pie plate to seal.
    12. Brush top of crust with whisked egg.
    13. Unroll remaining piecrust, place over top of pie and press together edges of the two crusts to seal.
    14. Cut several small vents in top crust and brush with egg.
    15. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden-brown.
    16. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
