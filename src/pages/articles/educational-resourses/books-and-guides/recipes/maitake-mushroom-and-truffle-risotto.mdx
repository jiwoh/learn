# Maitake Mushroom and Truffle Risotto
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Maitake Mushroom and Truffle Risotto is a classic Italian dish hailing from northern Italy. It's a popular rice-based dish that dates back centuries. It combines the earthy flavors of maitake mushrooms and truffles with creamy risotto and savory Parmesan cheese. It's an elegant dish that's sure to impress any dinner guests.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Maitake Mushroom and Truffle Risotto is 10 minutes, the Cooking Time is 45 minutes and the Skill Level is moderate. The Spice Level Scoville rating for Maitake Mushroom and Truffle Risotto is mild. To prepare Maitake Mushroom and Truffle Risotto, you will need a large pot, a wooden spoon, a ladle and a strainer.

# Ingredients

1. 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
    2. 1/2 cup finely chopped shallot
    3. 2 garlic cloves, minced
    4. 2 tablespoons white truffle oil
    5. 1 cup uncooked arborio rice
    6. 1/2 cup dry white wine
    7. 2 cups chicken broth
    8. 1 cup finely chopped maitake mushrooms
    9. 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    9. 1/4 cup freshly chopped parsley
    10. Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

# Instructions
