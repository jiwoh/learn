## Cream of Wild Mushroom Soup Recipe
    
    ### Ingredients
    - 1 tablespoon olive oil
    - 1 onion, diced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
    - 4 tablespoons butter
    - 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    - 4 cups chicken broth
    - 1/2 cup heavy cream
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    
    ### Instructions 
    1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat.
    2. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until the onion is soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
    3. Add the mushrooms and sauté until softened and lightly browned, about 5 minutes more.
    4. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the butter until melted.
    5. Add the flour and stir for about 1 minute.
    6. Slowly pour in the chicken broth, stirring constantly to prevent any lumps from forming.
    7. Increase the heat to medium and bring the soup to a boil; reduce the heat and let the soup simmer for 10 minutes.
    8. Stir in the cream and season with salt and pepper.
    9. Serve the soup hot. Enjoy!
