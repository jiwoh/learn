# Instructions

## Matsutake Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry
     
    ### Ingredients 
    - 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
    - 1 onion, diced
    - 2 cloves of garlic, minced 
    - 2 cups of matsutake mushrooms, sliced
    - 1 carrot, sliced
    - 1 zucchini, sliced
    - 1 cup of snow peas
    - 1/4 teaspoon of ground ginger
    - 2 teaspoons of soy sauce
    - Salt and pepper, to taste
    
    ### Instructions
    1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    2. Add onion and garlic to the skillet, and cook until softened, about 3 minutes.
    3. Add the mushrooms, carrot, zucchini and snow peas to the skillet.
    4. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    5. Add the ginger, soy sauce, salt and pepper, and stir to combine.
    6. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.
    7. Serve hot. Enjoy!
