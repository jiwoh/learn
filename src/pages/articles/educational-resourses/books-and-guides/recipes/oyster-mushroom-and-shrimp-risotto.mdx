## Origin and History of Oyster Mushroom and Shrimp Risotto
    
    Oyster mushroom and shrimp risotto is a classic Italian dish that dates back to the 17th century. This dish is traditionally made with arborio rice, mushrooms, shrimp, and Parmesan cheese, and it is cooked in a flavorful broth or wine. It is a popular dish in Northern Italy and is often served as an appetizer or as a main course. The earthy flavors of the mushrooms and the sweetness of the shrimp combine to create a unique and delicious dish that has been enjoyed for centuries.

# Recipe Overview

The Prep Time for Oyster Mushroom and Shrimp Risotto is approximately 10 minutes, the Cooking Time is 25 minutes and the Skill Level is Moderate. The Spice Level Scoville rating for Oyster Mushroom and Shrimp Risotto is Minimal. You will need a saucepan, a wooden spoon, a cutting board, and a knife to make this delicious dish.

# Ingredients

### Ingredients
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - 1/4 cup diced onion
    - 1 cup arborio rice
    - 2 cups vegetable broth
    - 1/4 cup white wine
    - 1/2 pound oyster mushrooms, sliced
    - 1/4 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
    - 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese 
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
    - Salt and pepper, to taste

# Instructions

## Oyster Mushroom and Shrimp Risotto
    1. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    2. Add 2 cloves of garlic, minced, and 1/2 cup of chopped onion, and sauté until the vegetables are softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.
    3. Increase heat to medium-high, and add 8 - 10 ounces of oyster mushrooms, diced. Sauté for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    4. Add 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper and 1 teaspoon of salt. Sauté for another 2 minutes.
    5. Add 1 cup of arborio rice and stir until the grains are coated with olive oil.
    6. Add 3 cups of chicken broth, reduce heat to low, and simmer, stirring occasionally, until most of the liquid is absorbed, about 15 minutes.
    7. Add 1/3 cup of white wine, and simmer until the rice absorbs the liquid, about 5 minutes.
    8. Add 1/2 pound of peeled, deveined shrimp and simmer until the shrimp are cooked through, about 3 - 5 minutes.
    9. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve.
