# Instructions

## Shiitake Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry Instructions
    
    1. Gather the ingredients: 
        * 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
        * 2 cloves garlic, minced
        * 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
        * 1/2 cup red bell pepper, thinly sliced
        * 1/2 cup carrots, trimmed, peeled, and thinly sliced
        * 1/2 cup emerald onions, thinly sliced 
        * 1 cup shiitake mushrooms, halved
        * 1/4 cup light soy sauce 
        * 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
        * 1 tablespoon cornstarch
        * 1 tablespoon sesame oil
        * 2 cups cooked white rice
    
    2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes, and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
    
    3. Add red bell pepper and carrots, and cook until just beginning to soften, about 3 minutes.
    
    4. Add emerald onions, mushrooms, soy sauce, and oyster sauce; stir to combine. Cook until vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes.
    
    5. In a small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and sesame oil; then add to the pan and stir to combine.
    
    6. Serve stir-fry over cooked white rice. Enjoy!
