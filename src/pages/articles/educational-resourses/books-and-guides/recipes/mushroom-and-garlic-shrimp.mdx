# Mushroom and Garlic Shrimp
    
    Mushroom and garlic shrimp is a classic Chinese dish that combines the savory flavors of garlic and mushrooms with succulent shrimp. It is a popular dish in both northern and southern China, and is easy to make and incredibly delicious. The dish has been adapted and enjoyed around the world and is a staple in Chinese cuisine.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Mushroom and garlic shrimp is 10 minutes, the Cooking Time is 15 minutes and the Skill Level involved is easy. The Spice Level Scoville rating is medium-hot.
    
    For preparing Mushroom and garlic shrimp, you will need a skillet, garlic press, garlic, butter, mushrooms, shrimp, white wine, paprika, cayenne pepper, and fresh parsley.

# Ingredients

## Mushroom and garlic shrimp Ingredients
    - 1/2 lb. jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    - 2 tablespoons white wine or vegetable broth
    - 2 tablespoons freshly chopped parsley, for garnish

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Garlic Shrimp Recipe
    
    1. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    
    2. When the oil is hot, add 1 pound of peeled and deveined shrimp to the skillet.
    
    3. Saute the shrimp for 2 minutes until they are fully cooked.
    
    4. Using a slotted spoon, remove the shrimp from the skillet and transfer to a plate.
    
    5. Add 1 tablespoon of butter to the skillet and reduce the heat to medium.
    
    6. Add 1/2 pound of sliced mushrooms and 1/2 cup of minced garlic to the skillet and saut√© for 3-4 minutes until the mushrooms are soft.
    
    7. Return the shrimp to the skillet and season with salt and pepper to taste.
    
    8. Add 1/4 cup of dry white wine to the skillet and simmer for 2-3 minutes until the wine has reduced.
    
    9. Turn off the heat and add the juice of 1 lemon to the skillet.
    
    10. Serve the mushroom and garlic shrimp with cooked rice or quinoa. Enjoy!
