# Mushroom and Eggplant Parmesan
    
    Originating in Italy, this classic Italian dish takes its name from the cheese used in the recipe, Parmesan. Combining the savory flavors of mushrooms and eggplant, Parmesan cheese, and a tomato-based sauce, this delicious dish is a popular item on many Italian restaurant menus today. It’s easy to make at home as well with just a few simple ingredients, and can be a great way to switch up your weekly menu.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Mushroom and Eggplant Parmesan is 30 minutes, the Cooking Time is 1 hour and 30 minutes, the Skill Level required is Moderate, and it has no Spice Level Scoville rating. When preparing Mushroom and Eggplant Parmesan, you will need a cutting board, a knife, a 9x13 inch baking dish, a saucepan, and a large skillet.

# Ingredients

**Ingredients:**
    - 2 eggs 
    - 1 cup bread crumbs
    - 2 tablespoons Italian seasoning 
    - ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
    - 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
    - 1 eggplant, sliced
    - 1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
    - 2 cups marinara sauce 
    - 8 ounces mozzarella cheese, grated

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Eggplant Parmesan Instructions
    
    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    
    2. Grease a 9 x 13 inch baking dish with olive oil.
    
    3. Slice the mushrooms and eggplant into 1/4 inch thick rounds.
    
    4. In a large bowl, combine the sliced mushrooms and eggplants with the olive oil, garlic, salt and pepper and mix until evenly coated.
    
    5. Spread the mushroom and eggplant slices in the greased baking dish.
    
    6. Pour the marinara sauce over the mushrooms and eggplant.
    
    7. Sprinkle with shredded cheese and breadcrumbs.
    
    8. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
    
    9. Serve hot. Enjoy!
