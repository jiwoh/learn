# Instructions

## Mushroom and Blue Cheese Tartlets Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
    
    2. Grease a 12-cup mini muffin tin with cooking spray or butter.
    
    3. In a large bowl, combine 4 ounces softened cream cheese, 2 tablespoons melted butter, ¼ teaspoon garlic powder, 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce, and ½ teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper. Beat until light and fluffy.
    
    4. Divide the cream cheese mixture among the muffin cups and spread evenly.
    
    5. In a separate bowl, combine 1 cup finely diced mushrooms, 1 cup finely diced onion, 1 cup finely diced ham, and 1 cup shredded blue cheese.
    
    6. Divide the mushroom mixture among the muffin cups and top with additional blue cheese if desired.
    
    7. Bake for 15-20 minutes until the tartlets are set and lightly golden.
    
    8. Let cool in the pan for 5 minutes before transferring to a plate or wire rack to cool completely.
