# Morel Mushroom and Leek Quiche 
    ## Ingredients:
    - 1 9-inch store-bought deep-dish pie crust 
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - 2 leeks, halved lengthwise, washed, and thinly sliced
    - 1/2 pound fresh morel mushrooms, cleaned and sliced
    - 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
    - Salt and freshly ground black pepper
    - 4 large eggs
    - 2 cups heavy cream
    - 1/2 cup grated Gruyere cheese
    
    ## Instructions:
    1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
    2. Line the pie crust with parchment paper and fill with baking weights or dried beans.
    3. Bake for 15 minutes, then remove the parchment paper and weights and bake for an additional 10 minutes until golden brown.
    4. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter and add the leeks. Cook until softened, about 7 minutes.
    5. Add the morel mushrooms, thyme, and salt and pepper to taste. Cook for another 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    6. Remove from heat and spread the mixture in the bottom of the pie crust.
    7. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and cream until combined, then add the Gruyere cheese and mix.
    8. Pour the egg mixture into the pie crust and bake for 40-50 minutes until the quiche is set and golden.
    9. Let cool before serving. Enjoy!
