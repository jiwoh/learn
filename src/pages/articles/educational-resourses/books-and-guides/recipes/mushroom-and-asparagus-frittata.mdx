# Mushroom and Asparagus Frittata
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom and Asparagus Frittata is an Italian-inspired dish that has been enjoyed for centuries. This delicious egg dish combines elements of a savory omelette and a quiche, making it a versatile meal option for breakfast, brunch and dinner. The incorporation of mushrooms and asparagus add a unique flavor and texture to the traditional frittata.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Mushroom and Asparagus Frittata is 15 minutes, the Cooking Time is about 30 minutes, the Skill Level is intermediate and the Spice Level rating is 0. To prepare Mushroom and Asparagus Frittata you would need a bowl, whisk, skillet, non-stick cooking spray and a knife.

# Ingredients

## Ingredients
    
    - 6 large eggs
    - 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
    - 1 small onion, diced
    - 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
    - 1 small bunch asparagus, chopped
    - 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
    - Salt and freshly ground black pepper
    - 2 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Asparagus Frittata Instructions
    
    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Grease a 9-inch (23 cm) ovenproof skillet or baking dish.
    3. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in the skillet over medium heat.
    4. Add 1 cup (150 g) of sliced mushrooms to the skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until they are just beginning to brown, about 4 minutes.
    5. Add 1 cup (180 g) of fresh asparagus pieces to the skillet and cook until the asparagus is tender-crisp, about 4 minutes.
    6. Whisk together 8 large eggs, 1/4 cup (60 ml) of milk, 1/4 teaspoon of salt, and 1/4 teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper in a large bowl until well combined.
    7. Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables in the skillet.
    8. Bake for 20 minutes, or until the eggs are set and golden brown.
    9. Slice into 8 wedges and serve hot. Enjoy!
