# Instructions

### Mushroom and Sausage Stuffed Acorn Squash
    
    *Ingredients:*
    - 2 acorn squash
    - 1 lb bulk Italian sausage
    - 1/2 yellow onion, diced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
    - 1 cup baby spinach, roughly chopped
    - 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
    - 1 tsp salt
    - 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
    - Olive oil
    
    *Instructions:*
    1. Preheat oven to 375° F. 
    2. Cut the acorn squash in half from top to bottom and scoop out the seeds.
    3. Drizzle both halves with olive oil and sprinkle with salt. Place squash cut side down on a baking sheet lined with foil.
    4. Bake for 30 minutes or until the flesh is tender when pierced with a fork.
    5. Meanwhile, in a large skillet over medium heat, add the sausage and cook, breaking it up with a wooden spoon, until the sausage is cooked through.
    6. Add the diced onion and garlic and cook, stirring occasionally, until the onion is softened and the garlic is fragrant.
    7. Add the mushrooms and cook, stirring occasionally, until the mushrooms are softened.
    8. Stir in the baby spinach and cook until spinach is wilted. 
    9. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper.
    10. Divide the sausage filling between the two acorn squash halves and spread it evenly across the squash.
    11. Return the baking sheet to the oven and bake for an additional 20 minutes or until the stuffing is golden and bubbly.
    12. Serve immediately. Enjoy!
