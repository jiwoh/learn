# Mushroom and Feta Stuffed Chicken
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom and feta stuffed chicken is a classic Mediterranean dish that has its roots in Greece, where it is a traditional staple. This hearty dish of juicy chicken breasts stuffed with a savory combination of mushrooms, feta cheese, and herbs is a popular weeknight meal choice for many families. It is easy to make and packed with flavor, making it a perfect dish for any occasion.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Mushroom and feta stuffed chicken is 10 minutes, the Cooking Time is 40 minutes, the Skill Level is Intermediate, and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0.
    
    To make Mushroom and feta stuffed chicken, you will need a knife, cutting board, large frying pan, baking dish, and bowl.

# Ingredients

- 2 chicken breasts
    - 4 oz. cremini mushrooms, thinly sliced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 4 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh thyme
    - 1/3 cup crumbled feta cheese
    - Salt and pepper to taste

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Feta Stuffed Chicken Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
    
    2. In a medium bowl, mix together crumbled feta cheese, minced mushrooms, chopped parsley, and garlic cloves.
    
    3. Place chicken breasts in a greased baking dish and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
    
    4. Spoon the feta cheese mixture onto each chicken breast, spreading evenly.
    
    5. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through and no longer pink inside.
    
    6. Serve warm. Enjoy!
