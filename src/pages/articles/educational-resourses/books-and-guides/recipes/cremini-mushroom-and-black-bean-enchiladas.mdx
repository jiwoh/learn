# Cremini Mushroom and Black Bean Enchiladas
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Cremini Mushroom and Black Bean Enchiladas are a classic Mexican dish that have been popular in Mexico for hundreds of years. They are traditionally made with corn tortillas, which are filled with cremini mushrooms, black beans, and spices, then baked in enchilada sauce. This flavorful combo makes an excellent entree for any Mexican-themed dinner. The filling can be tailored to any dietary preferences, making these enchiladas a perfect choice for vegetarians.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Cremini Mushroom and Black Bean Enchiladas is 10 minutes, the Cooking Time is 45 minutes, the Skill Level is Intermediate, and the Spice Level Scoville rating is Mild. You'll need a large shallow skillet, a large saucepan, a medium saucepan, a cutting board, a sharp knife and a baking dish to prepare this delicious dish.

# Instructions

## Ingredients
    - 10-ounce cremini mushrooms
    - 1 teaspoon olive oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
    - 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
    - 2 cans black beans, drained and rinsed
    - 2 cups prepared enchilada sauce
    - 1 cup shredded cheese
    - 8-10 small soft corn tortillas
    
    ## Directions
    1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
    2. Slice the mushrooms into thin strips and cook in a large skillet over medium-high heat with 1 teaspoon of olive oil.
    3. Add garlic, chili powder, and cumin, stirring until fragrant, about 1 minute.
    4. Add black beans to the mushrooms and mix until well combined. Allow to simmer until heated through.
    5. In an 8x8 inch baking dish, spread 1/2 cup of enchilada sauce to cover the bottom.
    6. Place 2-3 tablespoons of the mushroom and black bean mixture into each tortilla and roll it up. Place each filled tortilla into the baking dish, seam side down.
    7. Spoon the remaining enchilada sauce evenly over the top of the enchiladas and sprinkle cheese on top.
    8. Bake in preheated oven for 20-25 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
    9. Let cool for 5 minutes and serve. Enjoy!
