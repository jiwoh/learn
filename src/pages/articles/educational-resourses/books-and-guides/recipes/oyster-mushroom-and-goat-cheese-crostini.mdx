# Oyster Mushroom and Goat Cheese Crostini
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Oyster Mushroom and Goat Cheese Crostinis are a savory and flavorful appetizer that originated in Italy. It combines savory goat cheese, earthy oyster mushrooms, and crunchy crostini for a delicious combination of textures and flavors. This dish is a classic Italian appetizer that is perfect for any occasion.

# Recipe Overview

The Prep Time for Oyster Mushroom and Goat Cheese Crostini is 15 minutes, the Cooking Time is 25 minutes, and the Skill Level is Easy. This dish has a Spice Level Scoville rating of 0. To prepare Oyster Mushroom and Goat Cheese Crostini, you need a cutting board, chef's knife, and frying pan.

# Ingredients

### Ingredients 
    
    - 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided 
    - 1/4 cup thinly sliced shallots 
    - 1 clove garlic, minced 
    - 2 1/2 cups stemmed, wiped clean oyster mushrooms 
    - Salt and pepper 
    - 2 tablespoons fresh thyme leaves 
    - 1/4 cup dry white wine 
    - 2 ounces soft goat cheese 
    - 1/2 French baguette, cut into 1/2-inch slices 
    - Chopped fresh Italian parsley, optional

# Instructions

### Oyster Mushroom and Goat Cheese Crostini Instructions
    1. Preheat your oven to 425Â°F.
    2. Place your baguette slices on a baking sheet.
    3. Brush each slice generously with olive oil.
    4. Bake the baguette slices for 5-7 minutes, until lightly golden and toasted.
    5. Meanwhile, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    6. When the skillet is hot, add the diced shallots and cook for 2-3 minutes, until fragrant and softened.
    7. Add the garlic and cook for an additional minute.
    8. Add the oyster mushrooms, season with salt and pepper, and cook for 5-7 minutes, until the mushrooms are softened and lightly browned.
    9. Turn off the heat and add the parsley.
    10. To assemble the crostini, spread the goat cheese on each toasted baguette slice.
    11. Top the goat cheese with the mushroom mixture.
    12. Garnish with more parsley, if desired.
    13. Serve and enjoy!
