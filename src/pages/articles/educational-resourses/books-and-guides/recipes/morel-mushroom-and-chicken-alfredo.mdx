# Morel Mushroom and Chicken Alfredo
    
    ## Origin and History
    Morel Mushroom and Chicken Alfredo is a classic Italian-American dish, believed to have originated in the 19th century. It's creamy, rich and flavourful, made with mushrooms, cream, Parmesan cheese and chicken. This delicious dish is a staple of Italian-American cuisine and is commonly served with pasta.

    * Prep Time: 15 minutes

    To prepare Morel Mushroom and Chicken Alfredo, you will need a saucepan, cooking pot, whisk, spoon, and grater.

# Ingredients

## Morel Mushroom and Chicken Alfredo Ingredients
    
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - 1/2 cup diced onion
    - 3 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    - 2 cups chicken broth
    - 1/2 cup cream or half-and-half
    - 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
    - 1/2 teaspoon granulated sugar
    - 2 cups cooked, diced chicken
    - 2 cups sliced fresh morel mushrooms
    - 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
    - 1/2 pound cooked fettuccine

# Instructions

## Morel Mushroom and Chicken Alfredo Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
    2. In a medium saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
    3. Add the diced onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
    4. Add garlic and morels and toss to combine. Cook until fragrant and mushrooms are soft, about 5 minutes.
    5. Add chicken broth and white wine and simmer until sauce reduces, about 10 minutes.
    6. Add heavy cream, stirring to combine.
    7. Add parmesan cheese and season to taste with salt and pepper.
    8. Spread alfredo mixture into an 8x8-inch baking dish.
    9. Arrange cooked chicken in the dish on top of the alfredo mixture.
    10. Bake at 350°F for 20 minutes, or until cheese is golden and bubbly.
    11. Enjoy!
