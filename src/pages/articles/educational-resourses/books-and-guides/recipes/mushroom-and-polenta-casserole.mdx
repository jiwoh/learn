# Mushroom and Polenta Casserole
    
    ## Ingredients 
    * 1 tablespoon olive oil
    * 1 onion, diced
    * 2 cloves garlic, minced
    * 8 ounces sliced mushrooms
    * 1 cup vegetable broth
    * 1/2 teaspoon thyme
    * 1/2 teaspoon oregano
    * 1/2 teaspoon salt
    * 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
    * 3 cups cooked polenta
    * 2 tablespoons butter
    * 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    * 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
    
    ## Instructions
    
    1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
    
    2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until the onion is softened, about 5 minutes.
    
    3. Add the mushrooms and cook for 3 to 4 minutes, until they start to soften.
    
    4. Add the vegetable broth, thyme, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5 minutes.
    
    5. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish. Spread the cooked polenta in the bottom of the dish.
    
    6. Pour the mushroom mixture over the polenta and spread it to the edges. Dot the top of the casserole with the butter and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
    
    7. Bake for 20 minutes, until the cheese is melted and the casserole is hot.
    
    8. Sprinkle with parsley before serving. Enjoy!
