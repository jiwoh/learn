# Recipe Overview

    **Mushroom and Chicken Alfredo**
    * Prep Time: 30 minutes 
    * Cooking Time: 15 minutes 
    * Skill Level: Moderate 
    * Spice Level: Low 
    
    The necessary cooking utensils for preparing Mushroom and Chicken Alfredo include a large pot, colander, wooden spoon, paring knives, and grater.

# Instructions

###Ingredients
    - 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
    - 4 tablespoons butter
    - 1 cup sliced mushrooms
    - 4 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 cups heavy cream
    - 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    - 1 teaspoon salt
    - 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    - 3/4 pound cooked fettuccine
    
    ###Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    2. Place chicken into a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
    3. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink and the juices run clear.
    4. Melt the butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in the mushrooms and garlic, and cook until the mushrooms are soft.
    5. Pour in the cream and bring to a simmer. Simmer for 5 minutes.
    6. Add the Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper to the cream sauce, and mix until melted.
    7. Remove the chicken from the oven and cut into cubes.
    8. Place the cooked fettuccine into the skillet with the cream sauce. Add the cubed chicken, and stir together.
    9. Cook for 5 minutes, or until chicken is heated through. Serve.
