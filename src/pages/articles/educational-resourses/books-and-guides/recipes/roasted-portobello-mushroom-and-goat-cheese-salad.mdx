## Roasted Portobello Mushroom and Goat Cheese Salad Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F. 
    2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. 
    3. Clean mushrooms by gently scrubbing with a damp cloth and patting dry. 
    4. Cut 4 portobello mushroom caps into 1/2-inch slices. 
    5. Place sliced mushrooms onto the prepared baking sheet. 
    6. Drizzle mushroom slices with olive oil and sprinkle with freshly ground black pepper, to taste. 
    7. Roast in preheated oven for 20 minutes. 
    8. Meanwhile, combine mixed emeralds and tomatoes in a large bowl.
    9. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, garlic and salt. 
    10. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to combine. 
    11. Top the salad with the roasted mushrooms and crumbled goat cheese. 
    12. Serve and enjoy!
