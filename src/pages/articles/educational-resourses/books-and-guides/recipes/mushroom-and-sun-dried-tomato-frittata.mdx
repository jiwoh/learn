# Instructions

# Mushroom and Sun-Dried Tomato Frittata
    
    ## Ingredients
    
    - 8 large eggs
    - 1/4 cup cream
    - 2 tablespoons of butter
    - 1 cup sliced mushrooms 
    - 2 cloves of garlic, minced
    - 2 tablespoons sun-dried tomatoes, finely chopped
    - 1/4 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
    - 2 tablespoons parsley, finely chopped
    - 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
    
    ## Instructions
    
    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    
    2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs and cream until well combined.
    
    3. Heat the butter in an oven-safe 10-inch (25 cm) non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Add the mushrooms and garlic and cook until the mushrooms are softened and lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
    
    4. Reduce the heat to medium-low and add the sun-dried tomatoes, salt, pepper, and parsley to the skillet. Cook for 1 minute, stirring occasionally.
    
    5. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are set around the edges and the center is still slightly runny, about 5 minutes.
    
    6. Sprinkle the Parmesan cheese over the top of the frittata.
    
    7. Place the skillet in the preheated oven and bake until the center is set and the top is golden brown, about 15 minutes.
    
    8. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
