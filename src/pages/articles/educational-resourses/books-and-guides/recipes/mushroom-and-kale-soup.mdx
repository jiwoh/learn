# Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1 onion, chopped
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
    - 2 carrots, diced
    - 2 celery stalks, diced
    - 4 cups vegetable broth
    - 1 cup water
    - 2 bay leaves
    - 2 cups kale, chopped
    - ½ teaspoon dried thyme
    - Salt and freshly ground pepper, to taste
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Kale Soup Instructions
    1. Heat a large pot over medium-high heat. 
    2. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil, then add 1 large chopped onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until it begins to soften, about 5 minutes. 
    3. Add 1 pound of sliced mushrooms and cook, stirring occasionally, until the mushrooms are lightly browned, about 8 minutes.
    4. Add 1 bunch of chopped kale (about 4-5 cups) and cook, stirring occasionally, until the kale has wilted, about 5 minutes.
    5. Add 6 cups of vegetable broth and bring to a boil. 
    6. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 10 minutes. 
    7. Purée the soup using an immersion blender until desired consistency is achieved. 
    8. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve.
