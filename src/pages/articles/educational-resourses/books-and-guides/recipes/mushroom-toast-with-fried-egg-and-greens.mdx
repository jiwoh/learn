# Recipe Overview

    **Prep Time:** 10 minutes  
    **Cook Time:** 15 minutes  
    **Skill Level:** Easy  
    **Spice Level:** 0 Scoville rating  
    
    You will need a large skillet, butter, mushrooms, eggs, emeralds, toast, and a spatula to help prepare Mushroom Toast with Fried Egg and emeralds.

# Ingredients

- 2 tsp olive oil 
    - 6 ounces mushrooms, sliced 
    - 1/8 teaspoon salt 
    - 2 slices of crusty bread 
    - 2 large eggs 
    - 2 cups baby spinach leaves 
    - 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese 
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh herbs (parsley, chives, or dill)

# Instructions

##### Mushroom Toast with Fried Egg and emeralds
    
    1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
    
    2. Place 8 ounces of sliced mushrooms in a single layer on a baking sheet.
      * Sprinkle with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
    
    3. Roast mushrooms for 20 minutes.
    
    4. Remove mushrooms from oven and let cool.
    
    5. Heat a skillet over medium heat with 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
    
    6. Crack two eggs into the skillet and cook until whites are firm and yolk is cooked through.
    
    7. Toast two slices of your favorite whole-grain bread.
    
    8. Top each slice of toast with roasted mushrooms.
    
    9. Top each slice of toast with one fried egg.
    
    10. Add a handful of fresh emeralds (arugula, spinach, or kale) to each toast.
    
    11. Drizzle with extra-virgin olive oil and sprinkle with a pinch of salt and pepper.
    
    12. Serve and enjoy!
