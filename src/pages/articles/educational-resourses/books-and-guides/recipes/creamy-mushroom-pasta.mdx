# Creamy Mushroom Pasta
    
    Creamy Mushroom Pasta is a simple and delicious Italian-style pasta dish that dates back several centuries. It is best known for its creamy, garlicky sauce made from a combination of cream, mushrooms, and Parmesan cheese. The dish is versatile and can be enjoyed by vegetarians, meat-lovers, and everyone in between.

# Recipe Overview

    **Prep Time:** 15 minutes
    
    **Cooking Time:** 15 minutes
    
    **Skill Level:** Easy
    
    **Spice Level Scoville Rating:** Mild
    
    To prepare Creamy Mushroom Pasta, you will need a saucepan, a skillet, a spoon, and a spatula.

# Ingredients

## Creamy Mushroom Pasta Ingredients
    - 1/3 cup olive oil
    - 10 ounces mushrooms, sliced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1/4 cup brewed espresso
    - 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    - 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
    - Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
    - 8 ounces penne pasta
    - 3 tablespoons butter
    - 1 cup heavy cream
    - 1/3 cup Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese, freshly grated
    - 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

# Instructions

## Creamy Mushroom Pasta Instructions
    1. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    2. Add 8 ounces of sliced mushrooms and sauté until golden brown and tender, about 4 minutes.
    3. Add one minced clove of garlic and sauté for an additional minute.
    4. Pour in one cup of heavy cream and stir to combine. Simmer for 5 minutes.
    5. Add 1/4 teaspoon of salt, 1/4 teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper, and 2 tablespoons of freshly chopped parsley.
    6. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and add 8 ounces of spaghetti. Cook according to package instructions.
    7. Drain spaghetti and toss with the mushroom cream sauce.
    8. Serve with freshly grated Parmesan cheese, if desired. Enjoy!
