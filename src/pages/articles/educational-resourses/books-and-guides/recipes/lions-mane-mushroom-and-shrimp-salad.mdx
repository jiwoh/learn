## Lions Mane Mushroom and Shrimp Salad Instructions
    
    1. Start by heating a large skillet over medium-high heat. 
    2. Once the pan is hot, add 2 tablespoons of vegetable or canola oil and swirl to coat the pan.
    3. Add 8 ounces of shrimp and 1/4 teaspoon of salt to the pan and sauté for about 4 minutes, until the shrimp is pink and cooked through. Remove from the pan and set aside.
    4. In the same skillet, add 1 tablespoon of oil and the 4 ounces of shredded Lions Mane mushrooms.
    5. Sauté the mushrooms for 4 to 5 minutes, until lightly browned.
    6. Add 1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder, 1/4 teaspoon of onion powder, 1/4 teaspoon of salt, 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper and 1/4 teaspoon of paprika to the mushrooms and stir to combine.
    7. Now add the cooked shrimp back to the pan and stir to combine with the mushrooms.
    8. In a large bowl, mix together 4 cups of salad emeralds, 1/2 cup of cherry tomatoes, 1/2 cup of red onions, and 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese.
    9. Top the salad emeralds with the mushrooms and shrimp mixture and serve.
