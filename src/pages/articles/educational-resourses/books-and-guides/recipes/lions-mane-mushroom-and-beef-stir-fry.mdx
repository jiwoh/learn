# Instructions

## Lions Mane Mushroom and Beef Stir-fry 
    
    1. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
    
    2. Add the beef and cook, stirring occasionally, until cooked through, about 2-3 minutes. 
    
    3. Transfer the beef to a plate with a slotted spoon.
    
    4. Add the remaining oil to the pan and reduce the heat to medium.
    
    5. Add the onions, garlic, and peppers and saut√© until softened, about 5 minutes.
    
    6. Add the ginger and cook for 1-2 minutes.
    
    7. Add the Lions Mane mushrooms and stir to combine with the vegetables.
    
    8. Increase the heat and add the cooked beef, soy sauce, and sugar.
    
    9. Cook, stirring, until the mixture is evenly heated, about 3 minutes.
    
    10. Serve hot over cooked rice. Enjoy!
