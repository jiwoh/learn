# Mushroom and Swiss Quiche 
    
    ## Origin and History 
    Mushroom and Swiss Quiche is an egg-based French dish that was first prepared in the mid-1700s. It is a combination of mushrooms, onions, and Swiss cheese all baked in tart pastry. It is often served for breakfast, brunch, lunch, or dinner, and is a popular dish all over the world.
