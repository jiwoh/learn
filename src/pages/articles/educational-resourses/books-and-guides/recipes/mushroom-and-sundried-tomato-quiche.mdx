# Instructions

## Mushroom and Sundried Tomato Quiche Recipe
    
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F/175°C.
    
    2. In a medium bowl, combine the crumbled feta cheese and chopped sundried tomatoes.
    
    3. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil. When oil is hot, add the mushrooms and cook until golden, about 8 minutes. Remove from the heat and add to the bowl with the feta and sundried tomatoes.
    
    4. In a medium bowl, beat together the eggs, milk, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
    
    5. Grease a 9-inch pie dish with 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
    
    6. Pour the egg mixture into the dish.
    
    7. Evenly sprinkle the mushroom and sundried tomato mixture over the egg mixture.
    
    8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and a knife inserted into the center comes out clean.
    
    9. Allow to cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
