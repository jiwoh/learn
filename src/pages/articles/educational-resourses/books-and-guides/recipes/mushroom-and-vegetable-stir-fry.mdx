## Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry
    **Ingredients**
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 1/2 onion, chopped
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
    - 1 red bell pepper, chopped
    - 1/2 head of broccoli, cut into small florets
    - 1/4 cup soy sauce
    - 2 tablespoons cornstarch
    
    **Instructions**
    1. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat.
    2. Add onion and garlic; saut√© for 2 minutes.
    3. Add mushrooms, bell pepper, and broccoli. Cook until vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
    4. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce and cornstarch.
    5. Add soy sauce mixture to skillet and stir to coat vegetables.
    6. Cook for 2 minutes, until sauce thickens.
    7. Serve hot over rice.
