# Instructions

## Ingredients 
    * 1 pound pork tenderloin
    * 2 tablespoons olive oil
    * 1 onion, chopped
    * 2 cloves garlic, minced
    * 8 ounces baby spinach
    * 4 ounces sliced mushrooms
    * 1 teaspoon fresh thyme
    * ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
    * ¼ teaspoon salt
    * ¼ teaspoon pepper
    * 2 tablespoons butter, melted
    
    ## Instructions 
    1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
    
    2. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic and cook for 3 minutes.
    
    3. Add the spinach and mushrooms to the skillet and cook for 5 minutes.
    
    4. Remove from the heat and stir in the thyme, nutmeg, salt and pepper.
    
    5. Place the pork tenderloins in a large baking dish.
    
    6. Divide the vegetable mixture in half and spread it over each tenderloin.
    
    7. Roll up each tenderloin and tuck in the ends.
    
    8. Brush the melted butter over the tops of the stuffed pork tenderloins.
    
    9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
    
    10. Let stand for 10 minutes before slicing.
