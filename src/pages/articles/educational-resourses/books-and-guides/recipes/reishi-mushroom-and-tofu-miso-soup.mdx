# Reishi Mushroom and Tofu Miso Soup
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Reishi Mushroom and Tofu Miso Soup is a traditional Japanese dish that has been around for centuries, but has recently gained popularity as a healing tonic. The main ingredients of this soup are reishi mushrooms, silken or soft tofu, and miso paste, which is made from fermented soybeans. The reishi mushroom has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its healing properties, and is said to be beneficial for reducing inflammation and strengthening the immune system. Tofu adds a protein boost, while miso adds depth and umami flavor to the soup. This dish is a balanced and nutritious meal, and is a healthy and delicious way to nourish your body.

# Recipe Overview

The Prep Time for Reishi Mushroom and Tofu Miso Soup is 15 minutes, the Cooking Time is 15 minutes, the Skill Level is easy, and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0 (i.e. is not spicy). To prepare Reishi Mushroom and Tofu Miso Soup, you will need a large pot, a wooden spoon, a strainer, and a soup ladle.

# Ingredients

* **4 cups vegetable broth**
    * **1/4 cup miso paste**
    * **4-5 dried reishi mushrooms, cut into small pieces**
    * **1 block of firm tofu, cut into cubes**
    * **1 handful of chopped emerald onions**
    * **1/4 cup diced carrots**
    * **1/4 cup diced celery**
    * **1 teaspoon grated ginger**
    * **2 tablespoons soy sauce**
    * **2 tablespoons toasted sesame oil**
    * **2 tablespoons brown rice vinegar**
    * **1/2 teaspoon chili flakes (optional)**

# Instructions

### Reishi Mushroom and Tofu Miso Soup Instructions
    
    1. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
    
    2. Add 2 tablespoons of dried reishi mushrooms to the pot and reduce the heat to medium. Simmer for 10 minutes.
    
    3. Strain the water and mushrooms from the pot into a heat-safe bowl. Reserve the water.
    
    4. Chop the reishi mushrooms into small pieces and set aside.
    
    5. Return the reishi mushroom water to the pot over medium heat.
    
    6. Add 2 cups of vegetable broth and bring to a simmer.
    
    7. Chop 1/2 cup of emerald onions, 1/2 cup of celery and 3 cloves of garlic.
    
    8. Add the chopped vegetables to the pot and simmer for 8 minutes.
    
    9. Cut 1 block of firm tofu into small cubes and add to the pot.
    
    10. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
    
    11. In a separate bowl, mix 1/4 cup of miso paste with 1/4 cup of the reishi mushroom water.
    
    12. Add the miso mixture to the pot and stir to combine.
    
    13. Simmer for an additional 3 minutes.
    
    14. Turn off the heat and stir in the chopped reishi mushrooms.
    
    15. Serve the Reishi Mushroom and Tofu Miso Soup warm. Enjoy!
