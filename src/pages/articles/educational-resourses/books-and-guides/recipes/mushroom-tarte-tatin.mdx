# Mushroom Tarte Tatin
    
    Mushroom Tarte Tatin is a savory variation of the classic French Apple Tarte Tatin. It is made using caramelized mushrooms, butter, and puff pastry, and can be enjoyed as an appetizer or a main meal.
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom Tarte Tatin was created by French chef Guy Legay in 1975, who was inspired by the classic French Apple Tarte Tatin to create a savory version. Legay's recipe has since been adapted by many chefs and mushroom lovers, who all strive to recreate this delicious and visually appealing dish.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Mushroom Tarte Tatin is 10 minutes, the Cooking Time is 30 minutes, the Skill Level is medium, and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0. To prepare Mushroom Tarte Tatin, you will need a 9 inch tart pan, rolling pin, butter, mushrooms, and onions.
