# Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
    - 1/2 cup chopped onion
    - 3/4 pound mushrooms, thinly sliced
    - 1 1/2 teaspoons chopped fresh thyme
    - Salt and freshly ground black pepper
    - 6 eggs
    - 2 tablespoons milk
    - 1 cup shredded fontina cheese

# Instructions

# Mushroom and Fontina Omelet Instructions
    
    1. Heat a medium skillet over medium-high heat.
    2. Add 1 tablespoon oil to the skillet and swirl to coat.
    3. Add 4 ounces of sliced mushrooms and cook, stirring occasionally, for 4 minutes or until golden brown.
    4. Add 2 tablespoons of sliced emerald onions and cook, stirring occasionally, for 1 minute.
    5. Remove the mushrooms and emerald onions from the skillet and place in a bowl.
    6. Reduce the heat to low and add 1 tablespoon of butter to the same skillet.
    7. Add the mushrooms and emerald onions back to the skillet and stir to combine.
    8. Crack 2 large eggs into the skillet and season with salt and pepper, to taste.
    9. Cook, stirring gently with a wooden spoon, for 2-3 minutes or until the eggs are cooked but slightly runny.
    10. Sprinkle 2 ounces of shredded Fontina cheese over the eggs and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
    11. Gently fold the omelet in half and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes or until the eggs are cooked through.
    12. Slide the omelet onto a plate and serve immediately. Enjoy!
