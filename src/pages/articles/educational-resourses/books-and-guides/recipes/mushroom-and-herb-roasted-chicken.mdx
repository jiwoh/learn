# Instructions

## Mushroom and Herb Roasted Chicken 
    
    ###Ingredients: 
    - 4-5 lb. whole chicken
    - 1/2 lb. mushrooms, quartered
    - 1/4 cup fresh herbs, finely chopped (e.g. rosemary, thyme, oregano, parsley)
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1 teaspoon kosher salt
    - 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    
    ###Instructions: 
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
    2. Rinse and pat dry the chicken. Place in roasting pan.
    3. In a medium bowl, combine mushrooms, herbs, garlic, olive oil, salt and pepper. Stir to combine.
    4. Spread the mushroom and herb mixture over the top of the chicken.
    5. Place the chicken in the preheated oven and roast for 1 hour and 15 minutes (or until the internal temperature of the chicken is 165°F).
    6. Remove from the oven and let rest for 10 minutes.
    7. Carve the chicken and serve. Enjoy!
