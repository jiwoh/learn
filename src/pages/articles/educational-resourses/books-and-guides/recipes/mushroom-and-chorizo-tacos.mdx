# Instructions

## Mushroom and Chorizo Tacos Recipe
    
    ##### Ingredients:
    - 8 ounces of chorizo sausage
    - 8 ounces of mushrooms, sliced
    - 1 tablespoon olive oil
    - 4-6 flour or corn tortillas
    - 1 cup grated cheese
    - 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, roughly chopped
    - Salsa and sour cream for topping
    
    ##### Instructions:
    1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook the chorizo until browned and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
    2. Add the olive oil to the same skillet and add the mushrooms. Cook for 2-3 minutes until softened.
    3. Add the chorizo back to the skillet and stir to combine.
    4. Heat the tortillas in a dry skillet or in the oven.
    5. Place a few spoonfuls of the chorizo and mushroom mixture onto each warm tortilla. Top with grated cheese, cilantro, and salsa.
    6. Serve with a dollop of sour cream. Enjoy!
