# Enoki Mushroom and Beef Skewers 
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Enoki Mushroom and Beef Skewers are a popular Japanese dish that has been enjoyed for generations. It is most commonly served as an appetizer before a meal, and the skewered beef and mushrooms are marinated in a combination of soy sauce and other spices before being grilled or fried. The beef is usually cut into thin strips or cubes, and the Enoki mushrooms are usually left whole or sliced into smaller pieces. This dish is often served with ponzu sauce or daikon radish as an accompaniment.

# Recipe Overview

    The **Prep Time** for Enoki Mushroom and Beef Skewers is 15 minutes, the **Cooking Time** is 10 minutes, the **Skill Level** is Easy, and the **Spice Level** is Mild (1,000-2,000 Scoville rating).  To prepare this dish, you'll need a skillet, grill, skewers, and cutting board.

# Ingredients

- 1/2 pound thinly sliced beef (such as flank steak)
    - 8 ounces Enoki mushrooms, trimmed
    - 4-5 tablespoons soy sauce
    - 2 tablespoons dark brown sugar
    - 2 tablespoons sesame oil
    - 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
    - Salt and ground black pepper, to taste
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 1-2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds
    - 6-8 skewers

# Instructions

### Enoki Mushroom and Beef Skewers Instructions
    
    1. Gather the ingredients: 12 ounces of beef, 3 tablespoons of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of sesame oil, 1/4 teaspoon of ground ginger, 1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder, 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper, 12 ounces of enoki mushrooms, 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
    
    2. Trim the beef into 1-inch cubes.
    
    3. In a medium-sized bowl, combine soy sauce, sesame oil, ground ginger, garlic powder, and black pepper. Add the cubed beef and stir to coat all pieces. Cover and place in the refrigerator to marinate for at least 2 hours.
    
    4. Meanwhile, carefully separate the enoki mushrooms.
    
    5. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    
    6. Skewer beef cubes and enoki mushrooms alternately onto skewers. Discard remaining marinade.
