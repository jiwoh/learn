# Ingredients

- 2 large eggs 
    - 1 teaspoon olive oil 
    - 1 cup sliced mushrooms 
    - 2 tablespoons chopped onion 
    - 2 tablespoons grated cheddar cheese 
    - Salt and fresh ground black pepper to taste

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Cheese Omelette Instructions
    1. Heat a small non-stick skillet over medium heat.
    2. Add 1 teaspoon of butter and allow to melt in the pan.
    3. Add 1/4 cup of sliced mushrooms, 1/4 teaspoon of minced garlic, 1/4 teaspoon of onion powder, and a pinch of salt and pepper to the pan.
    4. Saut√© until mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes.
    5. Whisk together two eggs in a bowl and add 1 tablespoon of milk.
    6. Pour the eggs into the pan with the mushrooms.
    7. Let the eggs cook until the bottom is beginning to set, and then add 1/4 cup of grated cheese and stir.
    8. Cook until eggs are completely set and cheese is melted.
    9. Serve hot with toast. Enjoy!
