# Instructions

## Mushroom and Gouda Grilled Cheese Sandwich
    
    ### Ingredients
    
    - 2 slices of sandwich bread
    - 2-3 mushrooms, sliced
    - 1-2 oz Gouda cheese, sliced
    - 1 tablespoon butter
    
    ### Instructions
    
    1. Preheat a large skillet over medium heat. 
    2. Spread one side of each slice of sandwich bread with butter. 
    3. Place the slices of bread, buttered-side down, onto the preheated skillet. 
    4. Lay the slices of cheese and mushrooms onto one slice of bread. 
    5. Place the other slice of bread onto the sandwich, buttered-side up. 
    6. Cook for 4-5 minutes or until the bottom slice of bread is golden brown.
    7. Carefully flip the sandwich over and cook for an additional 4-5 minutes or until the other side is golden brown and the cheese is melted. 
    8. Remove from the skillet and serve. Enjoy!
