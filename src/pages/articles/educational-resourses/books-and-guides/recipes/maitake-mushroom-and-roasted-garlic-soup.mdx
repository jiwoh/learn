# Maitake Mushroom and Roasted Garlic Soup
    
    ## Ingredients 
    * 1 head of garlic 
    * 1/2 cup olive oil 
    * 1 large onion, chopped  
    * 2 cloves of garlic, minced  
    * 4 cups vegetable broth 
    * 1/2 pound maitake mushrooms, roughly chopped  
    * 1/2 cup white wine 
    * 2 tablespoons fresh thyme leaves 
    * 1 teaspoon sea salt 
    * 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper 
    
    ## Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
    2. Slice top off of the head of garlic and place in the middle of a piece of foil. Drizzle with 1 tablespoon of the olive oil and wrap the garlic up tightly in the foil. 
    3. Place garlic in the oven and roast for 30 minutes, or until garlic is golden brown and soft. 
    4. Meanwhile, heat remaining olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and minced garlic and saut√© for 5 to 8 minutes, until onion is soft and translucent.
    5. Add vegetable broth, mushrooms, white wine, thyme, sea salt, and black pepper to the pot. 
    6. Squeeze the roasted garlic out of the head and into the soup. Stir until well incorporated. 
    7. Bring soup to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes. 
    8. Taste soup and adjust seasoning as needed. Enjoy!
