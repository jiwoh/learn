# Instructions

## Ingredients 
    * 2 slices of your favorite bread
    * 2 tablespoons butter
    * 2 ounces sliced mushrooms
    * 1 small clove garlic, finely chopped
    * 2 slices gouda cheese
    
    ## Instructions 
    1. Heat a pan over medium-low heat.
    2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
    3. Place the slices of bread, butter side down, in the pan.
    4. Place the mushrooms and garlic on one of the slices of bread.
    5. Place the cheese slices on top of the mushrooms.
    6. Place the other slice of bread on top, butter side up.
    7. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until the bottom is golden brown.
    8. Carefully flip the sandwich and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the other side is golden brown and the cheese has melted.
    9. Serve hot. Enjoy!
