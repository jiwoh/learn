# Instructions

## Mushroom Tacos Instructions
    1. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    2. Add in 1 pound of sliced mushrooms and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until mushrooms are lightly browned.
    3. Add 1 tablespoon of freshly minced garlic and stir for 30 seconds.
    4. Add 1 teaspoon of ground cumin, 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika, and 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper.
    5. Stir to combine the seasonings and cook for 1 minute.
    6. Add in 1/2 cup of beef or chicken broth and scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet.
    7. Allow the mixture to simmer over medium-low heat for 5 minutes.
    8. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and warm 8-10 corn tortillas for 1-2 minutes per side.
    9. Once the tortillas are warm, place them on a plate and top with the mushroom mixture.
    10. Garnish the tacos with freshly chopped cilantro, diced tomatoes, and minced red onion.
    11. Serve immediately. Enjoy!
