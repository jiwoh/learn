# Instructions

## Garlic Mushroom Pizza Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. Grease a 9-inch (23 cm) round pizza pan with a little bit of olive oil.
    3. In a medium bowl, mix together 2 tablespoons of chopped garlic, 2 tablespoons of chopped parsley, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano. Set aside.
    4. In a separate bowl, mix together 1 cup of sliced mushrooms, 2 tablespoons of olive oil, and the the garlic, parsley, salt, and oregano mixture.
    5. Spread the mushroom mixture over the pizza pan.
    6. Sprinkle 1 cup of mozzarella cheese over the mushrooms.
    7. Bake for 20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
    8. Enjoy!
