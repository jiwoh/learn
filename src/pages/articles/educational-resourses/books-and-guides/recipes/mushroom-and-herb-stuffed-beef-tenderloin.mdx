**Mushroom and Herb Stuffed Beef Tenderloin**
    - Prep Time: 45 minutes 
    - Cooking Time: 1 hour and 25 minutes
    - Skill Level: Intermediate 
    - Spice Level Scoville Rating: None  
    
    This recipe requires a few cooking utensils including a cutting board, Chef's knife, measuring cups, measuring spoons, small bowl, rubber spatula, roasting pan, and aluminum foil.

# Ingredients

## Mushroom and herb stuffed beef tenderloin Ingredients
    
    * 2 lbs of beef tenderloin
    * 4 cloves of garlic
    * 1 small onion, diced
    * 2 tablespoons of olive oil
    * 1 cup of diced mushrooms
    * 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
    * 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
    * Salt and pepper to taste
    * 1 cup of dry white wine
    * 1 tablespoon of butter
    * 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Herb Stuffed Beef Tenderloin Instructions
    
    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
    2. Rub the beef tenderloin with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
    3. In a large bowl, mix together the mushrooms, herbs, garlic, shallots and butter.
    4. Spread the mushroom mixture over the top of the beef tenderloin.
    5. Wrap the beef tenderloin in bacon, making sure to cover the entire surface. 
    6. Place the beef tenderloin on a baking sheet and roast it in the preheated oven for 1 hour, or until the internal temperature reaches 135℉.
    7. Once the beef tenderloin is cooked, let it sit for 10 minutes before slicing.
    8. Serve with the roasted mushrooms and your favorite side dish. Enjoy!
