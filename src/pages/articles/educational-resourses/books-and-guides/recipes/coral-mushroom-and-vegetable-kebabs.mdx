# Instructions

## Coral Mushroom and Vegetable Kebabs
    
    ### Ingredients
    
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 teaspoons dried oregano
    - 2 teaspoons dried thyme
    - Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
    - 2 cups coral mushrooms, cleaned and cubed
    - 1 large red onion, cut into wedges
    - 1 large red pepper, cut into 1-inch cubes
    - 1 large zucchini, cut into 1-inch cubes
    - 16 cherry tomatoes
    - 8 metal or wooden skewers
    
    ### Instructions
    
    1. Preheat an outdoor grill or indoor grill pan to high heat.
    
    2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper.
    
    3. On each skewer, thread the mushrooms, onion, pepper, zucchini, and tomatoes.
    
    4. Place the kebabs on the preheated grill. Brush with the olive oil mixture and cook, turning occasionally, until the vegetables are lightly charred and tender, 8 to 10 minutes.
    
    5. Transfer the kebabs to a serving plate and serve. Enjoy!
