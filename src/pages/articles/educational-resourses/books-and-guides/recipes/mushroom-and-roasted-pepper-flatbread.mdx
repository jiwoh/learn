# Mushroom and Roasted Pepper Flatbread
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom and roasted pepper flatbread is a delicious and easy-to-make snack that originated in the Mediterranean region. It has become increasingly popular in recent years and is now a staple of many restaurants and households. It consists of mushrooms, roasted peppers, and spices all cooked together on a thin flatbread. The dish is best served warm and goes great with a glass of wine or your favorite dip.

# Recipe Overview

    **Mushroom and Roasted Pepper Flatbread**
    
    - Prep Time: 15 minutes
    - Cooking Time: 15 minutes
    - Skill Level: Easy 
    - Spice Level: Low (200-500 Scoville rating)
    
    You will need a baking sheet, rolling pin, sharp knife, and bowl to prepare your Mushroom and Roasted Pepper Flatbread.

# Ingredients

# Mushroom and roasted pepper flatbread Ingredients
    - 1 flatbread
    - 3 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1/2 cup marinated mushrooms
    - 1 roasted red bell pepper, chopped
    - 1/2 teaspoon Italian herb seasoning
    - 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
    - 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Roasted Pepper Flatbread Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. In a medium bowl, combine 1 1/2 cups (190 g) all-purpose flour, 1/2 teaspoon (2.5 g) salt and 1 teaspoon (5 g) sugar.
    3. Slowly pour in 3/4 cup (165 ml) warm water and 4 tablespoons (56 g) olive oil while stirring. Knead into a soft dough.
    4. On a lightly floured work surface, roll the dough into an 11-inch (28 cm) round. Place on a greased baking sheet.
    5. Spread 2 tablespoons (28 g) olive oil over the dough.
    6. Sprinkle lightly with 1/4 teaspoon (1.25 g) garlic powder and 1/4 teaspoon (1.25 g) dried oregano.
    7. Top with 3/4 cup (90 g) sliced mushrooms and 1/2 cup (65 g) roasted red peppers.
    8. Sprinkle with 1/4 cup (25 g) grated Parmesan cheese and 1/4 cup (25 g) crumbled feta cheese.
    9. Bake for 20 minutes or until golden brown.
    10. Cut into wedges and serve. Enjoy!
