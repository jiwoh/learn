# Mushroom and Cheddar Strata
    
    ## Origin and History 
    Mushroom and Cheddar Strata is an elegant, savory brunch dish made with egg-soaked bread, mushrooms, and Cheddar cheese. This classic dish originated in France, where it is known as a “strata” or layered dish. This hearty breakfast dish has been popularized in the United States since the late 1800s, and today it is still a favorite for weekend brunches.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Mushroom and Cheddar Strata is 10 minutes, the Cooking Time is 40 minutes, and the Skill Level is Easy. The Spice Level Scoville rating for this dish is 0. To prepare Mushroom and Cheddar Strata, you will need a large bowl, a 9x13 inch casserole dish, a whisk, a large skillet and a spoon.

# Ingredients

## Mushroom and Cheddar Strata Ingredients
    
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - 1/2 cup diced onion
    - 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
    - 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
    - 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    - Salt and pepper, to taste
    - 8 ounces sliced cremini mushrooms
    - 8 ounces French or Italian bread, cubed
    - 4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
    - 8 eggs
    - 2 cups whole milk
    - 2 tablespoons dijon mustard

# Instructions

###1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
    ###2. Grease and 8-inch square baking dish with butter.
    ###3. Place 6 slices of bread cut into cubes in baking dish.
    ###4. Heat 2 tablespoons of butter in a skillet and add 8 ounces of chopped mushrooms, 1/4 teaspoon of salt, and 1/4 teaspoon of pepper.
    ###5. Cook for 5 minutes and then add 1/4 cup of chopped onion and continue cooking for an additional 3 minutes until both the onion and mushrooms are tender.
    ###6. Place the cooked mushrooms and onions on top of the bread cubes in the baking dish. 
    ###7. Sprinkle 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese and 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese over the top.
    ###8. In a medium bowl, whisk together 6 eggs, 2 cups of milk, 1/4 teaspoon of salt, and 1/8 teaspoon of pepper.
    ###9. Pour the egg mixture over the cheese and bread cubes.
    ###10. Bake for 40-45 minutes until the top is golden brown.
