## Chicken of the Woods Mushroom and Beef Stew 
    
    ### Ingredients: 
    * 2 pounds beef (stew meat, cubed chuck roast, or steak) 
    * 1 teaspoon salt 
    * 1 tablespoon ground pepper 
    * 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil 
    * 1 medium yellow onion, diced 
    * 2 large carrots, peeled and diced 
    * 2 stalks celery, diced 
    * 2 cloves garlic, minced 
    * 2 tablespoons tomato paste 
    * 1 bay leaf 
    * 1 teaspoon fresh thyme, chopped 
    * 1 cup beef broth 
    * 1 1/2 cups red wine 
    * 2 cups fresh chicken of the woods mushroom or reconstituted dried mushrooms 
    * 1 large russet potato, peeled and diced 
    * 1 tablespoon butter 
    * 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 
    * 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped 
    
    ### Instructions:
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).
    2. Place beef, salt and pepper in a large bowl and toss to combine.
    3. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. When the oil is hot, add the beef and cook, stirring occasionally, until it is browned on all sides, about 8 minutes.
    4. Add the onion, carrots, celery, garlic, tomato paste, bay leaf, and thyme and cook, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are softened and the tomato paste has darkened, about 5 minutes.
    5. Add the beef broth and red wine, stir to combine, and bring to a boil.
    6. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer for 1 hour.
    7. Add the mushrooms, potato and butter and simmer, covered, for 30 minutes.
    8. Remove the lid and simmer for an additional 15 minutes.
    9. In a small bowl, mix together the butter and flour to form a paste.
    10. Add the paste to the stew and stir to combine. Simmer for 5 minutes.
    11. Remove the bay leaf, season to taste with salt and pepper, and stir in the parsley.
    12. Serve hot. Enjoy!
