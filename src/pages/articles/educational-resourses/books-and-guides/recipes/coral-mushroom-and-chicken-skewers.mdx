# Instructions

## Ingredients
    
    - 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    - 1 teaspoon ground ginger
    - 2 tablespoons honey
    - 1/2 pound fresh coral mushrooms, cleaned and cut into 1-inch pieces
    - 8-10 wooden skewers, soaked in water for at least 1 hour
    
    ## Instructions
    
    1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat (about 375Â° F).
    
    2. Cut chicken into 1-inch cubes and place in a large bowl.
    
    3. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, garlic, soy sauce, ginger, and honey. Pour over chicken and mix to coat.
    
    4. Place chicken cubes on skewers with mushrooms, alternating between each.
    
    5. Place skewers on the grill and cook for 8-10 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through, flipping occasionally.
    
    6. Serve hot. Enjoy!
