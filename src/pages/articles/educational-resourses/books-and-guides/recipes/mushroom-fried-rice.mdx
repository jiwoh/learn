# Mushroom Fried Rice
    
    Mushroom Fried Rice is a savory and flavorful dish of cooked rice mixed with fried mushrooms and other ingredients. It is believed to have originated in China, where it has been enjoyed for centuries. Today it is a popular dish in many countries due to its delicious flavor and versatility. It can be enjoyed as a main meal or as an accompaniment to other dishes.

# Recipe Overview

The Prep Time for Mushroom Fried Rice is 5 minutes, the Cooking Time is 15 minutes, the Skill Level is Easy and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0. To prepare Mushroom Fried Rice, you will need a cutting board, knife, spatula, large non-stick pan, and measuring spoons.

# Ingredients

## Mushroom Fried Rice Ingredients
    - 2 cups cooked white or brown rice
    - 2 tablespoons oil
    - 1 cup diced mushrooms
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1/2 cup frozen peas
    - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    - 2 eggs
    - 1/4 teaspoon pepper
    - 1/4 cup chopped emerald onion

# Instructions

## Mushroom Fried Rice Instructions
    1. Heat 1 tsp of sesame oil in a medium-sized pan over medium-high heat. 
    2. Add 1 cup of sliced mushrooms and cook until lightly browned. 
    3. Add 1/2 cup of diced onion, 1/2 cup of diced carrot and 1/2 cup of diced bell pepper. Saut√© until vegetables are softened.
    4. Add 2 cloves of minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.
    5. Add 3 cups of cooked rice, 1/4 cup of soy sauce, 1 tsp of white sugar and 1/2 tsp of ground black pepper. Stir to combine.
    6. Cook until heated through, about 5 minutes. 
    7. Serve with emerald onion on top. Enjoy!
