# Instructions

## Mushroom and Sausage Stuffed Mushrooms
    ### Ingredients: 
    - 12 medium mushrooms 
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil 
    - 1/2 cup diced onion 
    - 1/2 cup cooked and diced sausage 
    - 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese 
    - 1/4 cup seasoned bread crumbs
    - 2 tablespoons of butter 
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    
    ### Instructions: 
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a baking sheet with butter or cooking spray.
    
    2. Remove the stems from the mushrooms and place the caps on the baking sheet.
    
    3. Heat olive oil in a medium skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and sausage and cook until the onion is softened and the sausage is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
    
    4. Stir the cheese and bread crumbs into the sausage mixture. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    
    5. Divide the sausage mixture among the mushroom caps, pressing it down lightly.
    
    6. Bake for 20 minutes, or until the mushrooms are tender and the stuffing is golden brown.
    
    7. Serve warm. Enjoy!
