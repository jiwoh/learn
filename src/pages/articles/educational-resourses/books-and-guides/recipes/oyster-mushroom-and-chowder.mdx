# Instructions

## Oyster Mushroom and Chowder Recipe
    
    ### Ingredients 
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 6 ounces oyster mushrooms, roughly chopped
    - 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    - 2 cups vegetable broth
    - 1 cup heavy cream
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    
    ### Instructions
    1. Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
    Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
    
    2. Stir in the mushrooms and cook for 3 minutes, or until lightly browned.
    
    3. Sprinkle the flour over the mushrooms and stir until combined.
    
    4. Gradually add the broth and cream, stirring until smooth.
    
    5. Bring to a boil and reduce heat to low. 
    
    6. Simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    
    7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    
    8. Serve warm. Enjoy!
