# Mushroom and Pancetta Pasta
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom and pancetta pasta is a classic Italian dish that has been around for centuries. The dish can be traced back to the central regions of Italy, where pancetta and mushrooms were plentiful. The classic combination of hearty mushrooms, salty pancetta, butter, and fresh herbs has become a staple in Italian kitchens, especially those serving traditional rustic cuisine.

# Recipe Overview

*Mushroom and pancetta pasta has a prep time of 10 minutes, a cooking time of 30 minutes, a skill level of medium and a spice level Scoville rating of 5. To make this dish, you will need a large skillet, a pot, a cutting board and a knife.*

# Ingredients

## Mushroom and Pancetta Pasta Ingredients
    
    - 12 ounces of dried penne or other short pasta
    - 4 tablespoons of extra-virgin olive oil
    - 8 ounces of diced pancetta
    - 1 large onion, diced
    - 2 cloves of garlic, minced
    - 8 ounces of sliced fresh mushrooms
    - 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
    - 1/2 cup of dry white wine
    - 2 tablespoons of butter
    - 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
    - 2 tablespoons of freshly chopped parsley
    - Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

# Instructions

# Mushroom and Pancetta Pasta
    
    ## Ingredients
    - 500 g pasta of your choice 
    - 300 g sliced mushrooms 
    - 200 g pancetta cubes 
    - 100 ml cream 
    - 2 cloves garlic 
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil 
    - 2 tablespoons parsley, roughly chopped
    - Salt and pepper, to taste
    
    ## Instructions
    1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, then add the pasta and cook according to package instructions. 
    2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add olive oil. 
    3. Add garlic and pancetta and cook for about 5 minutes.
    4. Add mushrooms and cook for about 5 minutes more, stirring occasionally. 
    5. Reduce heat to low and add cream. Simmer for about 5 minutes.
    6. When the pasta is cooked, drain it and add it to the skillet. 
    7. Increase heat to medium and cook, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens and coats the pasta.
    8. Serve pasta with parsley and freshly ground black pepper. Enjoy!
