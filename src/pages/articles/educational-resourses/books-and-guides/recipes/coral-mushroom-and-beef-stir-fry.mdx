# Instructions

### Ingredients:
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 8 ounces beef, thinly sliced
    - 1 (8 ounce) can sliced coral mushrooms
    - 2 garlic cloves, minced
    - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    - 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
    - 1 teaspoon sesame oil
    - Salt, to taste
    
    ### Instructions:
    1. Heat the oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
    2. Add the beef to the wok and stir-fry for 2 minutes.
    3. Add the coral mushrooms and garlic to the wok, and cook until the mushrooms are tender, about 4 minutes.
    4. Pour in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Sprinkle with salt to taste.
    5. Cook for an additional 1 minute until the beef is cooked through.
    6. Serve warm.
