# Instructions

## Chanterelle Mushroom and Roasted Vegetable Salad 
    
    ### Ingredients: 
    - 1 cup chanterelle mushrooms
    - 3 cups sliced vegetables, such as carrots, sweet potatoes, and bell peppers
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - Salt and pepper, to taste
    - 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
    - 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
    
    ### Instructions:
    
    1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
    2. Place the vegetables on a baking sheet and drizzle with the olive oil. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
    3. Roast the vegetables for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.
    4. In the meantime, place the chanterelle mushrooms in a medium bowl and add the balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
    5. When the vegetables are done, add them to the chanterelles. Toss to combine.
    6. Place the salad on a serving platter and sprinkle with the fresh parsley. Serve.
