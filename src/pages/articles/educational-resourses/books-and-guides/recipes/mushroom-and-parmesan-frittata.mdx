# Mushroom and Parmesan Frittata
    
    ## Origin and History
    Mushroom and Parmesan Frittata is an Italian dish that is believed to have been served since the 1700s. It is made with eggs, mushrooms, and Parmesan cheese and is traditionally served for lunch or brunch. It is also a versatile dish, as it can be paired with salads or other proteins for a light dinner.

# Recipe Overview

    Mushroom and Parmesan Frittata has a Prep Time of 30 minutes, a Cooking Time of 40 minutes, a Skill Level of Easy, and a Spice Level of 0 on the Scoville rating. To prepare Mushroom and Parmesan Frittata you will need a large bowl, whisk, medium skillet, spatula, and medium pot.

# Ingredients

- 4 large eggs
    - 1/4 cup milk
    - 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
    - 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
    - 1/4 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 cups sliced mushrooms
    - 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    - 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives

# Instructions

# Mushroom and Parmesan Frittata Instructions
    
    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and lightly grease a 9-inch (23-cm) pie plate.
    
    2. Heat 1 tablespoon (15 ml) of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. When the oil is hot, add the mushrooms and cook, stirring occasionally, until they are golden brown and softened, about 8 minutes.
    
    3. Add the garlic and cook, stirring, until fragrant, about 1 minute. Remove the skillet from the heat and set it aside.
    
    4. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, black pepper, and salt until combined.
    
    5. Add the cooked mushrooms and garlic to the egg mixture and stir to combine.
    
    6. Pour the egg mixture into the greased pie plate and spread it out evenly.
    
    7. Place the pie plate in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes or until the frittata is golden brown and set in the middle.
    
    8. Remove the frittata from the oven and let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
