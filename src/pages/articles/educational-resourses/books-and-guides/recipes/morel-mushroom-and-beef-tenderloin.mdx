# Ingredients

## Ingredients
    - 4 beef tenderloin steaks
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 4 ounces morel mushrooms
    - 1/4 cup dry white wine
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    - 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

# Instructions

## Morel Mushroom and Beef Tenderloin Recipe
    
    1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
    
    2. Rub the beef tenderloin generously with sea salt and ground black pepper.
    
    3. Heat 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large oven-proof skillet over high heat.
    
    4. When the oil is hot, add the beef tenderloin to the skillet and cook until the meat starts to brown, about 2 minutes.
    
    5. Flip the beef over and add the morel mushrooms to the pan. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.
    
    6. Transfer the skillet to the oven and bake for 15 minutes or until cooked to desired doneness.
    
    7. Once cooked, remove the skillet from the oven and let the beef rest for 10 minutes before slicing into 1-inch thick slices.
    
    8. Serve the beef tenderloin and mushrooms with your favorite side dishes. Enjoy!
