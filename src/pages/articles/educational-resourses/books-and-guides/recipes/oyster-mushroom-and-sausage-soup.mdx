# Oyster Mushroom and Sausage Soup

It combines succulent, meaty sausage with the delicate, umami taste of oyster mushrooms to create a delicious and hearty soup. This classic dish has been enjoyed by many different cultures throughout its history, making it a classic comfort food favorite.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Oyster Mushroom and Sausage Soup is 20 minutes, the Cooking Time is 25 minutes, the Skill Level is Easy, and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0.
    For preparing Oyster Mushroom and Sausage Soup, you will need a cutting board, knife, frying pan, stock pot, wooden spoon and pepper mill.

# Ingredients

- 1 lb. smoked sausage, cut into 1-inch slices
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - 1 large onion, finely chopped
    - 4 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    - 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
    - 8 ounces oyster mushrooms, stems removed and caps cut into quarters
    - 4 cups vegetable broth
    - 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
    - 1/4 cup heavy cream
    - Salt and pepper to taste

# Instructions

## Oyster Mushroom and Sausage Soup Instructions
    1. Heat a large pot over medium-high heat.
    2. Add 1/2 cup of extra-virgin olive oil to the pot and let heat up.
    3. Add 1 pound of lean ground pork or ground turkey sausage and cook until sausage is cooked through.
    4. Once sausage is cooked through, add 1 large diced onion and cook until the onion is softened and lightly browned.
    5. Add 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika, 1 teaspoon of garlic powder, and 1 teaspoon of dried oregano.
    6. Stir to combine and then add 8 cups of chicken broth.
    7. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes.
    8. Add 12 ounces of sliced oyster mushrooms and 1/4 cup of freshly chopped parsley.
    9. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
    10. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.
    11. Serve the soup hot with crusty bread. Enjoy!
