# Mushroom Fettuccine Alfredo
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom Fettuccine Alfredo is an Italian-American dish that combines fettuccine noodles with a creamy Alfredo sauce and mushrooms. It was popularized in the early 1900s as a variation of Italian-American classics such as Alfredo and fettuccine. The dish was further popularized in the late 20th century by chefs and restaurants around the world.

# Recipe Overview

    Prep Time for Mushroom fettuccine Alfredo: 10 minutes
    Cooking Time for Mushroom fettuccine Alfredo: 30 minutes
    Skill Level for Mushroom fettuccine Alfredo: Moderate
    Spice Level Scoville rating for Mushroom fettuccine Alfredo: None
    
    To prepare Mushroom fettuccine Alfredo, you will need a large pot, a colander, a wooden spoon, a knife, a cutting board, and a large skillet.

# Ingredients

- 8 ounces fettuccine
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 8 ounces fresh mushrooms, thinly sliced
    - 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    - 2 tablespoons shredded Parmesan cheese
    - 1 cup chicken broth
    - 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

# Instructions

## Mushroom Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe
    
    1.	In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook 8 ounces of fettuccine noodles until they are al dente, about 10 minutes.
    
    2.	While the pasta cooks, heat 2 tablespoons of butter in a large heavy skillet over medium-high heat.
    
    3.	Once the butter has melted, add 1 pound of sliced mushrooms and cook them until they are lightly browned, stirring occasionally, about 5 minutes.
    
    4.	Add 1 teaspoon of minced garlic and cook for another minute.
    
    5.	Sprinkle 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour over the mushrooms and garlic and stir to combine.
    
    6.	Slowly add 2 cups of chicken broth, stirring constantly, and bring to a simmer.
    
    7.	Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for about 5 minutes, until the sauce has thickened.
    
    8.	Stir in 1/2 cup of heavy cream and 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese and continue to simmer for a few minutes.
    
    9.	Drain the cooked fettuccine and add it to the mushroom sauce.
    
    10. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    
    11. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve.
