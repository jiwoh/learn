# Mushroom and Cream Cheese Stuffed Chicken
    
    Originating from Italy, Mushroom and cream cheese stuffed chicken is a classic dish that has been served for centuries. The combination of mushrooms, cream cheese and chicken creates a savory and flavorful dish that is a crowd-pleaser. This dish is simple to make, yet elegant and delicious enough to serve guests.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Mushroom and cream cheese stuffed chicken is 15 minutes, the Cooking Time is 40 minutes and it requires a Moderate skill level. The Spice Level Scoville rating is 0. For this recipe, you will need a knife, cutting board, baking dish, aluminum foil, and mixing bowl.

# Ingredients

## Mushroom and cream cheese stuffed chicken Ingredients
    - 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
    - 12 ounces mushrooms
    - 2 cloves garlic, finely minced
    - 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
    - 1/2 cup heavy cream
    - 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
    - 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    - Salt and pepper, to taste
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil

# Instructions

## Mushroom and cream cheese stuffed chicken Instructions
     
    1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit. 
    2. Place 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    3. Mix together 1/4 cup of cream cheese, 1/4 cup of cooked mushrooms, 2 tablespoons of chopped red onion, and 2 tablespoons of minced garlic. 
    4. Spread the cream cheese mixture on top of each chicken breast. 
    5. Sprinkle 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese and 1/2 teaspoon of Italian seasoning on top of each chicken breast. 
    6. Bake in the preheated 375 degree Fahrenheit oven for 25 minutes. 
    7. Serve hot. Enjoy!
