# Instructions

## Mushroom Stew Instructions
    1. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large pot over a medium flame.
    2. Add 1 diced onion, 1 diced carrot, and 2 cloves of minced garlic to the pot. Cook until the vegetables are softened, stirring occasionally.
    3. Add 8 ounces of diced mushrooms, stirring to coat with the oil.
    4. Continue cooking until the mushrooms are softened and beginning to brown, about 5 minutes.
    5. Pour in 4 cups of vegetable broth and add 1 teaspoon of dried thyme, 1 bay leaf, and salt and pepper to taste.
    6. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer covered for 20 minutes.
    7. Remove the bay leaf and adjust the seasoning if necessary. 
    8. Serve the stew in bowls, with crusty bread or a side salad. Enjoy!
