# Mushroom, Onion, and Thyme Galette
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom, onion, and thyme galette is a savory French-inspired pastry dish made with pastry dough and a delicious filling of mushrooms, onions, and thyme. This classic French dish is perfect for a light lunch or dinner, or even as a side dish. It is believed to have been invented in the 19th century by the French chef Auguste Escoffier, who is widely considered the father of modern French cooking.

# Recipe Overview

The Prep Time for Mushroom, Onion, and Thyme Galette is 15 minutes, the Cooking Time is 40 minutes, the Skill Level is Moderate, and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0. To prepare Mushroom, Onion, and Thyme Galette, you would need a bowl, rolling pin, parchment paper, a cutting board, and a 9-inch tart pan.

# Ingredients

## Mushroom, Onion, and Thyme Galette Ingredients
    
    - 1 sheet all-butter puff pastry
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 8 ounces white or cremini mushrooms
    - 1/4 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    - 2 medium onions, halved and cut into 1/4 inch slices
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
    - 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
    - 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
    - 1 large egg
    - 2 tablespoons milk

# Instructions

## Mushroom, Onion, and Thyme Galette Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    
    2. Place puff pastry onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and set aside.
    
    3. In a large bowl, mix together the mushrooms, onion, thyme, garlic, salt and pepper.
    
    4. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg yolk and 2 teaspoons of water.
    
    5. Brush the puff pastry with the egg wash.
    
    6. Add the mushroom mixture onto the puff pastry, leaving a 1-inch border.
    
    7. Fold the edges of the puff pastry over the mushroom mixture.
    
    8. Brush the edges of the puff pastry with the remaining egg wash.
    
    9. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the edges of the puff pastry are golden brown.
    
    10. Let cool for 5 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
