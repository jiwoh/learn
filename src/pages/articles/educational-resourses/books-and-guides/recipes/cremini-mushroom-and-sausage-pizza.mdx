# Cremini Mushroom and Sausage Pizza
    
    ## Origin and History
    Cremini Mushroom and Sausage Pizza is a classic Italian dish that has been around for many years. This delicious combination of mushrooms, sausage, and cheese has become a favorite of pizza lovers all over the world. The combination of flavors and textures makes this pizza unique and delicious.

# Recipe Overview

The Prep Time for Cremini Mushroom and Sausage Pizza is 10 minutes, the Cooking Time is 15 minutes, and the Skill Level needed is Medium. The spice level has a Scoville rating of 5. To prepare this dish, you will need a cutting board, knife, large bowl, pizza dough, pizza sauce, Parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, cremini mushrooms, sausage, and a pizza stone.

# Ingredients

## Ingredients
    - 1 pizza dough
    - 1/4 cup of tomato sauce
    - 8 ounces of sausage, cooked and sliced
    - 1/2 cup of cremini mushrooms, sliced
    - 1/4 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
    - 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
    - 1/4 teaspoon of oregano
    - 1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder
    - 2 tablespoons of olive oil

# Instructions

## Cremini Mushroom and Sausage Pizza Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 425°F (218°C).
    
    2. Heat a medium-sized skillet over medium-high heat. Add 1 tablespoon of olive oil. When the oil is hot, add 8 ounces of sausage links, breaking them apart with a wooden spoon. Cook for 5-7 minutes, until the sausage is lightly browned and cooked through.
    
    3. Add 1/2 cup of chopped cremini mushrooms to the skillet. Cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the mushrooms have softened.
    
    4. Remove the skillet from the heat and allow it to cool for a few minutes.
    
    5. Spread 1/2 cup of pizza sauce over the surface of your favorite pre-made pizza crust.
    
    6. Top the pizza with the cooked sausage and mushrooms.
    
    7. Sprinkle 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese over the top.
    
    8. Bake in your preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
    
    9. Remove from the oven and allow to cool slightly before cutting into slices.
    
    10. Enjoy!
