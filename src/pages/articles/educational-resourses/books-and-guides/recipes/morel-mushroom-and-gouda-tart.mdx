# Morel Mushroom and Gouda Tart
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Morel Mushroom and Gouda Tart is a classic French pastry dish that has been enjoyed since the late 19th century. This recipe combines morels, a type of wild mushroom with a unique nutty flavor, with creamy Gouda cheese to create a unique and complex flavor. The combination of these two ingredients, along with the buttery tart shell, makes this a popular dish at dinner parties and family dinners alike.

# Recipe Overview

    **Prep Time:** 20 minutes 
    **Cooking Time:** 35 minutes 
    **Skill Level:** Beginner 
    **Spice Level Scoville Rating:** Not applicable 
    
    You will need a 9-inch tart pan, rolling pin, pastry brush, and a kitchen knife to prepare the Morel Mushroom and Gouda Tart.

# Ingredients

# Morel Mushroom and Gouda Tart Ingredients
    * 1 large sheet of puff pastry
    * 1/4 cup butter
    * 1/3 cup onion, minced
    * 1/2 lb morel mushrooms, cleaned and sliced
    * 1/2 cup white wine
    * 2 cloves garlic, minced
    * 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
    * 2 teaspoons fresh thyme, chopped
    * Salt and freshly ground pepper, to taste
    * 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
    * 4 ounces gouda cheese, grated
    * 2 tablespoons heavy cream

# Instructions

### Morel Mushroom and Gouda Tart Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
    2. Grease a 9-inch tart pan with butter.
    3. Place a wax paper circle on the bottom of the pan.
    4. In a food processor, pulse together 1/2 cup butter and 1/3 cup brown sugar until it is crumbly.
    5. Press mixture evenly across the bottom of the tart pan.
    6. Bake for 15 minutes, or until golden brown.
    7. Remove from oven and let cool.
    8. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat.
    9. Add 1/2 pound of morel mushrooms, chopped, and cook until they are softened and lightly browned.
    10. Add 3 cloves of garlic, finely chopped, and cook for 2 minutes.
    11. Add 1/2 cup of dry white wine and cook until most of the liquid has evaporated.
    12. Remove from heat and season with salt and pepper to taste.
    13. Spread mushroom mixture evenly on top of crust.
    14. Sprinkle 1/2 cup of grated Gouda cheese on top of mushrooms.
    15. Bake for 20 minutes, or until cheese is melted and golden brown.
    16. Let cool for 15 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
