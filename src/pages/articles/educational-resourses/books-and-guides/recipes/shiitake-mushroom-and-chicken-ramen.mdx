# Shiitake Mushroom and Chicken Ramen
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Shiitake Mushroom and Chicken Ramen is a popular dish from Japan, particularly from the region of Kyushu. It was originally created in the 1950s, and has since become a staple of ramen culture in Japan, and has even gained international popularity. Its flavors are a mix of shiitake mushrooms, chicken, and various vegetables all accompanied by a rich, umami-filled broth.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Shiitake Mushroom and Chicken Ramen is 15 minutes, the Cooking Time is 15 minutes, the Skill Level is Beginner and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0. To make the Shiitake Mushroom and Chicken Ramen, you will need a knife, cutting board, large pot, and measuring spoons.

# Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
    - 4 ounces shiitake mushrooms, stemmed and thinly sliced
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    - 4 cloves garlic, minced
    - 4 cups chicken broth
    - 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
    - 2 tablespoons sake (or dry Sherry) 
    - 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
    - 1 teaspoon white sugar
    - 2 bundles of ramen noodles
    - 2 cooked chicken breasts, shredded
    - 2 scallions, sliced
    - 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds

# Instructions

## Shiitake Mushroom and Chicken Ramen Instructions
    1. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large pot on medium-high heat. 
    2. Add 1 pound of sliced chicken breast and cook until lightly browned. 
    3. Add 2 cloves of minced garlic and cook for 1 minute. 
    4. Add 4 cups of chicken broth, 1 tablespoon of fish sauce, and 1 tablespoon of soy sauce to the pot. Bring to a boil. 
    5. Reduce heat to a simmer, and add 2 cups of sliced shiitake mushrooms. Simmer for 10 minutes. 
    6. Add 3-4 oz of ramen noodles to the pot and cook for 8-10 minutes, or until the noodles are tender. 
    7. Stir in 1/4 teaspoon of sesame oil and season to taste with salt and pepper. 
    8. Serve with sliced scallions, sesame-soy eggs, and chili oil, if desired. Enjoy!
