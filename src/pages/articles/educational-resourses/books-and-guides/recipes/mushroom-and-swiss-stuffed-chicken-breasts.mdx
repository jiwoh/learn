# Ingredients

## Mushroom and Swiss Stuffed Chicken Breasts Ingredients
    - 4 boneless and skinless chicken breasts
    - 2 teaspoons olive oil
    - 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
    - 1/2 cup baby bella mushrooms, diced
    - 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    - 1/4 cup onion, diced
    - 1/4 cup shredded Swiss cheese

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Swiss Stuffed Chicken Breasts Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F (177°C).
    
    2. In a medium pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
    
    3. Add mushrooms and cook for about 5 minutes until softened.
    
    4. Add garlic, salt, and pepper and cook for an additional minute.
    
    5. Remove from heat and set aside.
    
    6. Place chicken breasts in a baking dish.
    
    7. Stuff each chicken breast with the mushroom mixture.
    
    8. Top with Swiss cheese and bake for 25 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
    
    9. Serve chicken with your favorite sides and enjoy!
