## Mushroom and Onion Quiche Ingredients

    - 2 tablespoons butter 
    - 1/2 cup sliced onion

    - 3 eggs

# Instructions

&lt;h2&gt;Step 1: Gather Ingredients&lt;/h2&gt;
    
    -1 Pie crust
    -4 eggs
    -1/4 cup of heavy cream
    -1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
    -1/2 cup of shredded Swiss cheese
    -1 small onion, chopped
    -1/2 cup of sliced mushrooms
    -1/2 teaspoon of salt
    -1/2 teaspoon of black pepper 
    
    &lt;h2&gt;Step 2: Preheat oven to 375°F&lt;/h2&gt;
    
    &lt;h2&gt;Step 3: Sauté Onions and Mushrooms&lt;/h2&gt;
    
    -Heat a skillet on medium-high heat.
    -Add onion and mushrooms and a pinch of salt, and sauté for about 5 minutes, or until onions are translucent. 
    -Remove from heat and set aside. 
    
    &lt;h2&gt;Step 4: Prepare Quiche Filling&lt;/h2&gt;
    
    -In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs and cream until well blended. 
    -Stir in Parmesan cheese and Swiss cheese.
    -Add sautéed onions and mushrooms and mix well.
    -Season with salt and pepper
    
    &lt;h2&gt;Step 5: Assemble Quiche&lt;/h2&gt;
    
    -Place pie crust in a 9-inch pie plate.
    -Pour egg mixture into the crust.
    -Bake for 30-40 minutes until the center is set. 
    
    &lt;h2&gt;Step 6: Serve and Enjoy!&lt;/h2&gt;
