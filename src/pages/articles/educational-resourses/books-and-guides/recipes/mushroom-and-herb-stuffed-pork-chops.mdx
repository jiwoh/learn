# Recipe Overview

    The **Prep Time** for Mushroom and Herb-Stuffed Pork Chops is 20 minutes, the **Cooking Time** is 25 minutes, the **Skill Level** is Easy to Moderate, and the **Spice Level Scoville rating** is 0 to 1,000.  To prepare this dish, you will need a large skillet, a cheese grater, a mixing bowl, olive oil, salt, and pepper.

# Ingredients

- 6 pork chops
    - 1/2 cup fresh herbs, such as rosemary, thyme, and oregano, roughly chopped
    - 4 garlic cloves, minced
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1 onion, diced
    - 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
    - 1/2 cup dry white wine
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - 1/2 cup fresh breadcrumbs
    - 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    - Salt and pepper, to taste

# Instructions

&lt;h2&gt;Mushroom and Herb-Stuffed Pork Chops Instructions&lt;/h2&gt;
    
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
    
    2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
    
    3. Add 1/2 cup chopped onion and sauté for about 3 minutes.
    
    4. Add 8 ounces of sliced mushrooms and sauté for about 5 minutes.
    
    5. Add 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme and 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano.
    
    6. Cook for another 3 minutes and remove from heat.
    
    7. Add 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese and 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley to the mushroom mixture and mix to combine.
    
    8. Cut a pocket into each pork chop, being careful not to cut all the way through.
    
    9. Stuff each pocket with 1 tablespoon of the mushroom mixture.
    
    10. Place pork chops in a lightly greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
    
    11. Drizzle 1 tablespoon of olive oil over the pork chops.
    
    12. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, to taste.
    
    13. Bake in preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until pork chops are cooked through.
    
    14. Serve warm. Enjoy!
