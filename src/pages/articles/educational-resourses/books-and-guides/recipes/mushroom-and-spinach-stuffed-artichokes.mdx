# Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Artichokes
    
    Artichoke stuffed with mushrooms and spinach is a delicious and nutritious meal. This classic Italian dish is an easy and flavorful way to enjoy artichokes.

    Mushroom and spinach stuffed artichokes first appeared in the Italian culinary lexicon during the 15th century. This classic Italian dish has remained popular throughout the generations, and is still a popular and delicious meal today.

# Recipe Overview

    Prep Time for Mushroom and spinach stuffed artichokes: 30 minutes  
    Cooking Time for Mushroom and spinach stuffed artichokes: 30-35 minutes  
    Skill Level for Mushroom and spinach stuffed artichokes: Beginner-Intermediate  
    Spice Level Scoville rating for Mushroom and spinach stuffed artichokes: Low  
    
    To prepare Mushroom and spinach stuffed artichokes, you will need a knife, cutting board, bowl, spoon, measuring cups and spoons, medium saucepan, and a baking dish.

# Ingredients

- 4 medium artichokes
    - 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
    - 5 cloves garlic, minced
    - 8 ounces mushrooms, chopped
    - 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
    - 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
    - 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    - 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
    - 2 cups baby spinach
    - 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
    - 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Artichokes Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
    
    2. Cut off the top ½-inch of artichoke and trim bottom stems. Peel off the tough outer leaves and discard.
    
    3. Place artichokes in a steamer basket, standing upright. Steam for 15-20 minutes or until leaves are tender.
    
    4. Meanwhile, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add mushrooms and season with salt and pepper. Sauté until mushrooms have softened and released most of their juices.
    
    5. Add spinach and garlic; stir to combine. Cook until spinach is wilted and garlic is fragrant. Remove from heat.
    
    6. In a large bowl, combine cooked mushroom mixture with breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, lemon juice, and olive oil. Mix until well combined.
    
    7. Stuff each artichoke with the filling, then place in a greased baking dish.
    
    8. Place baking dish in preheated oven. Bake for 25 minutes or until tops are golden brown.
    
    9. Serve warm. Enjoy!
