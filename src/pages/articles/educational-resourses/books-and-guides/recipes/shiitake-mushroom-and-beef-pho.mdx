# Shiitake Mushroom and Beef Pho
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Shiitake Mushroom and Beef Pho is an adaptation of the Vietnamese classic national dish, Pho, which originated in Northern Vietnam. This version of the classic dish combines Shiitake mushrooms with beef broth to create a rich, savory flavor. It is often served in the winter as a warming, comforting dish and has become a staple culinary delight for many in Vietnam.

# Ingredients

## Shiitake Mushroom and Beef Pho Ingredients
    - 8 ounces sirloin steak, thinly sliced
    - 1/4 cup soy sauce
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 1 onion, diced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 8 cups beef stock
    - 1 tablespoon fish sauce
    - 2 tablespoons brown sugar
    - 2 tablespoons fresh grated ginger
    - 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
    - 4 ounces shiitake mushrooms, stemmed and thinly sliced
    - 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
    - 4 ounces vermicelli noodles
    - 2 tablespoons fresh chopped cilantro, for garnish
    - 2 tablespoons fresh chopped basil, for garnish
    - 2 tablespoons fresh chopped mint, for garnish
    - 2 limes, cut into wedges, for garnish

# Instructions

## Shiitake Mushroom and Beef Pho Instructions
    
    1. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
    
    2. Add 1 pound of thinly sliced beef, such as flank or sirloin, to the pot and sauté for 5 minutes, until the beef is cooked through.
    
    3. Add 6 cups of beef broth to the pot, along with 2 tablespoons of fish sauce, 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 2 tablespoons of brown sugar, 1 tablespoon of minced garlic, and 2 teaspoons of ground coriander.
    
    4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 15 minutes.
    
    5. Add 8 ounces of sliced shiitake mushrooms to the pot, along with 2 tablespoons of freshly squeezed lime juice. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
    
    6. Taste the broth and adjust the seasoning, adding more fish sauce, soy sauce, or sugar as needed.
    
    7. To serve, divide 8 ounces of cooked rice noodles into 4 bowl. Ladle the broth over the noodles, then top each bowl with the cooked beef and mushrooms.
    
    8. Garnish with fresh cilantro, jalapeño, bean sprouts, and lime wedges, if desired. Enjoy!
