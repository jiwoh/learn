# Instructions

## Chicken of the Woods Mushroom and Shrimp Fried Rice 
    ### Ingredients
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 1/2 pound peeled and deveined raw shrimp
    - 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    - 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
    - 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
    - 1/4 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon pepper
    - 3 cups cooked white rice
    - 1 cup diced chicken of the woods mushrooms
    - 1/2 cup diced carrots
    - 1/2 cup frozen peas
    - 4 beaten eggs
    - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    - 2 tablespoons sesame oil
    
    ### Instructions
    1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
    2. Add the vegetable oil and let it heat for 1 minute.
    3. Add the shrimp, garlic powder, ginger, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 3 minutes or until the shrimp is cooked through.
    4. Reduce the heat to medium and add the cooked rice, chicken of the woods mushrooms, carrots, and frozen peas. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 4 minutes.
    5. Push the rice and vegetables to the side of the pan.
    6. Add the eggs and cook, stirring often, for 2 minutes or until the eggs have set.
    7. Mix the eggs into the rice and vegetables.
    8. Add the soy sauce and sesame oil and stir to combine.
    9. Cook for an additional 2 minutes or until everything is heated through.
    10. Serve warm. Enjoy!
