# Lions Mane Mushroom and Broccoli Quiche
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Lions Mane Mushroom and Broccoli Quiche is a classic French bistro dish that dates back to the 1960s. Originating in Paris, this quiche combines earthy flavors of the Lions Mane mushroom with the crunchy texture of broccoli to create a perfect balance of flavors. Over the years, this dish has become a popular main course option due to its versatility, flavor and ease of preparation.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Lions Mane Mushroom and Broccoli Quiche is 15 minutes, Cooking Time for Lions Mane Mushroom and Broccoli Quiche is 1 hour and 20 minutes, Skill Level for Lions Mane Mushroom and Broccoli Quiche is Intermediate, and Spice Level Scoville rating for Lions Mane Mushroom and Broccoli Quiche is 0. To prepare it, you'll need a 9-inch pie plate, a medium-size bowl, a cutting board, a knife, and a large sauté pan.

# Ingredients

### Ingredients:
    - 2 cups broccoli florets
    - 3 tablespoons olive oil 
    - 1 small onion, finely chopped 
    - 3 cloves garlic, minced 
    - 2 cups sliced lions mane mushrooms 
    - 4 large eggs 
    - 1 cup skim milk 
    - 1 teaspoon salt 
    - ¼ teaspoon black pepper 
    - 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese 
    - ½ cup all-purpose flour 
    - 2 tablespoons butter, melted

# Instructions

## Lions Mane Mushroom and Broccoli Quiche Instructions
    
    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    
    2. Grease a 9-inch pie plate with butter.
    
    3. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt and pepper. Set aside.
    
    4. In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
    
    5. Add the mushrooms, broccoli, and onion and sauté until all the vegetables are tender, about 5-7 minutes.
    
    6. Spread the vegetables in the prepared pie plate, then pour the egg mixture over them.
    
    7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the quiche is golden brown and a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
    
    8. Let cool slightly before serving. Enjoy!
