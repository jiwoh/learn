## Oyster Mushroom and Broccoli Stir-fry
    
    ### Ingredients
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 tablespoons minced ginger
    - 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
    - 1/4 cup soy sauce
    - 1/4 cup dry white wine
    - 2 tablespoons honey
    - 2 cups oyster mushrooms, sliced
    - 2 cups broccoli florets
    - 2 tablespoons sesame oil
    - Salt, to taste
    
    ### Instructions
    1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. 
    2. Add the garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes (optional) and stir-fry for 1 minute.
    3. Add the soy sauce, white wine, and honey and cook for 1 minute more.
    4. Add the mushrooms and cook, stirring occasionally, for 3 minutes.
    5. Add the broccoli and cook, stirring occasionally, for 3 minutes more.
    6. Add the sesame oil and stir-fry for 1 minute longer.
    7. Season with salt, to taste, and serve immediately.
