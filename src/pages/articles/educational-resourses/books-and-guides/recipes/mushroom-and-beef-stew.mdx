# Mushroom and Beef Stew 
    
    ## Ingredients
    - 1 tablespoon olive oil
    - 1 pound stew beef (cut into cubes)
    - 1 teaspoon salt
    - 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    - 1/2 teaspoon pepper
    - 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
    - 6 cups beef broth
    - 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
    - 1 large onion (chopped)
    - 3 carrots (peeled and sliced)
    - 2 celery stalks (sliced)
    - 1 tablespoon tomato paste
    - 2 cloves garlic (minced)
    - 1 tablespoon fresh thyme (chopped)
    - 1/2 pound button mushrooms (sliced)
    - 2 tablespoons cornstarch
    - 2 tablespoons cold water
    
    ## Instructions
    
    1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
    
    2. Add beef to the pot and season with salt, garlic powder, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Stir to combine and sauté for about 7 minutes until beef is browned.
    
    3. Add beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, onion, carrots, celery, tomato paste, garlic, and thyme. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 30 minutes.
    
    4. Uncover pot and stir in mushrooms. Simmer for an additional 15 minutes.
    
    5. In a small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and cold water until smooth.
    
    6. Slowly stir cornstarch mixture into the stew and let it simmer for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the stew has thickened. 
    
    7. Serve hot with your favorite sides. Enjoy!
