## Porcini Mushroom and Fontina Tart
    
    ### Ingredients 
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 shallots, finely chopped
    - 2 garlic cloves, minced
    - 1/2 pound fresh porcini mushrooms, sliced
    - 1/2 cup dry white wine 
    - Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper 
    - 1/2 cup Fontina cheese, grated
    - 1/2 cup ricotta cheese 
    - 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano 
    - 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme 
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley 
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano 
    - 1 (9-inch) pre-made refrigerated pie crust
    
    ### Instructions 
    1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
    2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. 
    3. Add shallots and garlic, and cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes. 
    4. Add mushrooms, and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. 
    5. Increase heat to medium-high, and add wine. Cook until liquid is reduced and mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste. 
    6. Transfer to a medium bowl and cool.
    7. Add Fontina, ricotta, oregano, thyme, and parsley to the cooled mushroom mixture and stir to combine. 
    8. Unroll pie crust onto a greased 9-inch pie plate. 
    9. Pour mushroom mixture into the crust, and spread evenly. 
    10. Sprinkle with chopped oregano and bake for 30 minutes, until crust is golden and filling is bubbly. 
    11. Let cool before serving. Enjoy!
