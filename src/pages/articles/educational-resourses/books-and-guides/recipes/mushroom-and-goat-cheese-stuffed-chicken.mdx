# Instructions

## Mushroom and Goat Cheese Stuffed Chicken 
    
    ###Ingredients:
    - 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    - 4 ounces goat cheese
    - 4 ounces mushrooms, chopped
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 tablespoons garlic, minced
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - Salt and pepper, to taste
    
    ###Instructions:
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
    2. Butterfly the chicken breasts and rub with salt, pepper and garlic.
    3. Sauté mushrooms in olive oil for about 3 minutes over medium heat until softened.
    4. Add garlic, 1 tablespoon butter and salt and pepper to the mushrooms and sauté for another minute.
    5. Remove the mushroom mixture from heat and let cool for a few minutes.
    6. Place the goat cheese on the chicken breasts.
    7. Spread the mushroom mixture on top of the goat cheese.
    8. Roll up the chicken breasts and secure with toothpicks or string.
    9. Grease a baking dish with remaining tablespoon of butter.
    10. Place chicken breasts in the baking dish and bake for 25 to 30 minutes until chicken is cooked through and golden brown.
    11. Serve and enjoy!
