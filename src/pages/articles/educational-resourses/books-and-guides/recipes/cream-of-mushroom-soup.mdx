# Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter 
    - 2 small onions, chopped 
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced 
    - 2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed cream of mushroom soup 
    - 2 soup cans water 
    - 2 cups sliced mushrooms 
    - 1/2 teaspoon white sugar 
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley 
    - 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper 
    - 1/4 cup all-purpose flour 
    - 1 cup half-and-half cream 
    - 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

# Instructions

# Cream of Mushroom Soup Instructions
    
    1. Heat a large pot over medium heat and add 2 tablespoons of butter. 
    2. Once the butter has melted, add 1/2 cup of diced onion and 1/2 cup of diced celery. Saute until the vegetables are softened, about 3-4 minutes.
    3. Add 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder, 1/2 teaspoon of dried thyme, and 1/4 teaspoon of pepper. Saute for 1 minute more. 
    4. Add 8 ounces of sliced mushrooms and saute until softened, about 4-5 minutes. 
    5. Add 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth and stir to combine. 
    6. Bring the broth to a boil, reduce the heat to low, and simmer for 15 minutes. 
    7. Add 1/2 cup of heavy cream to the soup and stir to combine. Simmer for 5 minutes more. 
    8. Taste the soup and adjust the seasonings, if necessary.
    9. Serve warm with crusty bread. Enjoy!
