# Chicken of the Woods Mushroom and Tofu Stir-fry

Chicken of the Woods mushrooms, also known as Laetiporus, have long been a key component of the dish, providing an earthy and nutty flavor. # Ingredients

**Ingredients:**
    
    - 2 tablespoons of oil 
    - 2 cloves of garlic, minced
    - 1inch ginger, grated
    - 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
    - 1 teaspoon of toasted sesame oil
    - 2 tablespoons of rice wine vinegar
    - 1/3 cup of vegetable broth 
    - 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
    - 8 ounces of firm tofu, cubed
    - 8 ounces of Chicken of the Woods mushrooms, chopped
    - 2 cups of baby spinach leaves
    - 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh cilantro
    - Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

# Instructions

# Chicken of the Woods Mushroom and Tofu Stir-fry Instructions
    1. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
    2. Once the oil is hot, add 1/2 teaspoon of minced garlic and stir for 20-30 seconds.
    3. Add 8 ounces of cubed firm tofu to the skillet and stir for 3 minutes.
    4. Add 1 cup of cleaned and sliced Chicken of the Woods mushrooms to the skillet and stir for an additional 3 minutes.
    5. Add 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar, 1 teaspoon of sugar, 1 teaspoon of sesame oil, 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes, and 1/4 teaspoon of ground ginger to the skillet. Stir until the spices are evenly distributed.
    6. Once the ingredients are combined, reduce heat to medium and cook for an additional 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    7. Serve hot over cooked white or brown rice. Enjoy!
