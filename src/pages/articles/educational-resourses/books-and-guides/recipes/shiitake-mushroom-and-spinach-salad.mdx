    **Cooking Time:** 5 minutes

# Ingredients

- 2 Tbsp olive oil
    - 2 tsp minced garlic
    - 2 cups Shiitake mushrooms, sliced
    - 1/4 cup sweet onion, diced
    - 1/2 tsp dried thyme
    - 1/4 tsp salt
    - 2 cups baby spinach
    - 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
    - 2 Tbsp balsamic vinegar

# Instructions

## Shiitake Mushroom and Spinach Salad Instructions
    1. Gather the ingredients: 
        * 2/3 cup cooked quinoa
        * 8 ounces baby spinach
        * 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
        * 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
        * 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
        * 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
        * 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
        * 4 ounces shiitake mushrooms, sliced
        * 1/3 cup thinly sliced red onion
    
    2. Prepare the quinoa according to package directions.
    
    3. In a large bowl, combine the spinach, oil, vinegar, mustard, salt, and pepper.
    
    4. Add the cooked quinoa and mix to combine.
    
    5. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
    
    6. Add the mushrooms and cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 5 minutes.
    
    7. Add the cooked mushrooms and red onion to the quinoa and spinach mixture.
    
    8. Toss to combine, and serve.
