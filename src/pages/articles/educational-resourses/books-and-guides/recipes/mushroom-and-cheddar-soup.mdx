# Instructions

## Mushroom and Cheddar Soup Instructions
    1. Heat 2 tablespoons of butter in a large pot over medium heat.
    2. Add 2 diced onions and 2 cloves of minced garlic, stirring occasionally. 
    3. When the onions are soft and beginning to caramelize, add 8 ounces of sliced mushrooms.
    4. Cook until the mushrooms are lightly browned, about 8 minutes.
    5. Pour 5 cups of chicken broth into the pot and bring to a boil.
    6. Reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
    7. Stir in 1/2 cup of heavy cream and bring back to a simmer.
    8. Add 1 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce, 1/2 teaspoon of minced fresh thyme, 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper, and 1/4 teaspoon of kosher salt.
    9. Simmer for 15 minutes.
    10. Turn off the heat, add 2 1/2 cups of shredded Cheddar cheese, and stir until the cheese is fully melted.
    11. Serve hot with crusty bread or croutons. Enjoy!
