# Lions Mane Mushroom and Egg Salad
    
    Lions Mane Mushroom and Egg Salad is a light and tasty dish that combines the nutritious benefits of mushrooms and eggs. This delicious salad is popular in many regions, including Russia, China, Japan, and the United States. The dish has been enjoyed for centuries, with its origins believed to date back to the Middle Ages.

# Recipe Overview

    **Prep Time:** 15 minutes
    
    **Cooking Time:** 10 minutes
    
    **Skill Level:** Intermediate
    
    **Spice Level Scoville rating:** None
    
    You will need a knife, a cutting board, a plate, a bowl, a mixing spoon, and a pan to prepare Lions Mane Mushroom and Egg Salad.

# Ingredients

- Lions Mane mushrooms, finely chopped 
    - 4 Hard boiled eggs, chopped 
    - 2 tablespoons mayonnaise 
    - 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard 
    - 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice 
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley 
    - Salt and freshly ground pepper, to taste 
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil 
    - 2 tablespoons chopped chives 
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill

# Instructions

## Lions Mane Mushroom and Egg Salad Instructions 
    1. In a medium saucepan, add 3-4 cups of water. Bring the water to a boil and add 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
    2. Once the water is boiling, add 3 eggs to the pot and boil them for 8-10 minutes. 
    3. Remove the boiled eggs from the heat and place them in a cold water bath for 5 minutes.
    4. Peel the eggs and dice them. Place the diced eggs in a bowl and set aside. 
    5. Peel, rinse, and mince 1/4 of a medium onion. Place the minced onion in a separate bowl and set aside. 
    6. In a medium skillet, heat up 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. 
    7. When the oil is hot, add the diced onion. Sauté the onion until it is softened and fragrant, about 2-3 minutes.
    8. Add 1/2 cup of lion's mane mushroom pieces to the skillet and sauté with the onion for an additional 4-5 minutes until the mushrooms are cooked through. 
    9. Add the sautéed onion and mushroom to the bowl of diced eggs and stir together until evenly combined.
    10. To the bowl, add 1/4 cup of mayonnaise, 1 tablespoon of sour cream, 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Mix together until the salad is creamy. 
    11. Serve the salad on a bed of lettuce leaves. Enjoy!
