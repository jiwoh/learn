# Instructions

## Shiitake Mushroom and Bok Choy Soup
    
    ### Ingredients:
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 4 cloves garlic, finely minced
    - 1 teaspoon freshly grated ginger
    - 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
    - 1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper
    - 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
    - 5 cups low-sodium vegetable broth
    - 1 cup shiitake mushrooms, thinly sliced
    - 1 cup bok choy, thinly sliced
    - 2 tablespoons freshly chopped cilantro
    - Salt to taste
    
    ###Instructions:
    
    1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
    
    2. Add garlic, ginger, red pepper flakes, and white pepper. Cook for 1 minute, stirring continuously.
    
    3. Add soy sauce, vegetable broth, shiitake mushrooms and bok choy. Bring to a boil.
    
    4. Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
    
    5. Add cilantro and season with salt to taste.
    
    6. Serve hot and enjoy!
