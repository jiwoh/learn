# Instructions

## Mushroom and Spinach Omelet 
    
    ### Ingredients 
    - 4 large eggs 
    - 2 teaspoons olive oil 
    - 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms 
    - 1/2 cup spinach leaves 
    - 2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese 
    - Salt and pepper to taste 
    
    ### Instructions 
    1. Crack the eggs into a medium size bowl and whisk together lightly.
    2. Heat oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
    3. Add mushrooms and cook for 1-2 minutes or until lightly browned.
    4. Add spinach and season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until spinach is wilted.
    5. Pour the egg mixture over the mushroom and spinach mixture.
    6. Cook for about 2 minutes and then sprinkle parmesan cheese over the top.
    7. Cover skillet and cook for about 3 minutes until eggs are cooked through.
    8. Carefully fold the omelet and serve.
