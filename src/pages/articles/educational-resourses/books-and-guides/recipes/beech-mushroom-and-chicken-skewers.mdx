# Recipe Overview

    This Beech Mushroom and Chicken Skewers recipe has a prep time of 15 minutes, a cooking time of 15 minutes, and a skill level of intermediate. The spice level is a Scoville rating of medium-hot. Utensils needed for preparing this recipe include skewers, a grill, a grill brush, and a large bowl.

# Ingredients

- 2 chicken breasts
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    - 1 teaspoon dried thyme
    - 1 teaspoon sweet paprika
    - 2 cups beech mushrooms
    - 2 bell peppers
    - 2 red onions
    - Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

# Instructions

## Beech Mushroom and Chicken Skewers Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F. 
    2. In a large bowl combine ¾ cup of honey, 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 2 teaspoons of sesame oil and the juice of 1 lime.
    Add 1 teaspoon of minced garlic and ½ teaspoon of red pepper flakes.
    Mix all the ingredients until evenly combined.
    3. Place chicken breasts in a medium-sized bowl and pour the marinade over the chicken.
    Mix to evenly coat chicken with the marinade and let sit for 15-20 minutes.
    4. Soak 10-12 wooden skewers in water.
    5. Cut beech mushrooms into halves or quarters, depending on size.
    6. Thread chicken and mushrooms onto the skewers.
    7. Place the skewers on a parchment-covered baking sheet.
    8. Bake in preheated oven for 15-18 minutes, turning once halfway through baking.
    9. Serve warm and enjoy!
