# Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Flank Steak
    
    Mushroom and spinach stuffed flank steak is a hearty, flavorful dish that's sure to please. Flank steak is stuffed with sautéed mushrooms, spinach, garlic, and cheese, then rolled, tied, and seared. The result is a juicy, tender main course that can be served with your favorite side dishes.
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Although the exact origin of this dish is unknown, stuffed flank steak is believed to have originated in Europe during the Middle Ages. Over the centuries, various ingredients were added to the stuffing to enhance the flavor, such as mushrooms and spinach. Today, it is a popular dish that is enjoyed in many countries around the world.

# Recipe Overview

**Prep Time**: 15 minutes  
    **Cooking Time**: 60 minutes  
    **Skill Level**: Intermediate  
    **Spice Level**: 0  
    
    To make this delicious Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Flank Steak you will need a sharp knife, cutting board, baking sheet, bowl, and aluminum foil.

# Ingredients

* Flank steak
    * 8 oz. baby bella mushrooms
    * 2 cloves garlic, minced
    * 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
    * 1/2 cup chopped onion
    * 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    * 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    * 4 cups baby spinach
    * 1/3 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
    * 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    * 1/4 cup Panko breadcrumbs

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Spinach Stuffed Flank Steak Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
    
    2. Place flank steak between two pieces of plastic wrap. Using a meat mallet, pound steak until about 1/4-inch thick.
    
    3. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add mushrooms, spinach, garlic, and onion, and sauté until the vegetables are softened and lightly browned, about 7-8 minutes.
    
    4. Remove from heat and stir in feta.
    
    5. Spread vegetable and feta filling over steak. Roll steak up and secure with kitchen twine.
    
    6. Heat the remaining tablespoon of oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Add steak roll and sear on all sides until lightly browned, about 4 minutes.
    
    7. Transfer skillet to preheated oven. Bake until steak is cooked through, about 20 minutes.
    
    8. Remove from oven and let rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
    
    9. Serve with your favorite sides and enjoy!
