# Mushroom and Parmesan Polenta 
    
    ## Ingredients 
    * 3 tablespoons olive oil 
    * 1 onion, chopped 
    * 1 garlic clove, minced 
    * 8 oz mushrooms, sliced 
    * 1 teaspoon dried thyme 
    * 2 cups vegetable broth 
    * 1 cup polenta 
    * 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated 
    * Salt and pepper to taste 
    
    ## Instructions 
    1. Heat olive oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. 
    2. Add onion and garlic to the pan and cook, stirring frequently, until softened. 
    3. Add mushrooms and thyme and cook for an additional 7-10 minutes. 
    4. Reduce heat to low and add vegetable broth, stirring to combine with the vegetables. 
    5. Stir polenta into the mixture and increase heat to medium-high. 
    6. Cook for 8-10 minutes, stirring often, until polenta is cooked through. 
    7. Stir Parmesan cheese into the polenta, stirring to combine. 
    8. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
    9. Serve warm.
