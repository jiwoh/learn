# Morel Mushroom and Crab Cakes
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Morel Mushroom and Crab Cakes originated in the Chesapeake Bay region of the United States in the mid-1800s. This classic dish has been served as a meal or appetizer for over a century. It consists of a patty made with a mixture of finely chopped morel mushrooms, jumbo lump crabmeat and seasonings, then lightly fried and served with a creamy remoulade sauce. This indulgent treat is now popular throughout the United States and is a favorite of seafood-lovers everywhere.
