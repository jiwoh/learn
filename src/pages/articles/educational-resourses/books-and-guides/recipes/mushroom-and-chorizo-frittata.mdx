# Ingredients

## Mushroom and chorizo frittata Ingredients
    - 1 tablespoon olive oil
    - 2 chorizo sausages, diced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 175g mushrooms, trimmed and sliced
    - 4 eggs, lightly beaten
    - 2 tablespoons chopped chives
    - 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
    - Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Chorizo Frittata Instructions
    
    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    3. Add the chorizo and cook until lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
    4. Add the mushrooms and cook until golden brown, about 5 minutes more.
    5. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish.
    6. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, cream, salt, and pepper until combined.
    7. Pour the egg mixture over the chorizo and mushrooms in the baking dish.
    8. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes until the center is set and the top is golden brown.
    9. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
