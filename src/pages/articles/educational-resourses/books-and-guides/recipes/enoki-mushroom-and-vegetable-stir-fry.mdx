# Enoki Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Enoki Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry is a popular dish in Japanese cuisine. The combination of enoki mushrooms, vegetables, and a variety of ingredients such as soy sauce, sugar, and sake is traditional and delicious. This particular dish began to gain popularity in the late 20th century, and has since become a familiar sight on Japanese dining tables.

# Recipe Overview

    The prep time for Enoki Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry is 10 minutes, the cooking time is 15 minutes, the skill level is easy, and the spice level Scoville rating is not applicable. To prepare Enoki Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry, you will need a wok, cutting board, knife, and cooking spoon.

# Ingredients

**Ingredients:**
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 
    - 4 ounces enoki mushrooms
    - 1 cup red bell pepper, thinly sliced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 4 ounces snow peas, sliced lengthwise
    - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    - 1 teaspoon sesame oil
    - 1 teaspoon corn starch
    - 2 teaspoons honey
    - Red pepper flakes, to taste

# Instructions

1. Start by preparing the ingredients:
      - 2 cloves garlic, minced
      - 2 tablespoons freshly grated ginger
      - 2 tablespoons vegetable or canola oil
      - 1 bunch enoki mushrooms, cut into 1-inch pieces
      - 1 red bell pepper, cut into thin strips
      - 1 cup fresh snow peas, trimmed and cut in half
      - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
      - 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
      - 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds
      - Salt and pepper to taste
    
    2. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat and add the oil. 
    
    3. Add the garlic and ginger and stir-fry for 1 minute. 
    
    4. Add the mushrooms, bell pepper, and snow peas and continue to stir-fry for 4 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.
    
    5. Add the soy sauce, sesame oil, and sesame seeds and cook for an additional minute. 
    
    6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    
    7. Serve hot. Enjoy!
