# Instructions

## King Trumpet Mushroom and Vegetable Skewers
    
    ### Ingredients:
    - 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1/4 cup thinly sliced fresh basil
    - 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    - Salt and freshly ground black pepper
    - 8 King Trumpet mushrooms, cut in 3/4 inch slices
    - 1 red bell pepper, cut into 1 inch pieces
    - 1 orange bell pepper, cut into 1 inch pieces
    - 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into 1 inch pieces
    - 1 red onion, cut into 1 inch pieces
    - 4 skewers
    
    ### Instructions:
    1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, garlic, basil, oregano, salt and pepper.
    2. Add the mushrooms, bell peppers and onion and stir to combine.
    3. Marinate vegetables in the refrigerator for 1 to 2 hours.
    4. Preheat an outdoor grill to medium-high heat.
    5. Place the marinated vegetables on the skewers.
    6. Grill the mushrooms and vegetables for 4 to 5 minutes, or until tender, turning the skewers occasionally.
    7. Serve hot with your favorite sauce. Enjoy!
