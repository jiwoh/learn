# Chanterelle Mushroom and Pesto Pasta
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Chanterelle mushrooms have been used in cooking since ancient times. They have a unique flavor and are prized for their delicate aroma. Pesto is an Italian sauce made from basil, garlic, olive oil, and other ingredients. Pesto and chanterelle mushroom pasta is an easy yet delicious dish that is perfect for any occasion.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Chanterelle Mushroom and Pesto Pasta is 10 minutes, the Cooking Time is 25 minutes, the Skill Level is Intermediate, and the Spice Level Scoville rating is mild. To prepare this dish, you will need a skillet, a pot, an oven, a strainer, a cutting board, a knife and a spoon.

# Ingredients

- 12 oz dry pasta
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1 pound chanterelle mushrooms, cleaned and sliced
    - 1/2 cup pesto
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - Salt and pepper to taste

# Instructions

## Chanterelle Mushroom and Pesto Pasta Instructions
    
    1. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    2. Add 1 pound of Chanterelle mushrooms and cook until they are golden brown and fragrant, about 8 minutes.
    3. Remove the mushrooms and set aside.
    4. Lower the heat to medium and add 3 cloves of minced garlic and 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes to the pan.
    5. Cook until the garlic is fragrant, about 1 minute.
    6. Add the cooked mushrooms back in to the pan and stir to combine.
    7. Add 1/2 cup of white wine and bring the mixture to a boil.
    8. Reduce the heat to low and simmer until the wine has reduced by half.
    9. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    10. Add 1/2 pound of dry spaghetti and cook until al dente, about 8 minutes.
    11. Drain the pasta and set aside.
    12. Once the wine has reduced, stir in 1/4 cup of pesto and 1/4 teaspoon of salt.
    13. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and stir to combine.
    14. Serve the pasta with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and freshly cracked black pepper, if desired. Enjoy!
