# Ingredients

* 2 tablespoons olive oil
    * 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
    * 1/2 cup minced mushrooms
    * 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh herbs (such as rosemary, sage, thyme and oregano)
    * 1 teaspoon sea salt
    * freshly ground black pepper, to taste
    * 1 1/2 pounds pork tenderloin
    * 3/4 cup white wine
    * 2 tablespoons butter
    * 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    * 1/2 cup half and half or cream

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Herb Stuffed Pork Tenderloin Instructions
    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
    2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    3. Add 1 tablespoon oil, then add mushrooms and chopped onion.
    4. Sauté mushrooms and onions for about 5 minutes or until softened.
    5. Remove from heat and transfer to a bowl.
    6. Add the chopped herbs, breadcrumbs, feta cheese, and salt and pepper to the mushroom mixture and mix until combined.
    7. Cut a slit in the middle of the pork tenderloin, then stuff the mushroom mixture inside.
    8. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add 1 tablespoon oil.
    9. Place the stuffed pork tenderloin in the skillet and sear on both sides for 2 minutes.
    10. Transfer the pork to an oven-proof dish and bake for 25 minutes or until the pork is cooked through.
    11. Remove the pork from the oven and let it rest for 5 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
