    Mushroom and artichoke dip is a classic appetizer that is believed to have developed in Italy during the late 1800s. The dish is believed to have originated as a way to use leftovers to create a unique and delicious starter. # Recipe Overview

**Prep Time: 15 minutes**
    
    **Cooking Time: 25 minutes**
    
    **Skill Level: Moderate**
    
    **Spice Level Scoville rating: 0-2,000**
    
    To prepare the Mushroom and Artichoke Dip, you will need a cutting board, a knife, a bowl, a whisk, a skillet and a baking dish.

# Ingredients

- 8oz cream cheese, softened 
    - 1/4 cup sour cream 
    - 2 tablespoons mayonnaise 
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced 
    - 4 oz. canned artichoke hearts, drained and chopped 
    - 4oz. canned mushrooms, drained and chopped 
    - 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese 
    - 1/4 teaspoon dried basil 
    - 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
    - Salt and pepper to taste

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Artichoke Dip Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
    2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
    3. Add mushrooms and sauté until soft, about 5 minutes.
    4. Add diced garlic, artichoke hearts, and red pepper flakes and sauté until fragrant, about 3 minutes.
    5. Remove from heat and set aside. 
    6. In a medium bowl, combine cream cheese, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, and mozzarella cheese.
    7. Add mushroom and artichoke mixture and mix until combined.
    8. Grease an 8-inch baking dish and pour mixture into the dish.
    9. Sprinkle additional Parmesan cheese on top of the mixture.
    10. Bake for 25 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
    11. Serve hot with pita chips. Enjoy!
