## Mushroom and Broccoli Quiche
    
    ### Ingredients:
    - 3 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1 onion (chopped)
    - 2 cloves garlic (chopped)
    - 1 lb mushrooms (sliced)
    - 2 cups broccoli (chopped)
    - 1 teaspoon fresh thyme (chopped)
    - 5 eggs
    - 1/2 cup whole milk
    - 1/2 cup heavy cream
    - 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
    - 1 9-inch pre-made pie crust
    
    ### Instructions: 
    1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F. 
    2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. 
    3. Add onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until onion is softened and lightly browned, about 5 minutes. 
    4. Add mushrooms and cook until mushrooms have released their liquid and are lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
    5. Add broccoli, thyme, salt and pepper, and cook for an additional 2 minutes. 
    6. Remove pan from heat and set aside. 
    7. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs, milk and cream. 
    8. Place the pre-made pie crust in a 9-inch pie plate. 
    9. Add the mushroom and broccoli mixture to the pie crust. 
    10. Pour egg mixture over the vegetables. 
    11. Bake for 30 minutes, or until egg is set and top is lightly golden. 
    12. Allow to cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
