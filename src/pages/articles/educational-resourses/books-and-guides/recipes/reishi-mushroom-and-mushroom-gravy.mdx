# Reishi Mushroom and Mushroom Gravy Recipe
    
    ## Ingredients
    - Reishi mushrooms, dried, 1/3 cup
    - Flour, 2 tablespoons
    - Butter, 3 tablespoons
    - Beef stock, 2 cups
    - Salt and pepper, to taste
    - Onion, small, 1
    - Garlic, 1 clove
    
    ## Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
    
    2. In a food processor or coffee grinder, grind reishi mushrooms until they form a powder.
    
    3. In a medium saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
    
    4. Once the butter is melted, add the flour and whisk together until it forms a thick paste.
    
    5. Slowly add the beef stock, whisking constantly.
    
    6. Once all of the stock is added, reduce heat to low and stir in the reishi mushroom powder.
    
    7. Chop the onion and garlic, and add to the gravy.
    
    8. Simmer over low heat, stirring occasionally, for 10-15 minutes.
    
    9. Remove from heat, season with salt and pepper to taste.
    
    10. Serve over mashed potatoes or with your favorite roasted vegetables. Enjoy!
