# Instructions

#Matsutake Mushroom and Tofu Stir-fry
    
    ##Ingredients:
    
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    - 2 tablespoons minced garlic
    - 2 tablespoons minced ginger
    - 2 cups fresh matsutake mushrooms, stems removed, sliced
    - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    - 2 tablespoons sesame oil
    - 1 teaspoon sugar
    - 1 tablespoon chili sauce
    - 1 (16-ounce) package extra firm tofu, cubed
    - 2 tablespoons cornstarch 
    - 2 scallions, thinly sliced
    - Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
    
    ##Instructions:
    
    1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    2. Add garlic and ginger and cook for 30 seconds.
    3. Add mushrooms and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until tender.
    4. Add soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, and chili sauce.
    5. Add tofu, cornstarch, and scallions and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
    6. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
    7. Serve warm. Enjoy!
