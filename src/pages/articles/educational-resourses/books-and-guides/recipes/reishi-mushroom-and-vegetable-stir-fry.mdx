# Reishi Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Reishi Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry is a traditional Chinese dish that has been enjoyed since the Ming dynasty. It is made by combining a variety of vegetables with Reishi mushrooms, a mushroom that is valued highly in Chinese medicine for its health benefits. The vegetables are quickly cooked in a hot wok and the Reishi mushrooms are added to the stir-fry at the last minute, ensuring they retain their flavor and unique texture. It is a perfect balance of flavor, aroma, and nutrition.

# Recipe Overview

    The Prep Time for Reishi Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry is 15 minutes, the Cooking Time is 30 minutes, the Skill Level is intermediate, and the Spice Level Scoville rating is mild. To prepare Reishi Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry, you will need a cutting board, knife, wok, spatula, and colander.

# Ingredients

## Reishi Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry Ingredients
    
    - 1 tablespoon olive oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 teaspoons freshly grated ginger
    - 1/2 cup thinly sliced reishi mushrooms
    - 1/2 cup thinly sliced shiitake mushrooms
    - 1 cup thinly sliced carrots
    - 1/2 cup thinly sliced celery
    - 1/2 cup thinly sliced red bell pepper
    - 2 cups baby spinach
    - 1/4 cup low-sodium vegetable broth
    - 1 tablespoon tamari
    - 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
    - 2 teaspoons white miso paste
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

# Instructions

## Reishi Mushroom and Vegetable Stir-fry Instructions
    
    1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium heat and add 1 tablespoon of sesame oil.
    
    2. Once the oil is hot, add 1/2 cup of diced onion, 1/2 cup of diced red bell pepper and 1 garlic clove (minced). Cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    
    3. Add 1/2 pound of sliced reishi mushrooms and cook for 2 minutes.
    
    4. Add 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of honey, 1/2 teaspoon of ground ginger, 1/2 teaspoon of Chinese five spice powder, 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar, 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flake and 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper. Stir to combine.
    
    5. Add 1 cup of diced carrots, 1/2 cup of snow peas and 1/2 cup of sliced bamboo shoots. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    
    6. Reduce heat to low, cover skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes.
    
    7. Add 1 tablespoon of sesame seeds and stir.
    
    8. Remove skillet from heat and serve. Enjoy!
