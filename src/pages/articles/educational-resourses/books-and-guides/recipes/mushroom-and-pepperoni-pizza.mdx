# Mushroom and Pepperoni Pizza
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    While the origin of pizza is uncertain, the version including mushrooms and pepperoni is widely accepted as an American invention. It was first made popular in the 1950s by Italian-American immigrant chefs who used these common ingredients to top the classic Italian dish. Today, mushroom and pepperoni pizza remains a classic and beloved pizza topping combination.

# Recipe Overview

    **Mushroom and Pepperoni Pizza**
    - Prep Time: 15 minutes 
    - Cooking Time: 20-25 minutes 
    - Skill Level: Easy 
    - Spice Level Scoville rating: Mild
    
    You will need a large bowl, rolling pin, pizza pan, spoon and measuring cups to prepare Mushroom and Pepperoni Pizza.

# Ingredients

## Mushroom and Pepperoni Pizza Ingredients 
    - 1 pound frozen pizza dough
    - 1/2 cup pizza sauce
    - 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    - 2 cups diced pepperoni
    - 3 cups sliced mushrooms 
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
    - 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
    - 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    - 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes 
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Pepperoni Pizza
    
    1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Place frozen pizza dough on a lightly greased baking sheet.
    3. Press the dough out to fit the baking sheet.
    4. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce on the dough.
    5. Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese onto the pizza.
    6. Place the desired amount of sliced mushrooms and pepperoni onto the pizza.
    7. Sprinkle another layer of shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
    8. Bake in preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
    9. Enjoy!
