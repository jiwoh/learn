# Mushroom and Cheese Stuffed Pork Chops
    
    Mushroom and cheese stuffed pork chops is a classic dish featuring juicy, flavorful pork chops stuffed with mushrooms, cheese and spices. This hearty dish has been a favorite of home cooks and restaurant kitchens alike for decades. Originating in Germany, this dish has since been adapted in various ways throughout Europe and the United States.

# Recipe Overview

    Prep Time: Approximately 10 minutes 
    Cooking Time: Around 35 minutes 
    Skill Level: Moderate 
    Spice Level Scoville Rating: Mild 
    To make these delicious pork chops, you will need a knife, cutting board, mixing bowl, baking dish, and a spoon.

# Ingredients

- 4 pork chops
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1/2 cup chopped onion
    - 8 ounces sliced mushrooms
    - 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    - 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    - 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
    - 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
    - Salt to taste

# Instructions

**Mushroom and Cheese Stuffed Pork Chops Instructions:**
    1. Preheat oven to 375° F
    2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add 1 tablespoon of oil and then the mushrooms, onion and garlic. When the vegetables start to soften, about 5 minutes, stir in the parsley, thyme, salt and pepper then set aside to cool for a few minutes.
    3. Place pork chops between 2 pieces of plastic wrap and pound to ½ inch thickness.
    4. Place 1-2 tablespoons of the cooled mushroom filling and 1-2 tablespoons of shredded cheese in the center.
    5. Fold the pork chop over the filling, pressing the edges to seal then secure with toothpicks
    6. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in the skillet over medium-high heat then add the chops. Cook for about 4 minutes per side or until golden brown.
    7. Transfer chops to a greased baking dish. Bake for 20 minutes or until center is no longer pink.
    8. Serve and enjoy!
