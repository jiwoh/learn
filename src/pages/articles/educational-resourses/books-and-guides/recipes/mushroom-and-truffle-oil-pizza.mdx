# Instructions

## Mushroom and Truffle Oil Pizza 
    
    ### Ingredients 
    - Pizza dough 
    - Truffle oil 
    - 2 cloves of garlic, minced 
    - 7 ounces mushrooms (your choice of mushroom, sliced) 
    - 4 ounces parmesan cheese 
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley 
    
    ### Instructions 
    1. Preheat oven to 500Â°F. 
    
    2. Roll or stretch pizza dough into a large circle and transfer to a parchment-lined baking sheet. 
    
    3. Brush the pizza crust evenly with truffle oil. 
    
    4. In a separate bowl, mix together minced garlic, mushrooms, and parmesan cheese. 
    
    5. Spread the mixture evenly over the crust. 
    
    6. Bake the pizza for 15-20 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted. 
    
    7. Sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley and serve.
