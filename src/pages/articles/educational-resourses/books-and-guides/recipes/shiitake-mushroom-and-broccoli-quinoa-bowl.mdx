# Instructions

## Shiitake Mushroom and Broccoli Quinoa Bowl
    
    ### Ingredients
    
    - 2 cups uncooked quinoa
    - 5 cups vegetable broth
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil 
    - 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    - 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
    - 1 pound shiitake mushrooms, stems removed and sliced 
    - 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
    - 2 large heads of broccoli, cut into florets
    - 2 tablespoons tamari
    
    ### Instructions
    
    1. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, combine the quinoa and vegetable broth. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer, covered, for 15 minutes.
    
    2. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, mushrooms, and salt. Stir to combine and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    
    3. Add the broccoli florets to the skillet and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
    
    4. Add the cooked quinoa to the skillet and stir to combine. Stir in the tamari, and cook for an additional 2 minutes. 
    
    5. Serve hot. Enjoy!
