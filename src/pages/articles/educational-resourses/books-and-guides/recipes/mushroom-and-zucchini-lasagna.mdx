# Instructions

# Mushroom and Zucchini Lasagna 
    
    ## Ingredients
    - 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
    - 1 onion, chopped
    - 2 garlic cloves, minced
    - Salt
    - Pepper
    - 2 zucchinis, cut into very thin slices
    - 8 ounces sliced mushrooms
    - 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
    - 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
    - 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    - 2 cups vegetable stock
    - 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    - 8 no-boil lasagna noodles
    - 2 cups ricotta cheese
    - 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    - 1 egg
    - 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    
    ## Instructions 
    1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
    2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the onions, garlic and season with salt and pepper. Cook for 4-5 minutes, until softened. 
    3. Add the zucchinis and mushrooms and cook for another 3-4 minutes. Stir in the thyme and parsley and season with salt and pepper. 
    4. In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, vegetable stock and tomato paste until well combined.
    5. Pour the flour mixture over the vegetables in the skillet and stir to combine. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes.
    6. Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    7. Spread 1/3 of the vegetable mixture over the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Top with 4 lasagna noodles, overlapping them as needed.
    8. In a medium bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese and egg until combined. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
    9. Top with 1/3 of the vegetable sauce and 1 cup of mozzarella cheese.
    10. Place 4 more lasagna noodles over the cheese and spread the remaining ricotta mixture over them.
    11. Top with the remaining vegetable sauce and sprinkle with remaining mozzarella cheese.
    12. Cover baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes.
    13. Uncover and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
    14. Remove from oven and let cool for 15-20 minutes before cutting into slices and serving.
