# Mushroom and ham quiche
    
    ## Origin and History
    
    Mushroom and ham quiche is a classic French dish that has been a staple of kitchens around the world since the mid-20th century. It typically consists of a rich crust filled with a creamy egg mix and mixture of mushroom and ham. Many variations have been created over the years, with different types of cheese, additional vegetables, and even seafood added in. This dish is popular for serving on special occasions, as a brunch item, or just as a savory treat.
