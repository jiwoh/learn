# Mushroom, Potato, and Leek Gratin
    
    ## Ingredients
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - 2 large leeks, washed and thinly sliced
    - 6-8 mushrooms, washed and sliced
    - 2 large potatoes (such as russet), peeled and thinly sliced
    - 2 cups heavy cream
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 1/2 teaspoon fresh ground pepper
    - 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
    - 1 cup shredded Gruyere cheese
    
    ## Instructions 
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Grease an 8-inch (20 cm) baking dish with 1 tablespoon of butter.
    3. In a large skillet, melt remaining butter over medium-high heat. Add leeks and mushrooms and sauté for 5 minutes, until softened.
    4. Layer potato slices in the bottom of the baking dish. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and garlic powder.
    5. Pour cream over the potatoes.
    6. Arrange the mushroom and leek mixture over the top.
    7. Top with shredded Gruyere cheese.
    8. Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, until the potatoes are cooked through.
    9. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
