# Ingredients

## Ingredients
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1 onion, finely chopped
    - 2 cloves garlic, crushed
    - 2 cups arborio rice
    - 6 cups chicken broth, heated
    - 4 king trumpet mushrooms, sliced
    - 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
    - 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
    - Salt and ground black pepper, to taste

# Instructions

## King Trumpet Mushroom and Parmesan Risotto Instructions
    
    1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
    
    2. Add the diced onion and cook until onion is softened and slightly golden, approximately 5 to 6 minutes.
    
    3. Add the garlic, King Trumpet mushrooms, and a pinch of salt. Cook until the mushrooms are lightly browned, approximately 4 to 5 minutes.
    
    4. Add the Arborio rice and cook for 1 minute.
    
    5. Pour in the wine, stirring constantly until the liquid is absorbed by the rice.
    
    6. Add a ladle of hot stock at a time, stirring continually until most of the liquid is absorbed before adding the next ladle. Maintain a gentle simmering heat.
    
    7. Repeat this process with the remaining stock until the rice is cooked but still has a slight bite, approximately 18 minutes.
    
    8. Remove the pan from the heat and add the Parmesan cheese, butter, and pepper. Stir until everything is combined.
    
    9. Serve the risotto in warm bowls with freshly grated Parmesan cheese, extra virgin olive oil, and chopped parsley. Enjoy!
