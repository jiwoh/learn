# Sautéed Mushrooms with Fried Eggs and Toast
    
    ## Origin and History
    Sautéed mushrooms with fried eggs and toast is a classic dish originating from the northern part of Italy. This beloved dish has been around for generations within the Italian culture and is known for its delicious and nutritious combination of flavors. The combination of sautéed mushrooms and fried eggs with toast is often served as a light breakfast or dinner meal and is a great way to start your day with a healthy meal.

# Recipe Overview

. 
    
    The Prep Time for Sautéed Mushrooms with Fried Eggs and Toast is 5 minutes, the Cooking Time is 10 minutes, Skill Level is easy and the Spice Level Scoville rating is 0. To make this dish, you will need a skillet, non-stick cooking spray, butter, garlic, salt, pepper, mushrooms and eggs.

# Ingredients

- 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil 
    - 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    - Salt and pepper, to taste
    - 4 large eggs
    - 4 slices of toast

# Instructions

## Sautéed Mushrooms with Fried Eggs and Toast Instructions
    
    1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add 2 tablespoons of butter.
    
    2. Once the butter has melted, add 1 pound of sliced mushrooms, 1 teaspoon of minced garlic, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. 
    
    3. Cook the mushrooms for 6-7 minutes or until they are golden-brown, stirring occasionally. 
    
    4. Once the mushrooms are done, remove them from the skillet and place them on a plate. 
    
    5. In the same skillet, add 2 more tablespoons of butter and let it melt.
    
    6. Crack 4 eggs into the skillet and season them with salt and pepper.
    
    7. Cook the eggs for 3-4 minutes or until the whites are set and the yolks are cooked to your liking.
    
    8. Toast 4 slices of bread. 
    
    9. Place the mushrooms and eggs on individual plates and serve with the toast.
    
    Enjoy!
