# Mushroom and Egg Fried Rice
    
    Originating from Chinese and Taiwanese cuisines, Mushroom and Egg Fried Rice is a traditional and flavorful dish. This dish is made by stir-frying cooked rice with mushrooms, eggs and other ingredients. Common additions are chopped emerald onions, soy sauce, and various seasonings. This recipe can be easily modified to suit different tastes, making it a popular and versatile dish.

# Recipe Overview

**Prep Time**: 15 minutes 
    
    **Cooking Time**: 20 minutes 
    
    **Skill Level**: Beginner 
    
    **Spice Level Scoville rating**: Low 
    
    To prepare Mushroom and egg fried rice, you will need a wok or large saucepan, oil, eggs, mushrooms, garlic, rice, soy sauce, and optional chilli flakes or ground pepper.

# Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked white or brown rice
    - 1 tablespoon sesame oil
    - 1/2 cup mushrooms, chopped
    - 2 emerald onions, sliced
    - 2 eggs, lightly beaten
    - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro (optional)

# Instructions

## Mushroom and Egg Fried Rice Recipe
    
    1. Bring 2Â¼ cups of water to a boil in a medium-sized saucepan, then add 1 cup of long-grain white rice. 
    
    2. Simmer the rice, covered, for 18 minutes until it is tender, then fluff with a fork and set aside.
    
    3. Heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium heat.
    
    4. Add 1 cup of sliced button mushrooms and cook, stirring frequently, for 3 minutes.
    
    5. Beat 2 eggs in a bowl, then pour them into the heated skillet.
    
    6. Cook the eggs and mushrooms, stirring constantly, for 2 minutes until the eggs are scrambled.
    
    7. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and mix together with a spatula.
    
    8. Add 2 tablespoons of soy sauce and 1 teaspoon of sesame oil.
    
    9. Cook the rice, stirring constantly, for 3 minutes until it is hot and the flavors are combined.
    
    10. Garnish with sliced emerald onions and serve.
