# Instructions

## Black Trumpet Mushroom and Spinach Salad 
    
    ### Ingredients:
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil 
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced 
    - 2 cups fresh black trumpet mushrooms 
    - 1/2 cup toasted pine nuts 
    - 2 bunches baby spinach 
    - 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar 
    - Salt and pepper to taste 
    
    ### Instructions: 
    1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. 
    2. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant. 
    3. Add the mushrooms and cook for 8-10 minutes until golden brown and tender. 
    4. Add the pine nuts and stir to combine. 
    5. Transfer the mixture to a large bowl and set aside to cool. 
    6. Add the spinach and balsamic vinegar to the bowl and toss to combine. 
    7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. 
    8. Serve immediately.
