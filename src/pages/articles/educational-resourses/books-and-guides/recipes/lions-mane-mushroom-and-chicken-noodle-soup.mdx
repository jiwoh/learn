# Ingredients

**Ingredients**
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 cups of Lions Mane mushrooms, diced
    - 1 onion, diced
    - 4 cups chicken broth
    - 3 cups cooked chicken, diced
    - 3 carrots, diced
    - 2 celery stalks, diced
    - 2 cups uncooked egg noodles
    - 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
    - Salt and pepper to taste

# Instructions

## Instructions
    
    1. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large soup pot over medium heat.
    2. Add 1 diced onion and 1 minced clove of garlic to pot and cook until softened and fragrant, about 3 minutes.
    3. Add 2 tablespoons grated ginger, and stir to combine.
    4. Add 8 cups of chicken broth to the pot, and bring to a simmer.
    5. Add 2-3 cups of dried egg noodles to the soup.
    6. Add 1 diced carrot, 1 diced celery stalk, 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (cubed).
    7. Simmer for 15 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and vegetables are tender.
    8. Add 1 cup of fresh or frozen peas, and 2 cups of Lions Mane mushrooms (chopped).
    9. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
    10. Salt and pepper to taste, and serve warm. Enjoy!
