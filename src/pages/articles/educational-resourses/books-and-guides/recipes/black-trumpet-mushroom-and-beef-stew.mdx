# Instructions

##Ingredients:
    - 3 tablespoons olive oil 
    - 2 onions, finely chopped
    - 4 garlic cloves, minced
    - 2 pounds lean ground beef
    - 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    - 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    - 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    - 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
    - 2 cups beef broth
    - 2 cups canned crushed tomatoes
    - 1 pound black trumpet mushrooms, cleaned and chopped 
    - 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped 
    - Salt and freshly ground black pepper
    
    ##Instructions:
    
    1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the onions and garlic and cook, stirring occasionally, until the onions are softened, about 5 minutes.
    
    2. Add the ground beef and cook, breaking it up with a wooden spoon, until it is no longer pink, about 8 minutes.
    
    3. Add the tomato paste, oregano, cumin, and chili powder and cook, stirring, for 1 minute.
    
    4. Add the beef broth and tomatoes and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 15 minutes. 
    
    5. Add the black trumpet mushrooms and simmer for 10 minutes.
    
    6. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. 
    
    7. Stir in the fresh parsley and serve. Enjoy!
