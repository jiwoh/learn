# Instructions

## Mushroom Lasagna Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. 
    2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat 1/4 cup of olive oil. Add 1 pound of sliced mushrooms and cook until browned and most of the liquid has evaporated, about 8 minutes.
    3. Transfer mushrooms to a plate and set aside.
    4. In the same skillet over medium heat, add the remaining olive oil and 1 chopped onion. Cook for 4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    5. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.
    6. Add the tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and oregano. Simmer over low heat while you assemble the lasagna.
    7. In a medium bowl, stir together the ricotta cheese, beaten egg, 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese, 1/4 cup of parsley, and salt and pepper.
    8. In a separate bowl, mix together the mozzarella cheese and remaining Parmesan cheese.
    9. In a 9x13-inch greased baking dish, spoon 1/3 of the tomato sauce.
    10. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
    11. Spread 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles and sprinkle with 1/2 of the mushrooms.
    12. Top with 1/3 of the cheese mixture.
    13. Place 3 more lasagna noodles on top and spread the remaining ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
    14. Top with remaining mushrooms and 1/3 of the cheese mixture.
    15. Place the remaining lasagna noodles on top and spread the remaining tomato sauce over the noodles.
    16. Sprinkle with the remaining cheese mixture and cover the dish with foil.
    17. Bake for 25 minutes covered.
    18. Uncover and bake for an additional 15 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
    19. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
