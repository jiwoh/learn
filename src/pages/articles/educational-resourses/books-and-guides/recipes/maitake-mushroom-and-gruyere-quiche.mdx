# Instructions

## Maitake Mushroom and Gruyere Quiche Instructions
    
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    
    2. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a medium skillet set over medium heat.
    
    3. Add the maitake mushrooms to the skillet and season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the mushrooms are lightly browned and soft, about 5 minutes.
    
    4. Remove from heat and set aside.
